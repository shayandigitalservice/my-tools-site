Pucca Bigha vs Kucha Bigha: Rajasthan Standard

Rajasthan mein aksar log confuse ho jate hain ki Bigha ka sahi size kya hai. Iska jawab niche diya gaya hai:

Pucca Bigha: Yeh sarkari dastawezon mein use hota hai. 1 Pucca Bigha barabar 27,225 Square Feet hota hai. Isme 20 Biswa hote hain.

Kucha Bigha: Yeh Rajasthan ke kai districts mein local kisan aapsi baat-cheet mein use karte hain. 1 Kucha Bigha barabar 17,424 Square Feet hota hai. Yeh Pucca Bigha se chota hota hai.
