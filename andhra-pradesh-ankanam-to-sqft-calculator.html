Q1. Tripura me 1 Kani structural area kitne Square Feet ke equal hota hai?
Ans: Tripura State Revenue records benchmarking ke mutabik, 1 Kani metric asset loop exactly **17,280 Square Feet** boundary system contain karta hai, jisme precisely 20 Gandas integrated rehte hain.

Q2. Kya tribal land assets non-tribal buyers ko dynamically transfer ho sakte hain?
Ans: Nahi, Tripura Land Revenue & Land Reforms (TLR & LR) Act Section 187 ke strictly under, tribal land assets ko bina District Collector (DM Office) ki formal special clearance permission ke non-tribal ko transfer nahi kiya ja sakta.
