About This FD Calculator

This FD Calculator helps you quickly calculate the maturity amount of your fixed deposit investment. Fixed deposits are one of the most popular and safest investment options offered by banks. With this calculator, you can easily estimate the interest earned and the final maturity amount of your deposit.

Instead of manually calculating compound interest, this tool allows you to simply enter the deposit amount, interest rate, and time period. The calculator will instantly show the maturity amount based on the information you provide.

How to Use the FD Calculator

Using this calculator is very simple:

  • Enter the deposit amount you want to invest.
  • Enter the annual interest rate offered by the bank.
  • Enter the time period in years.
  • Click the calculate button.
  • The calculator will instantly display the maturity amount.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Fixed Deposit?
A fixed deposit is a savings scheme where you deposit money in a bank for a fixed time period and earn interest on it.

Is FD a safe investment?
Yes, fixed deposits are considered one of the safest investment options offered by banks.

Can I withdraw FD before maturity?
Yes, but banks may charge a penalty for early withdrawal.
