Assam Advanced Polygon Land Calculator: Complex Tedi-Medi Zameen ka Sateek Hisab

Assam mein zyadatar zameen ki boundaries nadiyon, pahadon ya purane raste ki wajah se bohot zyada tedi-medi hoti hain. Jab zameen ke 4 se zyada konne (corners) hote hain, toh use "Polygon" kaha jata hai. Aisi zameen ko naapne ke liye normal "Length x Breadth" formula kabhi kaam nahi karta. Iske liye sabse best method hai **"Multi-Triangle Method"**.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **Advanced Polygon Land Calculator** isi principle par kaam karta hai. Aap apni badi zameen ko chhote-chhote triangles mein baant lijiye (jaisa ki upar diagram mein dikhaya gaya hai) aur har triangle ki sides ko is tool mein bhariye. Ye tool Heron’s Formula ka use karke har hisse ka area nikalega aur end mein sabko jodkar Assam ke standard **Bigha, Katha, Lessa** mein convert kar dega.

How to Use This Tool? (কিদৰে ব্যৱহাৰ কৰিব?)

  1. Apni zameen ke nakshe par nazar daaliye aur usey triangles (trivhuj) mein baantiye.
  2. Har triangle ki teeno sides (A, B, C) ko feet mein naapiye.
  3. Tool mein pehle triangle ki values bhariye.
  4. Agar 1 se zyada triangles hain, toh "+ Add Triangle" button click karke aur boxes jodiye.
  5. Last mein "Calculate" par click karte hi aapko Hectare se lekar Lessa tak ka saara data mil jayega.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Heron's Formula kya hai?
Ans: Ye ek mathematical formula hai jo kisi bhi triangle ka area nikalta hai agar uski teeno sides pata hon.

Q2. Assam mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Lessa hote hain?
Ans: 1 Bigha = 100 Lessa (kyunki 1 Bigha = 5 Katha aur 1 Katha = 20 Lessa).

Q3. Kya ye tool mobile friendly hai?
Ans: Ji haan, Shayan Smart Tools khas taur par mobile users ke liye design kiya gaya hai.

Q4. Kya main meter mein measurement le sakta hoon?
Ans: Ye tool feet mein input leta hai, lekin result meter mein bhi dikhata hai.

Q5. Is tool ki accuracy kitni hai?
Ans: Ye 100% accurate hai agar aapki physical measurement sahi hai.
