2. Geometric Methodology: The Power of Multi-Triangle Method and Heron's Formula

Irregular polygon plots ka sateek framework map evaluate karne ka sabse ancient aur globally certified system hai bada asset patch ko chhote independent right/irregular triangles me convert kar dena. $$s = \frac{a + b + c}{2}$$

$$\text{Area} = \sqrt{s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c)}$$
