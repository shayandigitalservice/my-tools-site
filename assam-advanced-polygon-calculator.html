Assam Advanced Polygon Land Calculator: Complex Tedi-Medi Zameen ka Sateek Hisab

Assam mein zyadatar zameen ki boundaries nadiyon, pahadon ya purane raste ki wajah se bohot zyada tedi-medi hoti hain. Jab zameen ke 4 se zyada konne (corners) hote hain, toh use "Polygon" kaha jata hai. Aisi zameen ko naapne ke liye normal "Length x Breadth" formula kabhi kaam nahi karta. Iske liye sabse best method hai **"Multi-Triangle Method"**.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **Advanced Polygon Land Calculator** isi principle par kaam karta hai. Aap apni badi zameen ko chhote-chhote triangles mein baant lijiye (jaisa ki upar diagram mein dikhaya gaya hai) aur har triangle ki sides ko is tool mein bhariye. Ye tool Heron’s Formula ka use karke har hisse ka area nikalega aur end mein sabko jodkar Assam ke standard **Bigha, Katha, Lessa** mein convert kar dega.

How to Use This Tool? (কিদৰে ব্যৱহাৰ কৰিব?)

Assam-or mati-r jokh lwa tu kiba hman hohoj nohoi. Jetiya mati tedi-medi hoi, tetiya bhul howar sombhawona thake. Ei tool tu bebohar kora ekdam hohoj:

  1. Divide into Triangles: Apni zameen ke nakshe par nazar daaliye aur usey triangles (trivhuj) mein baantiye. Har triangle ke 3 corners honge.
  2. Measure Sides: Har triangle ki teeno sides (A, B, C) ko feet mein naapiye. Aapko diagonal line (tirchi line) bhi naapni hogi.
  3. Input Data: Tool mein pehle triangle ki values bhariye.
  4. Add More: Agar aapki zameen badi hai aur usme 1 se zyada triangles ban rahe hain, toh "+ Add Triangle" button click karke aur boxes jodiye.
  5. Final Result: Last mein "Calculate" par click karte hi aapko Hectare se lekar Lessa tak ka saara data mil jayega.

Why Use Polygon Method for Assam Land?

Dharitree portal ya sarkari nakshe mein aksar tedi-medi boundaries hoti hain. Jab hum 4-side wala normal calculator use karte hain, toh wo sirf rectangle ka area batata hai. Lekin agar zameen polygon shape mein hai, toh triangles mein divide karna hi sateek raasta hai.

Assam-or manuh-e jetiya mati kine ba beche, tetiya 'Lessa' r ekdam sateek jokh thaka tu dorkari. Ek lessa bhul holeo bohut poisa-r lukuwan hoi. Amari ei Advanced Calculator tu Heron's formula-r uporot base kori banua hoise, jitu geometry-r sobse powerfull formula.

5 Practical Examples: Real-Life Scenarios

  • Example 1: The L-Shape Plot: Agar aapka plot L-shape ka hai, toh usey beech se divide karke 2 triangles banayein. Dono ka data bharne par total area sateek aayega.
  • Example 2: 5-Corner Plot (Pentagon): 5 konne wali zameen ko hamesha 3 triangles mein baantein. Side A, B aur Diagonal line naapkar data enter karein.
  • Example 3: River-Side Land: Nadi ke kinare wali zameen aksar curves mein hoti hai. Wahan chhote-chhote triangles banakar sateek napai karein.
  • Example 4: Tea Garden Area: Badi chai bagan ki zameen ko Hectare se Bigha mein badalne ke liye pehle usey bade segments mein baantein aur fir calculate karein.
  • Example 5: Family Partition: Jab ghar ka batwara hota hai, toh tedi zameen ko barabar baantne ke liye ye multi-triangle tool sabse zyada kaam aata hai.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Heron's Formula kya hai?
Ans: Ye ek mathematical formula hai jo kisi bhi triangle ka area nikalta hai agar uski teeno sides pata hon. Iska upyog tedi zameen ke liye 100% accurate hai.

Q2. Assam mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Lessa hote hain?
Ans: 1 Bigha = 100 Lessa hota hai (5 Katha x 20 Lessa).
