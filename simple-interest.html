Simple Interest Calculator

About This Tool

The Simple Interest Calculator is a free online financial tool that helps users quickly calculate interest on loans, savings, or investments. Instead of manually applying formulas, this calculator provides instant results by simply entering the principal amount, interest rate, and time period. It is useful for students, investors, and anyone who wants to understand how simple interest works in financial calculations.

Simple interest is commonly used in short-term loans, basic banking calculations, and educational examples. This calculator simplifies the entire process and shows both the interest amount and the final total amount you will receive or pay.

How to Use

  • Enter the principal amount (the initial money).
  • Enter the yearly interest rate percentage.
  • Enter the time period in years.
  • Click the “Calculate Interest” button.
  • The calculator will instantly show the interest and total amount.

FAQ

What is simple interest?
Simple interest is calculated only on the original principal amount for the entire time period.

What is the formula of simple interest?
Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100

Who can use this calculator?
Students, business owners, loan borrowers, and anyone who wants quick interest calculations.
