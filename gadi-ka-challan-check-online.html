Gadi Ka Challan Online Kaise Check Kare 2026

Vehicle Number Se eChallan Status Check Kare

Agar aap car ya bike use karte ho to kabhi na kabhi traffic challan ka issue ho sakta hai. Aaj ke time me government ne eChallan system launch kiya hai jisse aap online challan check aur payment kar sakte ho.

Is article me hum aapko batayenge ki **gadi ka challan online kaise check kare**, vehicle number se challan status kaise dekhe aur payment kaise kare.

eChallan Kya Hai?

eChallan ek digital traffic challan system hai jise police aur transport department use karte hain. Jab koi traffic rule break hota hai to challan online system me generate hota hai.

Challan Check Karne Ke Liye Kya Chahiye

  • Vehicle Number
  • Driving License Number
  • Challan Number
  • Internet Connection

Gadi Ka Challan Online Kaise Check Kare

Step 1

Sabse pehle government ki official website open kare:

https://echallan.parivahan.gov.in

Step 2

Check Challan Status option par click kare.

Step 3

Vehicle number enter kare.

Step 4

Captcha fill kare aur search button par click kare.

Step 5

Ab screen par aapko challan details show ho jayegi.

Online Challan Payment Kaise Kare

  • Challan list open kare
  • Pay Now button par click kare
  • Payment method select kare
  • Payment complete kare

Challan Payment Options

  • UPI
  • Debit Card
  • Credit Card
  • Net Banking

Challan Na Bhare To Kya Hoga

  • Penalty increase ho sakti hai
  • Court notice aa sakta hai
  • Vehicle documents issue ho sakta hai
