How to use Punjab Unit Converter? (ਵਰਤੋਂ ਦਾ ਤਰੀਕਾ)

  1. Apne zameen ke documents (Jamabandi) ke hisab se Killa, Kanal aur Marla boxes mein bhariye.
  2. Agar choti unit Sarsai bhi hai, toh use bhi enter karein.
  3. "Convert Units Now" button par click karein.
  4. Tool sateek roop se Hectare, Acre, aur Square Meter ka breakdown dikha dega.
