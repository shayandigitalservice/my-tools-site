Ration Card Kya Hai?

Ration Card ek government issued document hai jo **Public Distribution System (PDS)** ke through subsidized food grains provide karta hai. Is card ke through low income families ko sasta ration milta hai.

Official information portal: https://nfsa.gov.in

Ration Card Ke Types

  • APL (Above Poverty Line)
  • BPL (Below Poverty Line)
  • AAY (Antyodaya Anna Yojana)
  • PHH (Priority Household)

Ration Card Ke Benefits

  • Sasta ration milta hai
  • Government schemes ka benefit
  • Identity proof ke liye use hota hai
  • Address proof ke liye use hota hai
  • Financial subsidy milti hai
