2. The Great Division: Pucca Bigha vs Kucha Bigha

Rajasthan mein land measurement ka sabse bada confusion Bigha ke types ko lekar hota hai. Isse vistar se samajhna bahut zaroori hai:

What is Pucca Bigha?

Pucca Bigha Rajasthan ka "Standard" maapan hai jo mukhya roop se sarkari dastawezon aur registry mein use hota hai. 1 Pucca Bigha = 27,225 Square Feet ($165 \text{ ft} \times 165 \text{ ft}$). Yeh unit mukhya roop se Ganganagar, Hanumangarh aur Bharatpur jaise upjau ilakon mein zyada manya hai.

What is Kucha Bigha?

Rajasthan ke pashchimi (Western) aur dehati ilakon mein kisan aaj bhi Kucha Bigha ka prayog karte hain. 1 Kucha Bigha = 17,424 Square Feet. Yeh Pucca Bigha ka lagbhag teen-pancham ($3/5$) hissa hota hai. Google par log aksar in dono ke beech ke antar ko search karte hain, aur humara calculator ise ek click mein solve kar deta hai.

4. Regional Variations: District-wise Units

Rajasthan itna bada hai ki har 100-200 km par naapne ka nazariya badal jata hai. Jaha Shekhawati region (Sikar, Jhunjhunu) mein Bigha ka use hota hai, wahi Marwar (Jodhpur, Pali) mein log zameen ko Acre aur Bigha dono mein dekhte hain.

  • Jaipur & Alwar: Yahan mukhya roop se Pucca Bigha aur Biswa chalta hai.
  • Jaisalmer & Barmer: Yahan Kucha Bigha aur Hectare ka prachalan zyada hai kyunki zameen ke plot bahut bade hote hain.
  • Hadoti Region: Kota aur Bundi mein bhi Pucca Bigha ko hi standard mana jata hai.

6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Rajasthan mein 1 Bigha mein kitne square feet hote hain?
Ans: Standard Pucca Bigha mein $27,225$ sq. ft. aur Kucha Bigha mein $17,424$ sq. ft. hote hain.
Q2: 1 Bigha mein kitne Biswa hote hain?
Ans: Rajasthan ke dono (Pucca aur Kucha) Bigha mein $20$ Biswa hi hote hain, bas unka total area alag hota hai.
Q3: 1 Acre mein kitne Bigha hote hain Rajasthan mein?
Ans: 1 Acre mein lagbhag $1.6$ Pucca Bigha ya lagbhag $2.5$ Kucha Bigha hota hai.
