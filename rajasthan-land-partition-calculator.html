Rajasthan Land Partition: Accurate Batwara Guide

Rajasthan mein zameen ka batwara (राजस्थान जमीन बंटवारा) hamesha se ek mahatvapurna vishay raha hai. Rajasthan ke vibhinn zilo mein zameen ki napai ke tareeke thode alag ho sakte hain, lekin standard kanooni batwara hamesha Bigha, Biswa aur Biswansi mein hi hota hai. **Shayan Smart Tools** ka ye **Rajasthan Mixed Land Partition Calculator** Rajasthan ke standard standards (1 Pucca Bigha = 27,225 Sq Ft) par adharit hai.

How to Use (उपयोग कैसे करें?)

  1. Apne zameen ke documents ke hisab se Bigha, Biswa, Acre ya Hectare sahi boxes mein darj karein.
  2. Kul hissedaro (shares) ki sankhya enter karein.
  3. "Divide Rajasthan Land" button par click karte hi sateek batwara aapke screen par hoga.
  4. Result ko copy karke ya WhatsApp ke zariye family ke saath share karein.

5 Practical Examples: Rajasthan Mixed Batwara

1. **Family Partition:** 5 Bigha aur 10 Biswa zameen ko 2 bhaiyon mein barabar baantna.
2. **Registry Check:** 1 Hectare zameen ko 4 hissedaro mein sateek divide karna.
3. **Acre Share:** 2 Acre plot ko 3 logo mein Biswa tak barabar baantna.
4. **Mixed Units:** Hectare aur Acre ko jod kar hissedaro mein divide karna.
5. **Small Area:** 15 Biswa aur 10 Biswansi ka barabar batwara nikalna.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Rajasthan mein 1 Bigha kitne feet ka hota hai?
Ans: Rajasthan mein standard 1 Pucca Bigha 27,225 Square Feet ka hota hai.
Q2. Biswansi kya hota hai?
Ans: 1 Biswa ka 20-wa hissa 1 Biswansi kehlata hai (lagbhag 68.06 Sq Ft).
