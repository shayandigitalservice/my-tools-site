3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Manipur Land Registry

Q1. Manipur me property registry charge kitna percent hai?
Ans: Manipur me land registry ke liye standard Stamp Duty rate Male ke liye 5%, Female ke liye 3.75% hai aur Registration Fee flat 1% value base revenue structure ke mutabik apply hoti hai.

Ans: Haan, Manipur state revenue policy me mahilaon ke naam par land register karwane par standard stamp duty me 1.25% ki rebate milti hai, jisse unka total matrix stamp slab 3.75% ho jata hai.

Q3. Manipur me 1 Pari (परারি) me kitne square feet hote hain?
