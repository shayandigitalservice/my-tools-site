Mandatory Documents Required for AP Land Registration (2026)

Andhra Pradesh sub-registrar office mein plot/land sales deed registry execute karne ke liye nishchit likhit dastavejo ki aavashyakta hoti hai. Inke bager IGRS token allocate nahi karta:

📋 Property Documents

  • Original Title Deed: Past sale deeds aur absolute ancestral ownership trace paths record.
  • Link Deeds: Pichle 30 varsho ke verification link documents (E-Encumbrance Certificate verification asset records).
  • AP Land Map Layout: Approved survey boundaries map blueprints (FMB sketch copy).

🆔 Identity & Tax Verification

  • KYC Vectors: Buyer aur Seller dono ke valid identity proof dastavez.
  • PAN Card verification: Property transactional flow value agar ₹5 Lakhs se upar cross karti hai toh PAN compulsory hai.
  • Pattadar Passbook: Agricultural zones validation ke liye mandatory resource profile ledger.
