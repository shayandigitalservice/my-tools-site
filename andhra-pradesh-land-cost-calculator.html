3. Mandatory Documents Checklist for Land Registry in Andhra Pradesh

AP IGRS registry process sub-registrar office me securely finalise karne ke liye khariddaar (buyer) aur bechne wale (seller) ke paas niche diye gaye valid certificates aur reports hone anivarya hain:

  • MeeBhoomi Portal Documents: Digitally signed **Adangal** and **1-B Khationi** copy jisse land ownership and classification validity trace hoti hai.
  • Official Land Map (FMB Map): Revenue department द्वारा verified map copy tracking grid sheets showing layout structures.
  • Collector Guideline Certificate: Region ke standard valuation verification benchmark sheet copy.
  • Identity Proof Verification: Dono party (Buyer, Seller & Witness) ka Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, ya Voter ID documents.
  • Property Diversion Certificate: Agar zameen agricultural se residential/commercial purpose layout zone me badli gayi ho, toh NALA conversion clearance receipt.
  • No-Objection Certificate (NOC): Endowment or government lands rule check specifications limits logs clear documents sheet.

5. 15 Important Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Andhra Pradesh Land Registry & Stamp Duty

Q1. AP mein property registry ke liye stamp duty kitni hai?
Ans: Andhra Pradesh mein land aur property registration par standard Stamp Duty 5% lagti hai.

Q2. Andhra Pradesh mein land registration fee kitne percent hai?
Ans: AP registration rules ke mutabik property ki kul market value par flat 1% registration fee li jaati hai.

Q3. AP mein property transfer par transfer duty ya cess kitna lagta hai?
Ans: AP mein property transactions par standard 1.2% Transfer Duty (Cess) lagta hai, jo urban aur rural dono zones mein barabar hai.

Q4. Kya AP mein mahilaon (female buyers) ke liye stamp duty mein koi chhoot hai?
Ans: Nahi, Andhra Pradesh government ke current rule ke anusar mahila aur purush dono ke liye stamp duty rate ek samaan (5%) hai.

Q5. AP IGRS registry calculator mein kul milakar kitne percent charges lagte hain?
Ans: Kul milakar 7.2% charges lagte hain (5% Stamp Duty + 1.2% Transfer Duty + 1% Registration Fee).

Q6. Andhra Pradesh mein sarkari land record online kaise check karein?
Ans: Aap Andhra Pradesh ke official web portal MeeBhoomi (meebhoomi.ap.gov.in) par jaakar online land details check kar sakte hain.

Q7. Property ki registry kis value par hoti hai - Market Rate ya Circle Rate?
Ans: Registry hamesha government dwara tay kiye gaye Circle Rate (Sarkari Rate) ya usse adhik ki value par hi ho sakti hai, kam par nahi.

Q8. MeeBhoomi portal par Adangal aur 1-B document kya hote hain?
Ans: Adangal mein zameen ki type aur details hoti hain, jabki 1-B document zameen ke malikana haq (ownership history) ko darshata hai.

Q9. Andhra Pradesh mein 1 Acre mein kitne Square Feet hote hain?
Ans: AP land revenue standard ke mutabik 1 Acre mein exact 43,560 Square Feet hote hain.

Q10. Zameen kharidte samay NALA conversion certificate kyu zaroori hai?
Ans: Agar aap kheti ki zameen (agricultural land) ko residential ya commercial use ke liye kharid rahe hain, toh NALA conversion receipt anivarya hai.

Q11. Kya is calculator ke budget mein user ki scanning fee ya court fee shamil hai?
Ans: Nahi, yeh tool sirf core elements (Land Cost, Stamp Duty, Registration Fee aur Cess) calculate karta hai. Local operating fees alag se hoti hain.

Q12. AP mein registry hone ke kitne din baad zameen ka mutation hota hai?
Ans: Registry data automatic sync hone ke baad lagbhag 15 se 30 dino ke andar MeeBhoomi portal par dakhil-kharij (mutation) process poori ho jaati hai.

Q13. Registry ke samay kaun-kaun se identity proof valid maane jaate hain?
Ans: Dono pakshon (buyer aur seller) ke paas PAN Card, Voter ID, ya valid Passport hona chahiye.

Q14. Kya urban aur rural areas ke liye stamp duty tax alag hota hai?
Ans: Nahi, Andhra Pradesh mein urban aur rural dono kshetro ke liye base stamp duty tax samaan roop se 5% hi lagta hai.

Q15. AP IGRS dashboard par market rate check karne ka kya tarika hai?
Ans: Aap registration.ap.gov.in portal par 'Market Value Assistance' tab par jaakar district, mandal aur village select karke rate jaan sakte hain.
