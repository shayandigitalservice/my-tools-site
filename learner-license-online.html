What is Learner License?

Learner License ek temporary driving permit hai jo aapko driving seekhne aur training start karne ki permission deta hai. Ye license valid hai initial 6 months ke liye aur aap iske saath Permanent Driving License ke liye apply kar sakte ho.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 16 years for motorcycle without gear, 18 years for car / other vehicles.
  • Applicant must be physically fit.
  • Applicant must have valid government ID proof (Aadhaar / Passport / Voter ID).
  • Resident of the state where applying.

Required Documents

  • Proof of Identity – Aadhaar / PAN / Passport / Voter ID
  • Proof of Address – Aadhaar / Passport / Ration Card / Utility Bill
  • Passport size photograph (if required)
  • Age proof – Birth Certificate / School Certificate / Aadhaar

Step by Step Guide to Apply Learner License Online

Step 1 – Visit Official Parivahan / State RTO Portal

👉 Official Parivahan Sewa Portal

Select your state and go to “Driving License / Learner License” section.

Step 2 – Fill Online Application Form

Click “Apply for Learner License”, fill details like Name, DOB, Address, Contact, Vehicle type.

Step 3 – Upload Documents

Scan and upload ID, address, and age proof documents. Ensure clarity and file format compliance.

Step 4 – Schedule Learner License Test

Select preferred date and RTO center for online / offline driving knowledge test.

Step 5 – Pay Fees Online

Pay applicable fees using net banking / UPI / credit card / debit card.

Step 6 – Submit Application

Review all details and submit application. Save Application Reference Number (ARN) for tracking.

Step 7 – Appear for Learner License Test

Attend the driving knowledge test at selected RTO. Passing is mandatory for approval.

Step 8 – Check Application Status

Login to portal using ARN / Aadhaar to check status. On approval, digital Learner License is issued.

Common Issues and Fixes

  • OTP not received – Ensure mobile number registered and active.
  • Document rejected – Upload clear and correct document.
  • Test date unavailable – Choose alternate RTO center / date.
  • Application status pending – Allow 2–7 working days for verification.

FAQs

1. Can I apply for Learner License for car and bike together?

Yes, specify both vehicle types in the application.

2. How long is Learner License valid?

6 months from the date of issue.

3. Can I drive with Learner License?

Yes, but only with a licensed driver accompanying you (rules vary by state).

4. Is there any fee for online application?

Yes, nominal fee applies, pay online via portal.

5. What if my application is rejected?

Check reasons in portal, correct details, and re-submit application.

Tips for Successful Learner License Application

  • Use official Parivahan portal only.
  • Keep Aadhaar / ID / address proof ready and clear scanned copies.
  • Ensure mobile number is active for OTP.
  • Check details before submission to avoid rejection.
  • Track application using ARN for updates.
