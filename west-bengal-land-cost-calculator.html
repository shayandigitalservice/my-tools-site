Introduction (Dumdar Prastavana)

West Bengal mein real estate market hamesha se hi active raha hai. Kolkata, Siliguri ya Durgapur—kahin bhi property kharidne se pehle sabse badi tension hoti hai total cost ka calculation. West Bengal mein zameen ka rate zyadatar "Per Bigha" ya "Per Katha" ke taur par bataya jata hai. Urban areas mein toh "Per Chatak" ka rate bhi chalta hai.

How to Use WB Land Cost Calculator?

Is calculator ka istemal karna behad saral hai:

  1. Dropdown menu se unit select karein (Bigha, Katha, ya Chatak).
  2. "Enter Area" mein zameen ki quantity bharein (Example: 5 Katha).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. West Bengal mein 1 Bigha mein kitna Katha hota hai?
Ans: West Bengal standard ke anusar 1 Bigha mein 20 Katha hote hain.

Q2. 1 Katha mein kitna Chatak hota hai?
Ans: West Bengal mein hamesha 1 Katha mein 16 Chatak hote hain.

Q3. Kya Registry fees total price mein shamil hai?
Ans: Nahi, Registry fees aur Stamp Duty (lagbhag 5-7%) aapko alag se pay karni hoti hai.

Q4. 1 Katha kitne Square Feet ka hota hai?
Ans: WB mein 1 Katha exactly 720 Square Feet ka hota hai.

Q5. Kya ye tool rural Bengal ke liye accurate hai?
Ans: Ji haan, yeh pure West Bengal ke standard units par based hai.
