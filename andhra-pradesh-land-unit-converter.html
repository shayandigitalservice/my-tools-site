How to use AP Unit Converter? (ఉపయోగించే విధానం)

  1. Apne land documents (Adangal/Pahani) ke hisab se Ankanam, Gajam aur Cent boxes mein bhariye.
  2. Agar aapke paas sirf ek hi unit hai, toh baaki boxes ko '0' ya khali chod dein.
  3. "Convert Units Now" button par click karein.
  4. Tool turant Square Feet, Acre aur Hectare ka sateek parinaam dikha dega.
