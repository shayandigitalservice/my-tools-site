1. Introduction to Punjab Land Revenue Records (Fard aur Jamabandi Kya Hai?)

Punjab mein land administration aur revenue management ka itihaas sadiyon purana hai, jise Mughal kaal aur Maharaja Ranjit Singh ke samay ke baad British rule ke dauran ek vyavasthit niyamawali di gayi. Aaj ke is modern and digitalized era me jab hum zameen ki kharid-farokht (Property transaction), bank loan processing, partition of ancestral property (Khewangi), ya legal verification karte hain, toh sabse pehla aur sabse important document jo samne aata hai, use hum Jamabandi ya aam bhasha mein Fard kehte hain.

Digital Punjab Land Records Authority (PLRS - Punjab Land Records Society) portal par zameen ka ye pura record online darj kiya ja chuka hai. Lekin is portal par ya physical patwari dastawez par jab koi aam nagrik apni zameen ka area dekhta hai, toh wo hamesha complex sub-units me divide hota hai—jaise ki 2 Killa, 5 Kanal, 12 Marla. Ek aam vyakti jo market me gaj (Square Yards) ya shehron me Square Feet (Sq Ft) ke hisab se deals karta hai, uske liye is traditional metric system ka calculation nikalna kafi tedhi kheer ban jata hai. Aapki isi samasya ko door karne aur sateek mathematical data pradan karne ke liye Shayan Smart Tools ne is high-precision Punjab Jamabandi Land Calculator ko develop kiya hai.

2. Understanding the Hierarchy: Killa, Kanal, Marla, and Sarsahi

Punjab ka revenue department zameen ko maapne ke liye ek specific hierarchy ka upyog karta hai. Agar aap is hierarchy ke logic ko samajh lete hain, toh aapke liye kisi bhi Patwari document ya Fard Badar ko padhna behad aasan ho jayega. Punjab system mein zameen ki sabse choti standard unit hoti hai Sarsahi. 9 Sarsahi ke area ko milakar banta hai 1 Marla. Iske baad, 20 Marla ke pure group ko bacha kar rakha jata hai jise hum 1 Kanal kehte hain. Aur ant mein, jab pure 8 Kanal ka area ek jagah mil jata hai, toh use 1 Killa ya 1 Acre ka naam diya jata hai.

Shehron me real estate market me Gaj (Square Yards) ka istemal hota hai. Punjab revenue rules ke mutabik, 1 Marla me sateek 30.25 Gaj hote hain. Agar hum ise 9 se multiply karein (kyunki 1 Gaj me 9 Square Feet hote hain), toh 30.25 × 9 = 272.25 Square Feet banta hai, jo ki Punjab me 1 Marla ki universal legal value hai. 4. Crucial Identifiers in a Punjab Fard: Khewat, Khatauni, and Khasra

Jab aap hamare calculator ka upyog karke zameen ka sateek mathematical area nikaal lete hain, toh agla mahatvapurna kadam hota hai dastawez me likhe legal numbers ko cross-verify karna. Punjab Jamabandi portal par teen mukhya numbers darj hote hain jo har land parcel ki identity hote hain:

A. Khewat Number (ਖੇਵਟ ਨੰਬਰ): Ise 'Khata Number' bhi kaha jata hai. Yeh un maalikoon (owners) ke group ya individual maalik ko darshata hai jo zameen ke us pure bade tukde ke sanmukh zimmedar hote hain. Agar zameen ke co-sharers badalte hain ya zameen ka batwara hota hai, toh Khewat number badal jata hai.

B. Khatauni Number (ਖਤੌਨੀ ਨੰਬਰ): Yeh number darshata hai ki us zameen par kheti kaun kar raha hai yaani cultivator kaun hai. Agar maalik khud kheti kar raha hai, toh wahan 'Khud Kasht' likha hota hai. Agar zameen kisi kirayedaar ya kisan ko patte (lease) par di gayi hai, toh Khatauni number se uski pehchan hoti hai.

C. Khasra Number (ਖਸਰਾ ਨੰਬਰ): Yeh sabse mahatvapurna parcel number hota hai. Yeh state revenue map (Sajra) par nirdharit kiya gaya ek specific plot number hota hai. Khasra number se hi zameen ki geographical location, uske aaspas ki boundaries aur sateek area dimension ki nishandehi ki jaati hai.
