Introduction (Zaroori Jankari)

Jab aap Punjab mein apni zameen ki Jamabandi (Fard) online check karte hain, toh kuch sarkari portals par back-end data standardisation ke liye area Hectare mein likha hota hai. Lekin Punjab ke kisan aur aam log apni zameen ka hisaab-kitab hamesha Kanal, Marla, Killa (Acre) aur Bigha mein karte hain.

Punjab Revenue Department (PLRMS) ke mutabik, Punjab mein zameen ki sabse mukhy ikai (main unit) Kanal aur Marla hai. Hamara yeh calculator **Punjab Govt Patwari system** ke bilkul sateek mathematical formula par kaam karta hai, taaki kisanon ko apni fard samajhne mein koi pareshani na ho.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Punjab Jamabandi mein zameen kis unit mein hoti hai?
Ans: Punjab online Jamabandi portal par zameen mukhy roop se Kanal-Marla aur Killa (Acre) mein dikhai deti hai, jise back-end par Hectare se map kiya jata hai.

Q2. Punjab mein 1 Hectare mein kitne Kanal hote hain?
Ans: Punjab Government standard ke anusaar 1 Hectare mein 19 Kanal aur 15.4 Marla hote hain.

Q3. 1 Killa (Acre) mein kitne Kanal hote hain?
Ans: Punjab mein 1 Killa pure 8 Kanal (yaani 160 Marla) ke barabar hota hai.

Q4. Kya Punjab ke har ilaqe mein Bigha ka size same hai?
Ans: Nahi, Punjab ke kuch ilakon mein Bigha chota-bada ho sakta hai, lekin sarkari taur par computerised records mein Kanal-Marla ko hi standard mana jata hai.

Q5. Kya yeh tool bilkul accurate hai?
