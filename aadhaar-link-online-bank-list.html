Complete Bank List + Real Guide

Important:
Har bank me Aadhaar online link facility available nahi hoti. Kuch banks me hi ye option milta hai.

Aadhaar Online Link Kya Hai

Aadhaar online link ka matlab hai apne bank account ko Aadhaar se internet banking ya mobile app ke through connect karna bina branch visit kiye.

Kaunse Bank Me Aadhaar Online Link Hota Hai

1. SBI (State Bank of India)

SBI me net banking aur YONO app ke through Aadhaar link kar sakte ho.

2. HDFC Bank

HDFC net banking aur mobile app me Aadhaar linking option available hai.

3. ICICI Bank

ICICI bank me internet banking se Aadhaar update kar sakte ho.

4. Axis Bank

Axis bank ke app aur net banking me Aadhaar linking option milta hai.

5. Bank of Baroda

BOB ke online portal se Aadhaar link possible hai.

6. Punjab National Bank (PNB)

PNB net banking se Aadhaar link kar sakte ho.

7. Kotak Mahindra Bank

Kotak app aur website se linking facility available hai.

Kaunse Bank Me Online Nahi Hota

1. Regional Rural Banks

RRB banks me usually branch visit karna padta hai.

2. Small Cooperative Banks

In banks me online facility limited hoti hai.

Step 2 – Login Kare

Net banking login kare.

Step 3 – Aadhaar Linking Option

Profile ya services me option select kare.

Important Tips

  • Mobile number Aadhaar se linked hona chahiye
  • Sirf official bank website use kare
  • OTP share na kare
  • Details sahi enter kare

Conclusion

Sab banks me Aadhaar online link nahi hota, lekin major banks jaise SBI, HDFC, ICICI me ye facility available hai. Agar aapka bank online option deta hai to aap ghar baithe linking kar sakte ho.
