UAN Balance Check Online 2026

Step by Step Complete Hinglish Guide

UAN Balance Check Kya Hai

UAN (Universal Account Number) ke through aap apna EPF balance online check kar sakte ho. Ye service EPFO ke through provide ki jati hai jisse employees apni savings aur contribution track kar sakte hain.

UAN Balance Check Kyu Important Hai

1. PF Balance Track Karne Ke Liye

Aap apni total savings aur employer contribution dekh sakte ho.

2. Withdrawal Planning

Balance check karke aap decide kar sakte ho kab withdrawal karna hai.

3. Transparency

Har transaction aur update easily check hota hai.

Required Details

  • UAN Number
  • Registered Mobile Number
  • Internet Connection
  • EPFO Account Active Hona Chahiye

Method 1 – EPFO Portal Se Balance Check

Step 1 – Official EPFO Website Open Kare

Browser me EPFO member portal open kare.

Step 2 – Login Kare

UAN aur password enter kare aur login kare.

Step 3 – Passbook Option Select Kare

“View Passbook” option par click kare.

Step 4 – Balance Check Kare

Aapka total PF balance screen par show hoga.

Method 2 – Missed Call Se Balance Check

Step 1 – Registered Mobile Se Call Kare

Registered mobile number se EPFO missed call number par call kare.

Step 2 – Call Cut Hone Ke Baad SMS

Kuch seconds me SMS ke through balance details mil jayegi.

Method 3 – SMS Se Balance Check

Step 1 – SMS Send Kare

EPFO ke format me SMS send kare (EPFOHO UAN ENG).

Step 2 – Balance SMS Receive Kare

Aapko PF balance ka SMS receive hoga.

Method 4 – UMANG App Se Balance Check

Step 1 – UMANG App Install Kare

App install karke open kare.

Step 2 – EPFO Service Select Kare

EPFO section me jaakar balance check kare.

Step 3 – Login Kare

UAN enter karke login kare aur balance dekhe.

Important Tips

  • UAN activate hona chahiye
  • Mobile number linked hona chahiye
  • Official portal hi use kare
  • Password secure rakhe

Common Problems Aur Solutions

Login Nahi Ho Raha

Password reset kare.

OTP Issue

Mobile network check kare.

Balance Show Nahi Ho Raha

Employer update delay ho sakta hai.

Benefits of UAN Balance Check

Anywhere balance check kar sakte ho.

Free Service

Koi charge nahi lagta.

Conclusion

UAN balance check karna ab bahut easy ho gaya hai. Upar diye gaye methods follow karke aap apna PF balance kabhi bhi check kar sakte ho aur apni financial planning better bana sakte ho.
