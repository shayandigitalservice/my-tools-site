DBT Linked Bank Kya Hota Hai

DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) linked bank wo bank hota hai jisme aapka Aadhaar number mapped hota hai aur jisme government schemes ka paisa direct transfer hota hai.

Kaunsa Bank DBT Ke Liye Active Hota Hai

Ek time par sirf ek bank DBT ke liye active hota hai. Jo bank last me Aadhaar se link hota hai wahi DBT bank ban jata hai.

DBT Linked Bank Kaise Check Kare

Step 1 – Upar Diye Gaye Button Par Click Kare

Official UIDAI website open ho jayegi.

Step 2 – Aadhaar Number Enter Kare

Apna 12 digit Aadhaar number enter kare.

Step 3 – OTP Verification Kare

Registered mobile par OTP aayega, use enter kare.

Step 4 – Bank Name Dekhe

Screen par jo bank name show hoga wahi aapka DBT linked bank hai.

Important Information

Sirf Ek Bank Active Hota Hai

Chahe aapke paas multiple accounts ho, DBT sirf ek bank me hi aata hai.

Last Linked Bank Active Hota Hai

Jo bank last me Aadhaar se link hota hai wahi DBT ke liye active ho jata hai.

Agar Galat Bank Show Ho Raha Hai

1. Bank Me Aadhaar Update Kare

Jis bank me paisa chahiye waha Aadhaar link karwaye.

2. Dubara Linking Kare

Latest linking se DBT bank change ho jata hai.

3. Bank Branch Visit Kare

Nearest branch me jaakar update karwaye.

Important Tips

  • Mobile number Aadhaar se linked hona chahiye
  • OTP kisi ke sath share na kare
  • Sirf official website use kare
  • Correct Aadhaar number enter kare

Conclusion

Ab aap easily check kar sakte ho ki aapka DBT ka paisa kis bank me aa raha hai. Upar diya gaya button direct official website open karta hai jahan se aap turant apna DBT linked bank check kar sakte ho.
