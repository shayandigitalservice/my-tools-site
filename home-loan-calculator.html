FAQs

1. What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment you make to repay a home loan.

2. Can I use this calculator for other loans?

Yes, you can use it for car loans, personal loans, or any other fixed EMI loans by adjusting the loan amount and interest.

3. How does down payment affect EMI?

Higher down payment reduces the principal, which decreases EMI and total interest payable.
