Tedi-Medi Zameen Kaise Naapein? (How to Measure Irregular Land)

Irregular land ko naapne ka sabse sateek tarika hai use triangles (tribhuj) mein baant lena. Jaisa ki aap upar diye gaye diagram mein dekh sakte hain, jab aap kisi tedi zameen ko triangles mein baant dete hain aur har triangle ki teeno sides ka map lete hain, toh error ki gunjayish khatam ho jati hai.

Agar aapka plot UP mein hai aur aap use standard units mein badalna chahte hain, toh aap hamara UP Land Unit Converter bhi use kar sakte hain. Lekin polygon shapes ke liye ye advanced tool hi sabse behtar hai.

How to Use This Advanced Tool: Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Triangulation: Apne plot ko triangles mein divide karein. Har triangle ke teen kone hone chahiye.
  2. Measurement: Har triangle ki teeno sides (A, B, C) ko feet mein naapiye.
  3. Data Entry: Is calculator mein "Triangle #1" ki values bhariye.
  4. Add More: Agar aapki zameen badi hai aur usme 1 se zyada triangles ban rahe hain, toh "+ Add Triangle" button ka upyog karein.
  5. Calculate: Sabhi values bharne ke baad "Calculate" par click karein. Tool aapko Bigha, Biswa, Biswansi, Acre aur Hectare mein sateek hisab dikha dega.
