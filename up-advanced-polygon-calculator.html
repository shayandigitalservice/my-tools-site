UP Advanced Polygon Land Calculator: Complex Tedi-Medi Zameen ka Sateek Hisab

Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein khet ya plot aksar ekdam square ya rectangle shape mein nahi hote. Chakbandi ke baad bhi kai baar boundaries tedi-medi reh jati hain. Aise mein kisan bhai aksar "Average Method" ka use karte hain, jo ki galat hai. Isse ya toh aapka nuksan hota hai ya fir padosi ka.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **UP Advanced Polygon Area Calculator** "Heron’s Formula" ka upyog karta hai. Is formula se tedi zameen ko kai triangles (tribhuj) mein baant kar ekdam sateek area nikala jata hai. Chahe aapka plot Lucknow mein ho ya Varanasi mein, ye tool UP Bhulekh ke standard Bigha, Biswa, aur Biswansi ke hisab se perfect result deta hai.

उपयोग कैसे करें? (Step-by-Step Guide)

  1. Apni tedi zameen ko triangles mein divide karein (Diagram dekhein).
  2. Har triangle ki teeno sides ko feet mein naapiye.
  3. Tool mein pehle triangle ki values daaliye.
  4. "+ Add Triangle" button click karke baki triangles ka data jodiye.
  5. Calculate par click karte hi aapko sateek UP standard result mil jayega.
