What is Digital Locker?

Digital Locker (DigiLocker) ek official government platform hai jahan aap apne personal, educational aur professional documents online store aur access kar sakte ho. Ye legal proof ke roop me bhi valid hai.

Benefits of Digital Locker

  • Secure online storage of documents
  • Access anytime from anywhere
  • Share documents instantly with government / educational institutions
  • Reduces dependency on physical copies
  • Integrated with Aadhaar for verification

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Aadhaar Number (mandatory for registration)
  • Registered Mobile Number linked to Aadhaar
  • Internet connection and smartphone / PC
  • Documents in PDF / JPG / PNG format

Step by Step Guide to Register in Digital Locker

Step 1 – Visit Official DigiLocker Portal

👉 Official DigiLocker Portal

Step 2 – Sign Up Using Aadhaar

Click on “Sign Up” and enter your Aadhaar number. Verify using OTP sent to your registered mobile number.

Step 3 – Set Username & Password

Create a secure username and password for future login. Remember these credentials for accessing your locker.

Step 4 – Upload Documents

Login to your account and click on “Upload Document”. Select the file (PDF/JPG/PNG) and enter document details like name, type, and issuer.

Step 5 – Issued Documents Verification

Documents issued by registered authorities (e.g., school mark sheet, PAN, Aadhaar) can be linked digitally using issuer API verification.

Step 6 – Access Documents Anytime

Login with your credentials to view, download, or share documents securely. You can also generate shareable link with QR code.

Common Issues and Fixes

  • OTP not received – Ensure mobile number is linked with Aadhaar.
  • Document not uploaded – Check file format and size limits.
  • Login issues – Clear cache, check internet, or reset password.
  • Verification failure – Ensure correct issuer details or use official document copies.

FAQs

1. Is DigiLocker legally valid?

Yes, documents uploaded or issued in DigiLocker are legally valid under the Indian IT Act 2000.

2. Can I upload all types of documents?

You can upload personal, educational, and government-issued documents. Certain documents require issuer verification.

3. Can I share documents with institutions?

Yes, you can share digitally verified documents with schools, colleges, banks, and government authorities.

4. Is there any fee for using Digital Locker?

No, DigiLocker is completely free for all citizens.

5. What if I forget my login credentials?

Use the “Forgot Password / Username” option to reset your account securely using Aadhaar verification.

Tips for Efficient Use

  • Keep Aadhaar and mobile number updated.
  • Upload clear scanned copies of documents.
  • Use strong passwords for account security.
  • Regularly backup important documents.
  • Verify issued documents for authenticity.
