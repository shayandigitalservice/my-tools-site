What is SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds instead of investing a lump sum. SIP allows investors to benefit from rupee cost averaging and compounding, making it a smart long-term investment strategy.

With SIP, you can start investing with a small amount and gradually build a large corpus over time. It is widely used for achieving financial goals like retirement planning, education funds, buying a house, and wealth creation.

How SIP Calculator Works

The SIP calculator uses a standard mathematical formula to calculate the future value of your monthly investments based on the expected rate of return and investment duration.

SIP Formula

Future Value = P × [ ( (1 + r)^n - 1 ) / r ] × (1 + r)

  • P = Monthly investment amount
  • r = Monthly interest rate
  • n = Number of months

This formula helps calculate how your investments grow over time due to the power of compounding.

Benefits of Investing Through SIP

1. Power of Compounding

SIP allows your investments to grow exponentially over time through compounding. The longer you stay invested, the higher your returns.

2. Rupee Cost Averaging

You invest regularly regardless of market conditions, reducing the impact of market volatility.

3. Disciplined Investment

SIP encourages regular investing habits, helping you stay consistent with your financial goals.

4. Affordable Investment

You can start SIP with a small amount, making it accessible for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is SIP better than lump sum investment?

SIP is better for beginners as it reduces market risk and promotes disciplined investing.

Can I change SIP amount?

Yes, you can increase or decrease your SIP amount anytime.

Is SIP risk-free?

No, SIP is linked to market performance, but it reduces long-term risk.

What is ideal SIP duration?

Minimum 5–10 years is recommended for good returns.
