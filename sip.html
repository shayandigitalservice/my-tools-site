About This SIP Calculator

The SIP Calculator is a simple and powerful online tool that helps you estimate the future value of your investments made through a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP). SIP is a popular investment method used in mutual funds where investors invest a fixed amount every month instead of investing a large amount at once.

With this calculator, you can quickly find out how much wealth you can build over time based on your monthly investment, expected annual return, and investment duration. The tool automatically calculates the total invested amount, estimated returns, and total future value of your investment.

This calculator is useful for beginners as well as experienced investors who want to plan their financial goals such as retirement planning, wealth creation, education funds, or long-term savings. The tool works directly in your browser and provides instant results without requiring any registration.

How to Use

  • Enter the amount you want to invest every month.
  • Enter the expected annual return rate.
  • Enter the number of years you plan to invest.
  • Click the Calculate button.
  • The calculator will instantly show the invested amount, returns, and total value.

FAQ

What is SIP?
SIP stands for Systematic Investment Plan where you invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds.

Is this SIP calculator free?
Yes, the calculator is completely free to use.

Is the return guaranteed?
No, the result shown is only an estimate based on the expected return rate.

Can I use this tool on mobile?
Yes, the calculator works perfectly on smartphones and tablets.
