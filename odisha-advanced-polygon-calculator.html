2. Geometric Methodology: Triangulation Engine and Heron's Formula

Complex geometric plots ka map accurate calculate karne ka absolute globally approved method hai complex asset block ko discrete right or scalable triangles me turn kar dena. Jab aap plot map boundaries par diagonals lines design karte hain, toh single complex plot data multiple mathematical sub-units me break ho jata hai. Teeno side parameters data variables (Side A, Side B, Side C) feed hote hi backend framework **Heron’s Formula** apply kar deta hai:

$$s = \frac{a + b + c}{2}$$

$$\text{Area} = \sqrt{s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c)}$$
