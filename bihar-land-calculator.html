How to Use the Bihar Land Measurement Tool?

Bihar mein zameen ki lambai aur chaudai se area nikalna ab bahut asan hai. Bas niche diye gaye char steps follow karein:

  • 1. Enter Length: Zameen ki total lambai (Length) fill karein.
  • 2. Enter Width: Zameen ki total chaudai (Width) darj karein.
  • 3. Choose Unit: Aap measurement Feet (ft) ya Meter (m) mein se chunaav kar sakte hain.
  • 4. Get Result: 'CONVERT NOW' par click karte hi aapko Bigha, Kattha, aur Dhur ka accurate breakdown mil jayega.

Example: 1 Kattha Zameen Kaise Nikalein?

Maan lijiye aapke paas ek rectangular plot hai jiski lambai 40 feet hai aur chaudai 34 feet hai.

Calculation: 40 ft × 34 ft = 1,360 sq. ft.
Bihar ke standard ke mutabiq, 1,361.25 sq. ft. barabar **1 Kattha** hota hai. Iska matlab hai ki aapka plot lagbhag 1 Kattha ka hai.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Bihar mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Kattha hote hain?
Ans: Bihar mein 1 Bigha mein total 20 Kattha hote hain.
Q2: 1 Kattha mein kitna Dhur hota hai?
Ans: 1 Kattha mein total 20 Dhur hote hain. Yeh Bihar ki sabse common unit hai.
Q3: Bihar aur Assam ke Bigha mein kya antar hai?
Ans: Bihar ka Bigha (27,225 sq. ft.) Assam ke Bigha (14,400 sq. ft.) se lagbhag dugna bada hota hai.
Q4: 1 Acre mein kitna Bigha hota hai Bihar mein?
Ans: Bihar mein 1 Acre lagbhag 1.6 Bigha ke barabar hota hai.
Q5: Kya yeh calculator Bihar ke sabhi districts ke liye sahi hai?
Ans: Haan, yeh Bihar ke standard state measurement par adharit hai. Halanki kuch districts mein local 'Laggi' ke hisaab se thoda antar ho sakta hai.
