Driving License Renewal Online Kaise Kare 2026

Step by Step Guide with Official Government Links

Driving License (DL) renewal India me state transport departments ke through hoti hai. Agar aapka DL expire hone wala hai ya expired hai, to aap online easily DL renewal process complete kar सकते हैं।

Driving License Renewal Kya Hota Hai

DL renewal ka matlab hai ki aap apne existing driving license ko update karte हैं aur valid period extend karte हैं। Ye process official government portal ke through hoti hai।

Required Documents for DL Renewal

  • Existing Driving License
  • Address proof (Aadhaar / Passport / Voter ID)
  • Age proof (Aadhaar / Birth certificate)
  • Medical certificate (if required)
  • Registered mobile number for OTP verification

Driving License Renewal Online Kaise Kare

Step 1 – Official Portal Open Kare

Parivahan Sewa Official Website open kare. Ye Ministry of Road Transport & Highways का official portal hai।

Step 2 – Driving License Services

“Driving License Related Services” section me jaaye aur apne state select kare।

Step 3 – Renewal Option Select Kare

“Renewal of Driving License” option select kare aur application form open kare।

Step 4 – Form Fill Kare

Form me apni personal details, existing DL number aur address proof details enter kare।

Step 5 – Upload Documents

Required documents scan karke upload kare।

Step 6 – Payment Kare

Nominal fee online pay kare (₹200-₹500, state dependent)।

Step 7 – Appointment / Slot Book Kare

Agar driving test ya verification required hai to appointment book kare। Otherwise, renewal certificate generate ho jayega aur aapke registered address par dispatch ho jayega।

DL Renewal Ke Benefits

  • Legal driving continue karna possible
  • Updated license with latest validity
  • Official record updated with RTO

Important Tips

  • Official Parivahan portal hi use kare
  • Mobile number aur email active ho
  • Document scan clear ho
  • Fake websites se bachke rahe
