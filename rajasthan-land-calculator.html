Introduction (Dumdar Prastavana)

Rajasthan mein zameen ki napai (land measurement) ke liye sadiyon se Bigha ka upyog hota aa raha hai. Rajasthan ka land system thoda alag hai kyunki yahan Pucca Bigha aur Kucha Bigha dono prachalit hain. Pucca Bigha adhiktat registry aur sarkari kamo mein upyog hota hai, jabki Kucha Bigha ka upyog kai baar kheti ke liye local star par hota hai.
