West Bengal Advanced Polygon Land Calculator: টেড়া-বেঁকা জমির সঠিক পরিমাপ (Jomir Hisab)

West Bengal-e jomi porimap kora kafi jotil kaj, karon ekhane rural ebong urban areas-e alada alada unit bebohar hoy. Rural areas-e Decimal (Satak) ebong urban areas-e Kottah (Katha) ebong Chatak-er hisab cholte thake. Kintu jokhoni jomi tedi-medi (Irregular/Polygon) hoy, tokhoni sateek area ber kora kothin hoye pore.

Shayan Smart Tools-er ei **WB Advanced Land Tool** Heron's Formula ebong Multi-Triangle method bebohar kore 100% sateek result dey. Apnar jomi jotoi tedi-medi hok na keno, ei tool-er maddhome apni Decimal, Kottah ebong Chatak-er sateek hisab paben.

কিভাবে ব্যবহার করবেন? (Step-by-Step Guide)

  1. Jomir chari diker map (A, B, C, D) feet-e nin ebong jomi ke triangles-e divide korun.
  2. Proti-ti triangle-er 3-ti side-er map calculator-e enter korun.
  3. Jodi jomi boro hoy, tobe "+ Add Triangle" button click kore aro triangle jog korun.
  4. Shesh-e "Calculate" click korle apni sateek results paben.
