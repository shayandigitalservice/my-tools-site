DBT Payment Status Check Online 2026

Direct Paisa Check Kare (Official Method)

👉 DBT Payment Status Check Kare
Quick Info:
• Mode: Online (PFMS Portal)
• Required: Bank Account / Aadhaar
• Time: 2-5 minutes

DBT Payment Kya Hai

DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) ek system hai jisme government schemes ka paisa directly aapke bank account me transfer hota hai jaise LPG subsidy, pension, scholarship, etc.

DBT Payment Status Kaise Check Kare

Step 1 – Upar Diye Gaye Button Par Click Kare

Official PFMS website open ho jayegi.

Step 2 – “Know Your Payments” Option Select Kare

Website par payment status check option select kare.

Step 3 – Details Enter Kare

Bank account number ya Aadhaar number enter kare.

Step 4 – Bank Select Kare

Apna bank choose kare.

Step 5 – OTP / Captcha Fill Kare

Verification complete kare.

Step 6 – Status Check Kare

Aapka DBT payment status screen par show ho jayega.

Kaun Kaun DBT Use Karte Hai

  • LPG Gas Subsidy
  • Pension Yojana
  • Scholarship
  • PM Kisan Yojana
  • Other Government Schemes

Agar Payment Nahi Aaya To Kya Kare

1. Aadhaar Bank Link Check Kare

Aadhaar account se linked hona chahiye.

2. Bank Details Verify Kare

Account number sahi hona chahiye.

3. Scheme Status Check Kare

Scheme active hai ya nahi check kare.

Important Tips

  • Sirf official PFMS website use kare
  • OTP kisi ke sath share na kare
  • Correct details enter kare
  • Regular status check kare

Conclusion

DBT payment status check karna bahut easy hai. Upar diye gaye button se aap direct official website par jaakar apna paisa status turant check kar sakte ho.
