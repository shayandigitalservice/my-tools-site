Driving License Online Kaise Banaye 2026

Driving License (DL) India me ek mandatory document hai agar aap vehicle chalate hain. 2026 me RTO aur Ministry of Road Transport ne online process simplify kar diya hai, jisse log ghar baithe hi DL apply, verify aur download kar sakte hain. Is step-by-step Hinglish guide me hum cover karenge kaise aap **Driving License online apply kare**, documents prepare kare, aur bina registered mobile number ke bhi process complete kare.

Tip: Driving License apply karne se pehle apna Aadhaar aur RTO registration details ready rakhein.

Step 1: Official RTO Website Visit Kare

Sabse pehle apne state ka official RTO website visit karein. Central portal: https://parivahan.gov.in. Yahaan se aap secure aur verified DL apply kar sakte hain.

Step 2: Learner License Verify Kare

Agar aap pehli baar driving license apply kar rahe hain, to pehle **Learner License (LL)** hona zaruri hai. LL ke bina permanent DL apply nahi hota. Agar LL hai, to aap online application form me enter kar sakte hain.

Step 3: Online Application Form Fill Kare

  1. Form type choose kare: New DL / Renewal / International Driving Permit
  2. Personal details fill kare: Name, Date of Birth, Address
  3. LL number (agar applicable ho) ya previous DL details enter kare
  4. Document upload kare: Passport size photo, Signature, Address proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID etc.)
  5. Captcha verify karein aur submit karein

Step 4: Appointment Schedule Kare

Form submit karne ke baad aapko RTO slot select karne ka option milega. Yahaan aap apne convenient date aur time choose kar sakte hain. Ye online slot booking process se aapko **RTO visit me long waiting avoid** karne me help milegi.

Step 5: Fees Payment

Online portal pe aapko fees pay karna hoga. Payment ke liye net banking, debit/credit card aur UPI options available hain. Receipt generate kar lein, future reference ke liye.

Step 6: Driving Test & Verification

Selected date pe RTO me jaake **driving test** complete karein. Test me traffic rules aur practical driving check hoti hai. Agar pass ho jate hain, to RTO aapke DL ka process initiate karta hai.

Step 7: Download & Print Driving License

RTO approval ke baad aapko online **DL PDF download** ka option milega. Ye PDF officially valid hoti hai aur print karke use ki ja sakti hai. PDF ka password usually aapka DOB format me hota hai: DDMMYYYY.

Step 8: Common Errors & Solutions

  • Form submit error: Browser refresh karein ya dusra browser try karein.
  • LL number mismatch: Correct LL details enter karein.
  • Appointment slot issue: 2-3 different dates check karein.
  • Payment failed: Transaction details verify karein aur RTO helpline contact karein.

FAQs

  1. Question: Driving License online safe hai kya?
    Answer: Haan, official RTO portal se apply karna safe aur secure hai.
  2. Question: Bina mobile number DL apply ho sakta hai?
    Answer: Haan, alternative verification methods available hain jaise Aadhaar or Enrollment ID.
  3. Question: DL download ka password kya hota hai?
    Answer: DOB format me hota hai, DDMMYYYY.
  4. Question: Renewal process kya hai?
    Answer: Renewal ke liye previous DL number aur expiry date enter karke online form submit karein.

Pro Tips

  • Application ke liye stable internet use karein.
  • Browser latest version me ho aur pop-ups enable ho.
  • Official RTO site ka hi use karein, third-party apps avoid karein.
  • Documents scan quality high rakhein, colors clear ho.
  • Future me mobile number link kar lein, taki OTP aur TOTP easy ho jaye.
