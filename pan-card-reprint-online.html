PAN Card Reprint Online Kaise Kare 2026

Duplicate PAN Card Order Karne Ka Complete Guide

PAN card India me ek important financial document hai. Bank account open karne, income tax return file karne aur financial transactions ke liye PAN card zaruri hota hai.

Agar aapka PAN card lost ho gaya hai, damaged ho gaya hai ya aapko naya physical copy chahiye to aap online PAN card reprint order kar sakte ho.

PAN Card Reprint Kya Hota Hai

PAN card reprint ka matlab hai ki aapka existing PAN number same rehta hai aur uski ek new physical copy issue ki jati hai.

PAN Card Reprint Karne Ke Liye Required Details

  • PAN number
  • Aadhaar number
  • Date of birth
  • Registered mobile number

PAN Card Reprint Online Kaise Kare

Step 1

Official website open kare:

NSDL PAN Services Website

Step 2

Reprint of PAN Card option select kare.

Step 3

PAN number aur Aadhaar details enter kare.

Step 4

OTP verification complete kare.

Step 5

Reprint request submit kare aur payment complete kare.

PAN Card Reprint Fees

  • India address: ₹50 approx
  • Foreign address: ₹959 approx

PAN Card Delivery Time

Normally PAN card reprint order karne ke baad 10–15 days me PAN card address par deliver ho jata hai.

PAN Card Reprint Ke Benefits

  • Lost PAN card ka replacement mil jata hai
  • Same PAN number rehta hai
  • Online process easy hai
