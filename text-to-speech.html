About Text to Speech Converter

This free online Text to Speech converter allows you to transform written text into natural sounding voice instantly. Text to Speech (TTS) technology is widely used for accessibility, language learning, content creation, and productivity. With this tool, you can listen to written content instead of reading it manually.

The tool supports multiple voices and languages depending on your device and browser capabilities. It works directly in your web browser using built-in speech synthesis technology, so you do not need to install any software. You can adjust voice speed and pitch to customize how the speech sounds.

How to Use This Tool

  • Type or paste your text into the text box.
  • Select your preferred voice from the voice list.
  • Adjust the speech speed if needed.
  • Change the pitch to make the voice deeper or higher.
  • Click the Speak button to hear the text.
  • Use the Stop button to stop the voice anytime.

Features
