UP Land Area Addition: उत्तर प्रदेश जमीन का कुल जोड़ कैसे निकालें?

Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein zameen ki kharid-firokht (Property dealing) aur kheti ki zameen ka hisab-kitab hamesha se thoda pechida raha hai. Jab ek kisan ya property dealer ke paas UP Bhulekh (Khatauni) mein darj kayi chote-chote plots hote hain, toh unhe ek saath jodna (Total Sum) ek badi chunauti ban jati hai.

The UP Land Area Addition Tool by Shayan Smart Tools is the ultimate digital solution for farmers, lekbpals, and real estate agents in Uttar Pradesh. Since the land measurement system in UP follows the Bigha, Biswa, and Biswansi methodology, it operates on a "Base 20" logic. This means 20 Biswansi make 1 Biswa and 20 Biswa make 1 Pucca Bigha. Our tool automates this complex carry-forward logic, ensuring 100% accuracy and saving you from manual calculation errors.

UP Land Measurement Standards (Bigha-Biswa System)

Uttar Pradesh mein zameen ki ikaiyan (Units) purnatah state revenue standards par aadharit hoti hain. Hamara tool Pucca Bigha ke standard calculation par kaam karta hai jo ki pure UP mein manya hai:

Unit Name (इकाई) Relation (सम्बन्ध) Sq Ft (वर्ग फीट - Approx)

Note: Yeh calculation Uttar Pradesh ke manak (Standard) Pucca Bigha par aadharit hai. Kucha Bigha ke liye calculation alag ho sakti hai.

5 Practical Examples for UP Land Sum (यूपी जमीन के जोड़ के उदाहरण)

Aaiye dekhte hain ki UP mein zameen ka jod kaise kaam karta hai:

  1. Example 1: 10 Biswa + 15 Biswa = 1 Bigha 5 Biswa (Simple math mein ye 25 Biswa hota, par UP land logic mein 20 Biswa ka 1 Bigha ban jata hai).
  2. Example 2: 18 Biswansi + 5 Biswansi = 1 Biswa 3 Biswansi.
  3. Example 3: 3 Bigha 12 Biswa aur 1 Bigha 10 Biswa ka jod 5 Bigha 2 Biswa hoga.
  4. Example 4: Agar 3 plots jode (5 Biswansi + 10 Biswansi + 10 Biswansi), toh total 1 Biswa 5 Biswansi aayega.
  5. Example 5: 0.5060 Hectare record ko jab hum Bigha mein badalte hain aur jodte hain, toh sateek Biswa result milna zaruri hai.
