UP Land Area Addition: Uttar Pradesh Mein Zameen Ka Total Jod Kaise Nikalein?

Doston, Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein jab bhi hum zameen ki baat karte hain, toh sabse pehle dimaag mein Bigha, Biswa aur Biswansi aata hai. Halaki UP Bhulekh (official records) mein area Hectare mein darj hota hai, lekin local star par sauda aur registry hamesha local units mein hi hoti hai. Sabse badi problem tab aati hai jab aapko apne 2-3 chote plots ya khet ko ek saath jodna (Sum) hota hai.

Agar aap ek kisan hain, property dealer hain, ya fir apne purvajon ki zameen ka hisab kar rahe hain, toh ye UP Land Area Addition Tool aapke liye hi bana hai. Manual calculation mein aksar "Carry Forward" ki galti ho jati hai, kyunki ye normal math nahi hai. Yahan 20 Biswansi milkar 1 Biswa banta hai aur 20 Biswa milkar 1 Pucca Bigha. Shayan Smart Tools ka ye calculator isi "Base 20" logic par chalta hai taaki aapka result 100% accurate rahe.
