Aadhaar Card Bina Registered Mobile Number Ke Download Kaise Kare 2026

Aadhaar card har Indian citizen ke liye ek important document hai. Lekin kai log apna Aadhaar **bina registered mobile number ke** download karna chahte hain. Is guide me aapko step by step bataenge ki kaise aap apna Aadhaar card online 2026 me bina mobile number ke download kar sakte hain.

Tip: Aadhaar download karne ke liye aapke paas basic details ready honi chahiye, jaise Aadhaar number ya Enrollment ID.

Aadhaar Card Download Without Registered Mobile Number

  1. 1 Visit kare official UIDAI website: https://uidai.gov.in
  2. 2 “Download Aadhaar” section me click kare.
  3. 3 Option choose kare: “Aadhaar Number” ya “Enrollment ID”.
  4. 4 Form me apna full name, date of birth, aur gender fill karein.
  5. 5 Captcha enter kare aur request OTP ke bajaye “Alternative OTP” ya “TOTP” option choose kare (agar available ho).
  6. 6 Agar mobile number linked nahi hai, to aapko “Download via EID + QR Code verification” ka option milega.
  7. 7 Aadhaar PDF download ke liye ek **password** set hota hai, jo aapka DOB (DDMMYYYY) hota hai.
  8. 8 Download kare aur PDF open kare using DOB password.

Important Tips

  • Ensure kare ki aapke paas Enrollment ID ya Aadhaar number ready ho.
  • Browser me pop-ups enable honi chahiye taaki PDF download ho sake.
  • Password ke liye correct date of birth use karein.
  • PDF secure rakhein aur share na karein sensitive information.

Common Problems & Solutions

  • Aadhaar download link kaam nahi kar raha → Browser refresh karein ya dusra browser try karein.
  • Enrollment ID missing → Aadhaar enrollment slip check karein.
  • PDF open nahi ho raha → DOB format check karein (DDMMYYYY).
  • OTP option available nahi → Alternative TOTP ya QR code verification use karein.
