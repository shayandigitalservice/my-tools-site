1. Introduction: West Bengal's Rich Land History

West Bengal, jo apni upjau dharti aur mitti ke liye jana jata hai, wahan zameen ki naap-jokh (Land Measurement) ka system sadiyon purana hai. British kaal se lekar aaj ke digital yug tak, Bengal ne apne paramparagat units jaise Bigha, Katha, aur Chatak ko sanjo kar rakha hai. Jab aap Kolkata ke posh ilakon se lekar Sundarban ke delta tak ki zameen ki baat karte hain, toh har jagah paimaish ka tarika West Bengal Revenue Department ke standard ke mutabiq hota hai.

2. Core Units: Bigha, Katha, and the Unique Chatak

West Bengal mein zameen ko teen pramukh hisson mein baata gaya hai. Inhe samajhna isliye zaroori hai kyunki registry aur mutation ke waqt data isi format mein hota hai.

What is 1 Bigha in West Bengal?

West Bengal mein 1 Bigha standard roop se 14,400 Square Feet hota hai. Agar hum ise meter mein badlein, toh yeh lagbhag 1,337.8 Square Meters hota hai. Dhyaan dene wali baat yeh hai ki Bengal ka Bigha, Uttar Pradesh ya Bihar ke Bigha ($27,225 \text{ sq. ft.}$) se kafi chota hota hai, lekin Assam ke Bigha ke barabar hota hai.

The Concept of 20 Katha

Bengal mein 1 Bigha = 20 Katha hota hai. Har katha ka size 720 square feet hota hai. Shehri ilakon mein plots ki kharid-farixt mukhya roop se Katha mein hi hoti hai. 1 Katha zameen lagbhag 30 feet lambi aur 24 feet chaudi hoti hai.

Chatak: The Micro Measurement

Chatak West Bengal ki sabse khas aur choti standard unit hai. 1 Katha = 16 Chatak. Iska matlab hai ki 1 Chatak barabar 45 square feet hota hai. Jab zameen ki registry point-to-point accuracy ke saath hoti hai, toh Chatak ka hona bahut mahatvapurna ho jata hai.

5. How to Check Land Records in WB (Banglarbhumi)

Digital India ke antargat, West Bengal sarkar ne **Banglarbhumi Portal** shuru kiya hai. Is portal par aap apne Mouza, Khatian Number aur Plot Number ke zariye zameen ka area dekh sakte hain. Aksar wahan area Hectare ya Acre mein hota hai. Agar aapko confuse ho raha hai ki wo area kitna Bigha-Katha hai, toh aap humara calculator upar se use kar sakte hain.
