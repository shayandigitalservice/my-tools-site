Introduction

West Bengal mein bhumi paimaish (land measurement) ke liye Bigha, Katha, aur Chatak ka upyog sadiyon se chala aa raha hai. West Bengal ka land system kafi had tak Assam se milta-julta hai, lekin yahan Decimal (Shatak) ka bhi bahut prachalan hai. Property registry aur kheti ki zameen ke hisab ke liye Square Feet (Sq Ft) mein conversion samajhna anivary hai.
