About This GST Calculator

The GST Calculator is a free online tool that helps you easily calculate Goods and Services Tax in India. It allows you to quickly add or remove GST from any amount using standard GST rates such as 5%, 12%, 18% and 28%.

This calculator is useful for shop owners, small business owners, accountants, freelancers and students who want to calculate GST instantly without manual calculations. Instead of calculating tax manually, you can simply enter the amount, select the GST rate and get the result immediately.

The tool works directly in your web browser and does not require any installation or registration. It is designed with a simple interface so anyone can use it easily on mobile phones, tablets and desktop devices.

How to Use

  • Enter the amount in the input field.
  • Select the GST rate such as 5%, 12%, 18% or 28%.
  • Click the Add GST button to calculate GST amount.
  • Click the Remove GST button if the amount already includes GST.
  • The calculator will instantly display the GST amount and total value.
