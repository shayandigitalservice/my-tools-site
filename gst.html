About GST Calculator

This GST Calculator helps you quickly calculate Goods and Services Tax in India. You can easily add GST to an amount or remove GST from a total amount using standard GST rates like 5%, 12%, 18% and 28%.

How to Use

  • Enter amount
  • Select GST rate
  • Click Add GST or Remove GST
