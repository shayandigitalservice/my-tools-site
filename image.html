About Image Compressor Tool

The Image Compressor tool helps you reduce image file size quickly and efficiently without losing noticeable quality. Large image files can slow down websites, increase upload time, and consume unnecessary storage space. With this free online compressor, you can easily reduce image size in KB and make your images optimized for websites, social media, and email attachments.

This tool works directly in your browser and does not require any software installation. Simply upload your image, enter the desired target size in KB, and click the compress button. The tool automatically adjusts image quality to reach the closest possible size while keeping the image visually clear.

How to Use the Image Compressor

  1. Click the upload area or drag and drop an image.
  2. Enter the target file size in KB.
  3. Click the Compress Image button.
  4. Preview the compressed image and download it instantly.
