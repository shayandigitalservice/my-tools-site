Bihar Advanced Polygon Land Calculator: Tedi-Medi Zameen ki Sateek Napai

Bihar mein zameen ka batwara aur napai aksar vivad ka karan banti hai, khas karke jab khet ya plot **Irregular (Polygon)** shape mein hon. Bihar ke bhulekh records mein area bohot bariki se darj hota hai, isliye Dhur aur Dhurki ka hisab rakhna jaruri hai.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **Bihar Advanced Polygon Calculator** Heron's Formula ka upyog karke zameen ko kai triangles mein baant kar uska sateek area nikalta hai. Chahe aapki zameen Patna mein ho, Muzaffarpur mein ya Bhagalpur mein, ye tool Bihar ke standard **Bigha-Kattha-Dhur** logic par based hai.

उपयोग कैसे करें? (Step-by-Step Guide)

  1. Apni tedi zameen ko triangles mein divide karein (Diagram dekhein).
  2. Har triangle ki teeno sides ko feet mein naapiye.
  3. Tool mein pehle triangle ki values daaliye.
  4. "+ Add Triangle" button click karke baki triangles ka data jodiye.
  5. Calculate par click karte hi aapko sateek Bihar standard result mil jayega.
