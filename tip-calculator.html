Tip Calculator

About Tip Calculator

The Tip Calculator is a simple online tool that helps you quickly calculate the tip amount and the total bill. When you visit restaurants, cafes, hotels, or order food delivery, it is common to leave a tip for the service staff. Instead of manually calculating the tip percentage, this tool allows you to calculate it instantly.

All you need to do is enter the bill amount and the tip percentage you want to give. The calculator will automatically display the tip amount and the total bill including the tip. This tool is very useful for people who want a quick and accurate way to split bills or calculate tips without doing manual math.

How to Use the Tip Calculator

Using this calculator is very easy. First, enter the total bill amount in the first input field. Then enter the tip percentage you want to give in the second field. After that, click the Calculate button. The tool will instantly show the tip amount and the final bill including the tip.

For example, if your bill is ₹1000 and you want to give a 10% tip, the calculator will show a ₹100 tip and a total bill of ₹1100.

Features of This Tool

This online Tip Calculator is fast, lightweight, and easy to use. It works directly in your browser without requiring any downloads or registration. The simple interface makes it convenient for anyone to calculate tips quickly.

The tool works smoothly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers. Whether you are dining out with friends or calculating a restaurant bill, this calculator helps you save time and avoid calculation mistakes.
