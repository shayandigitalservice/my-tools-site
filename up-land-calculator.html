Introduction to Uttar Pradesh Land Mapping System

Uttar Pradesh me jab bhi aap koi plot, farm house layout, ya agricultural farmlands purchase ya sell karte hain, toh sabse pehle us zameen ka **Land Area Calculator** chalana sabse important framework mana jata hai. Understanding Local Uttar Pradesh Revenue Unit Metrics

1. Hectare & Acre Standard Base: Bhulekh UP portal par jab aap apni jamabandi ya Khatauni copy nikalte hain, toh zameen ka size hamesha Hectare me likha hota hai. Sarkari barabari ke anusar **1 Hectare holds exactly 2.471 Acres** land allocation. Sahi calculation matrix me 1 Hectare me official rules ke mutabik **1,07,600 Square Feet** standard design parameters mapping apply hote hain.

2. Pucca vs Kacha Bigha System: Standard revenue records ke mutabik **1 Pucca Bigha contains exactly 27,225 Square Feet**. Iske contrast me jo **Kacha Bigha** hota hai aur standard framework parameters baseline loop mapping ke mutabik hamesha **9,075 Square Feet** yield karta hai.
