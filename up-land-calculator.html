Uttar Pradesh Land Area Calculator: Bigha, Biswa, and Dhur Guide

Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein zameen ki paimaish (measurement) ka tarika India ke baaki rajyon se alag aur kaafi purana hai. Yahan mukhya roop se Pucca Bigha ka istemal hota hai. Agar aap Lucknow, Kanpur, Noida, ya Varanasi mein zameen kharid rahe hain, toh aapko area calculation ke liye sahi tool ki zaroorat hoti hai. Shayan Digital Service ka yeh calculator UP ke standard revenue system par adharit hai.

How to Use the UP Land Calculator?

Is tool ko use karna bahut saral hai. Aap niche diye gaye steps follow karke apni zameen ka accurate area nikal sakte hain:

  • Step 1: Zameen ki kul Lambai (Length) Feet ya Meter mein enter karein.
  • Step 2: Zameen ki kul Chaudai (Width) enter karein.
  • Step 3: Unit dropdown se apni unit (Feet/Meter) select karein.
  • Step 4: "Convert" button dabate hi aapko Bigha, Biswa aur Dhur ka result mil jayega.

Agar aapko zameen ki keemat bhi nikalni hai, toh humara Land Cost Calculator zaroor use karein. Saath hi, Bihar ke users ke liye humara Bihar Land Calculator bhi live hai.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: UP mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Biswa hote hain?
Ans: Uttar Pradesh mein 1 Bigha mein total 20 Biswa hote hain.
Q2: Kya Kucha Bigha aur Pucca Bigha ek hi hain?
Ans: Nahi, Pucca Bigha 27,225 sq. ft. ka hota hai, jabki Kucha Bigha iska lagbhag ek-tihaayi (1/3) hota hai.
Q3: 1 Biswa mein kitne square feet hote hain?
Ans: 1 Biswa barabar 1,361.25 square feet hota hai.
