UP Land Measurement Guide 2026: Everything About Bigha and Biswa

Uttar Pradesh, jo Bharat ka sabse bada rajya hai, wahan zameen ki naap-jokh ka system kaafi purana aur mukhya roop se Pucca Bigha aur Kucha Bigha par adharit hai. Jab aap Lucknow, Kanpur, Noida ya Varanasi mein zameen kharidte hain, toh aapko "Biswa" aur "Biswansi" jaise shabd sunne ko milenge. Shayan Digital Service ke is vishal guide mein hum UP ke land units ko vistar se samjhayenge.

1. What is 1 Bigha in Uttar Pradesh?

UP mein 'Bigha' do tarah ke hote hain. Mukhya roop se sarkari dastawezon (Bhulekh UP) mein Pucca Bigha ka istemal hota hai. 1 Pucca Bigha barabar 27,225 Square Feet hota hai. Wahi dusri taraf Kucha Bigha iska lagbhag ek-tihaayi (1/3) hota hai, jo zyada tar dehati ilakon mein kisan aapsi baat-cheet mein use karte hain. Sahi naap ke liye humara UP Land Calculator use karein.

2. Biswa and Biswansi: The Sub-units

UP mein Bigha ko Biswa mein baata jata hai. 1 Bigha = 20 Biswa. Ek Biswa ka area 1,361.25 Square Feet hota hai. Iske niche ki unit ko Biswansi (ya Dhur) kehte hain. 1 Biswa mein 20 Biswansi hote hain. Yeh units bilkul waisi hi hain jaisi Bihar mein Kattha aur Dhur hoti hain, bas naam ka antar hai.
