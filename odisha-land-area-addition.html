Odisha Land Area Addition: How to Calculate Total Area of Multiple Plots?

The most precise system to add multiple land patches in Acre, Guntha, and Biswas formats matching the official Bhulekh Odisha Record of Rights (RoR).

1. Introduction: Custom Land Metrics Addition in Odisha

In Odisha, land computation ranges from regional frameworks to standard global systems. While government departments and online portals like Bhulekh Odisha log lands under Hectares, Ares, and Square Meters, local farmers and builders systematically carry out deals in traditional metrics: Acres, Guntha, and Biswas (also known as Asba in several districts).

The core computational problem is that adding up different land metrics doesn't run on standard base-10 decimal scales. Odisha land configurations handle custom numerical overflow mechanics. If values are added directly via typical calculators, results create major discrepancies during documentation, legal valuation, or mutation procedures at the Tahasildar offices.

2. The Mathematical Rules of Acre, Guntha, and Biswas

To accurately compute multiple land sheets, our smart engine applies the traditional scale laws used by land surveyors (Amin) across Odisha:

  • 1 Acre = 40 Guntha
  • 1 Guntha = 16 Biswas (Asba)
  • 1 Acre = 43,560 Square Feet

This means when the sum of Biswas crosses 16, it transfers 1 complete Guntha over to the Guntha scale. Similarly, when accumulated Guntha counts reach or exceed 40, they carry forward as 1 Acre. This intricate double-carry math is resolved instantly by Shayan Smart Tools.
