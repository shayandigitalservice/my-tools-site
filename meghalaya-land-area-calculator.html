Understanding Local Meghalaya Revenue Unit Metrics & Conversions

Jab aap plot boundary coordinates length (lambai) aur width (chaudai) feet ya meter me measure karte hain, toh follow kiye jane wale conversion factors persistent standard ratios me bind hote hain:

1. Hectare & Acre Framework: Official revenue survey papers aur government cadastral mapping grids me land measurements Hectare aur Acre baseline scaling par register kiye jaate hain. **1 Hectare me exactly 2.471 Acres** hote hain. Mathematical mapping engine ke tehat **1 Hectare holds 1,07,639 Square Feet**, jo regional land mutation records ka benchmark factor setup hai.

2. Square Feet & Square Meter Conversion: Real estate property valuation, residential layout maps, aur plot registration bills me Square Feet sabse ziada istemal hone wali unit hai. Baseline logic ke tehat **1 Acre me exactly 43,560 Square Feet** standard units mapped hote hain. Jabki metric system me **1 Square Meter me 10.764 Square Feet** calculate hote hain.

3. Assam-Meghalaya Border Metrics (Bigha & Katha System): Meghalaya ke border districts (Ri-Bhoi, West Garo Hills, South West Garo Hills) me Assam-adjacent trade areas me traditional Bigha aur Katha units ka chalan bhi hai. Is regional mesh parameters me **1 Border Bigha = 14,400 Square Feet** hota hai, aur **1 Bigha me 5 Katha** hote hain, yaani **1 Katha = 2,880 Square Feet** area footprint hold karta hai.

Land Records Verification & Legal Registration Workflow in Meghalaya

Meghalaya me property transfer ya land ownership documentation complete karne ke liye teen zaroori stages follow ki jaati hain:

  1. Local Headman (Raid/Dorbar Shnong) Verification: Land boundary and possession Certificate sabse pehle local Village Dorbar aur Headman se verify karwaya jaata hai.
  2. Autonomous District Council (ADC) Clearance: Land transfer suitability checking KHADC, JHADC, ya GHADC ke Revenue & Land Affairs Directorate se obtain ki jaati hai.
  3. Directorate of Land Records & Surveys Verification: Final survey demarcation and digital land mapping records government land revenue registry portals (MegRevenue) par upload karke clear registration seal di jaati hai.
