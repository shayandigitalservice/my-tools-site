About Barcode Generator

This free online Barcode Generator allows you to quickly create barcodes for products, inventory systems, business labels, and other applications. A barcode is a machine-readable representation of data that is widely used in retail stores, warehouses, logistics, and product packaging. With this tool, you can easily convert text or numbers into a barcode image that can be scanned by barcode readers.

The tool uses the popular CODE128 barcode format, which supports letters, numbers, and symbols. It is one of the most commonly used barcode standards because of its high data capacity and reliability. You can generate a barcode instantly and download it as an image for printing or digital use.

How to Use This Tool

  • Enter the text, product code, or number in the input field.
  • Click the Generate Barcode button.
  • Your barcode will appear instantly on the screen.
  • Click the Download Barcode button to save the barcode image.
  • Use the downloaded barcode for labels, packaging, or scanning systems.
