DBT Link Kya Hota Hai

DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) link ka matlab hai aapke Aadhaar number ko bank account se connect karna taaki government schemes ka paisa direct account me aa sake.

DBT Link Karne Ke Liye Kya Zaruri Hai

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account
  • Mobile Number (Aadhaar se linked)
