Introduction (Dumdar Prastavana)

Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein jab bhi aap apni zameen ka Bhulekh (Khatauni) nikalte hain, toh kshetrafal hamesha Hectare mein likha hota hai. Lekin UP ke gramin aur shehari ilakon mein zameen ka lenden aaj bhi Pucca Bigha, Biswa aur Biswansi mein hota hai. Kisanon aur property kharidne walon ke liye `0.2530 Hectare` jaise anko ko samajhna mushkil ho jata hai.
