Kahan Kaam Aata Hai JPG Format?

JPG format sabse zyada use hone wala image format hai. Ye size mein chhota hota hai aur har device (Mobile, PC, Tablet) par sahi se khulta hai. Students ko admission forms ke liye aur developers ko website load speed badhane ke liye aksar JPG format ki zaroorat padti hai.
