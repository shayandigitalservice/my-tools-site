Age Calculator Use Karne ke Faide

Wese toh log dimaag se bhi calculate kar lete hain, lekin online tool use karne ke kuch khaas faide hain:

  • Accuracy: Ye tool Years, Months aur Days ke saath-saath Total Minutes aur Seconds bhi batata hai.
  • Job Forms: Sabhi exams ke liye exact age limit check karne mein help karta hai.
  • Leap Year Support: Ye leap years ko bhi dhyaan mein rakhta hai, isliye calculation hamesha sahi hoti hai.

Kaise Use Karein? (Step-by-Step)

  1. Sabse pehle Age Calculator page par jayein.
  2. Apni Date of Birth (DOB) calendar se select karein.
  3. Agar kisi purani ya future date par apni age dekhni hai, toh "Today's Date" ko change karein (nhi toh use wesa hi rehne dein).
  4. "Calculate" button par click karein. Bas, aapka result screen par hoga!
