LIC Online Policy Apply Kya Hai?

LIC Online Policy Apply ek process hai jisme aap Life Insurance Corporation of India ke official website ya mobile app ke through nayi policy ke liye apply karte ho. Paperless aur convenient process hai.

Benefits of LIC Online Policy Apply / Renewal

  • Ghar baithe apply ya renew
  • Instant policy copy aur receipt
  • Multiple policy plans ka comparison
  • Secure payment options
  • Policy status track karna easy

Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar, PAN, Passport
  • Address Proof: Aadhaar, Passport, Utility Bill
  • Bank account details for premium payment
  • Passport size photo (optional for some plans)

Step by Step Guide – LIC Online Policy Apply

Step 1 Official LIC Website Visit Kare

  • LIC India Official Website

Step 2 Policy Plan Choose Kare

Nayi policy ke liye plan select kare: Term Plan, Endowment, Money Back, Jeevan Anand etc. Plans ka comparison karna important hai.

Step 3 Personal & Nominee Details Fill Kare

Full name, date of birth, gender, nominee name, relation aur contact details enter kare. Details correct hone chahiye for future claims.

Step 4 Payment & Receipt

Online payment kare via Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card, UPI ya Wallet. Payment confirmation ke baad policy receipt generate hota hai.

Step 5 Policy Document Download / Email

Policy document PDF me download kare ya registered email pe receive kare. Printout bhi rakh sakte ho.

LIC Mobile App se Policy Apply / Renewal

  • LIC Mobile App – Android / iOS
  • App me login karke nayi policy apply ya renewal ka option select kare
  • Payment aur document upload same process ke through complete hota hai

Common Issues & Solutions

  • Payment failure – retry ya alternate method use kare
  • Policy status update me delay – customer care contact kare
  • Document verification issues – upload correct documents
  • Login issues – password reset kare

Tips for Fast & Safe Online Policy Application

  • Official website ya app hi use kare
  • Correct aur valid documents upload kare
  • Secure device aur internet use kare
  • Payment proof save kare
