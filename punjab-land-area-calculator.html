Punjab Land Measurement: A Complete Professional Guide

Punjab Land Revenue metrics aur PLRS portal jamabandi structures ke anusaar length aur width se zameen nikalne ka master blueprint guide.

Punjab mein zameen ki napai (Land Measurement) ka ek bohot hi purana aur sateek system hai. Chahe aap Amritsar mein ho ya Ludhiana mein, Punjab Revenue Department (Jamabandi) hamesha **Killa, Kanal aur Marla** ka prayog karta hai. Understanding Punjab Land Units (ਪੰਜਾਬ ਜ਼ਮੀਨ ਦੀਆਂ ਇਕਾਈਆਂ)

Punjab mein zameen naapne ke liye 'Karam' ka use kiya jata hai. Ek standard Karam 66 inches (5.5 feet) ka hota hai. Niche di gayi table se aap units ko behtar samajh sakte hain:
