Punjab Land Area Calculator: Killa, Kanal & Marla Converter

Punjab mein zameen ki napai (Land Measurement) ka ek purana aur sateek riwaaj hai jo Killa, Kanal, aur Marla par adharit hai. Jamabandi ho ya Registry, Punjab ke farmers aur property dealers hamesha inhi units ka istemal karte hain. Hamara tool 2026 ke updated revenue laws ke mutabik sateek calculations deta hai.

How to Use the Punjab Land Tool?

  • Feet mein apni zameen ki lambai (Length) aur chaudai (Width) naapiye.
  • Inputs box mein values enter kijiye.
  • Punjab standard result pane ke liye 'Calculate' click karein.
  • Aap results ko Punjabi ya English kisi bhi language mein dekh aur share kar sakte hain.

Example Calculations

1. Standard Killa: 220ft x 198ft = 43,560 sq ft (1 Killa).
2. Small Plot: 30ft x 45ft = 1,361 sq ft (Approx 5 Marla).
3. Commercial: 60ft x 90ft = 5,400 sq ft (Almost 1 Kanal).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Punjab mein 1 Killa kitna hota hai?
Ans: Punjab mein 1 Killa 1 Acre ke barabar hota hai, yani 43,560 Square Feet.
Q2: 1 Kanal mein kitne Marla hote hain?
Ans: Standard Punjab measurement ke mutabik 1 Kanal mein 20 Marla hote hain.
