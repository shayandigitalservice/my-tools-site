Punjab Land Measurement: A Complete Professional Guide

Punjab mein zameen ki napai (Land Measurement) ka ek bohot hi purana aur sateek system hai. Chahe aap Amritsar mein ho ya Ludhiana mein, Punjab Revenue Department (Jamabandi) hamesha **Killa, Kanal aur Marla** ka prayog karta hai. Hamara **Punjab Land Area Calculator** 2026 ke updated standards par kaam karta hai, jo aapko Length aur Width ke aadhar par turant sateek parinaam deta hai.

Understanding Punjab Land Units (ਪੰਜਾਬ ਜ਼ਮੀਨ ਦੀਆਂ ਇਕਾਈਆਂ)

Punjab mein zameen naapne ke liye 'Karam' ka use kiya jata hai. Ek standard Karam 66 inches (5.5 feet) ka hota hai. Niche di gayi table se aap units ko behtar samajh sakte hain:

1 Sarsai (ਸਰਸਾਈ)1 Karam × 1 Karam30.25 Sq Ft
