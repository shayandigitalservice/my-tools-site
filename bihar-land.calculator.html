2. Core Measurement Units in Bihar

Bihar mein zameen naapne ka ek fix formula hai jo har district mein follow hota hai. Niche humne isse vistar se samjhaya hai:

A. Bigha (The Largest Unit)

Bihar mein 1 Bigha ka matlab hota hai 27,225 Square Feet. Yeh unit mukhya roop se khet aur bade bagano ko naapne ke liye use hoti hai. Bihar ka Bigha, Assam ke Bigha se lagbhag dugna bada hota hai.

B. Kattha (The Most Popular Unit)

Shehri ilakon mein Kattha sabse mahatvapurna hai. 1 Bigha = 20 Kattha. 1 Kattha ka area 1,361.25 Square Feet hota hai. Ghar banane ke liye log aksar 1 ya 2 Kattha zameen hi kharidte hain.

C. Dhur (The Smallest Unit)

Zameen ki micro-level naap ke liye Dhur ka use hota hai. 1 Kattha = 20 Dhur. 1 Dhur lagbhag 68.06 square feet ke barabar hota hai.

5. Importance of 'Laggi' in Bihar

Bihar mein naap ke liye 'Laggi' ka bahut bada role hai. Laggi ki lambai har district mein thodi alag ho sakti hai (jaise 4 haath, 5.5 haath, ya 6 haath). Standard survey mein 5.5 haath ki laggi use hoti hai. Agar aapke yahan laggi ka size alag hai, toh 1 Kattha ka area 1361.25 sq. ft. se badal kar kuch aur ho sakta hai.
