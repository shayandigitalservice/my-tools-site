2. Standard Units: Bigha, Kattha, aur Dhur ki Hierarchy

Bihar mein land measurement ek set hierarchy par chalti hai. Isse niche di gayi list se samjhein:

  • 1 Bigha = 20 Kattha
  • 1 Kattha = 20 Dhur
  • 1 Dhur = 20 Dhurki
  • 1 Dhurki = 20 Phurki

Square Feet mein Area:

Bihar ke standard system (jahan 5.5 haath ki laggi chalti hai) ke mutabiq:

  • ✅ 1 Bigha = 27,225 Square Feet
  • ✅ 1 Kattha = 1,361.25 Square Feet
  • ✅ 1 Dhur = 68.06 Square Feet

3. Understanding 'Laggi' in Bihar Land Survey

Bihar mein zameen naapne ki sabse badi paheli 'Laggi' hai. Laggi ek baans (bamboo pole) hota hai jisse zameen ko naapa jata hai. Bihar ke alag-alag districts mein laggi ki lambai alag hoti hai (jaise 4 haath, 5.5 haath, ya 6 haath).

Agar laggi ki lambai badalti hai, toh ek Kattha ka square feet area bhi badal jata hai. Lekin, Bihar Sarkar ne standard survey ke liye 5.5 haath ki laggi ko hi paimaan (standard) mana hai. Hamara calculator isi 5.5 haath ke logic par kaam karta hai jo poore rajya mein sabse zyada manya hai.

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Bihar mein 1 bigha mein kitna decimal hota hai?
Ans: Standard measurement ke hisaab se Bihar mein 1 Bigha lagbhag 62.5 Decimal hota hai.
Q2: 1 Acre mein kitna bigha hota hai Bihar mein?
Ans: Bihar mein 1 Acre lagbhag 1.6 Bigha ke barabar hota hai.
Q3: Bihar Bhulekh par area kaise check karein?
Ans: Aap Bihar Sarkar ki official site 'Biharbhumi' par ja kar apna Khatiyan ya Jamabandi dekh sakte hain, wahan area Bigha-Kattha-Dhur mein hota hai.
