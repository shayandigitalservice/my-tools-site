Bihar Land Measurement Guide 2026: Detailed Analysis of Bigha, Kattha, and Dhur

Quick Summary: Bihar mein land units districts ke hisaab se thoda badal sakti hain, lekin standard system 20 Kattha ka hota hai. Bihar ka 1 Bigha = 27,225 Sq. Ft. hota hai. Sahi naap ke liye humara Bihar Land Measurement Tool use karein.

1. Introduction: Bihar's Ancient Land Culture

Bihar ki bhoomi hamesha se hi upjau rahi hai aur yahan ki arthvyavastha mukhya roop se krishi (agriculture) par adharit hai. Yahi wajah hai ki Bihar mein zameen ki naap-jokh ka itihas kaafi purana aur dilchasp hai. Mughal kaal se lekar British raaj tak, Bihar mein zameen naapne ke tarike badle, lekin jo system aaj bhi sabse zyada lokpriya hai wo hai Bigha, Kattha, aur Dhur.

2. Detailed Breakdown of Land Units in Bihar

Bihar mein zameen naapne ke liye ek hierarchy follow ki jati hai. Agar aapne ek baar is basic formula ko samajh liya, toh aap kabhi dhokha nahi khayenge.

The Standard Bihar Bigha (27,225 Sq. Ft.)

Bihar mein "Bigha" ko do hisson mein dekha jata hai—Pucca Bigha aur Kucha Bigha. Lekin sarkari aur vyavsayik (commercial) taur par Pucca Bigha hi chalta hai. 1 Pucca Bigha barabar 27,225 Square Feet hota hai. Agar aap ise yard mein badlein, toh yeh 3,025 Square Yards hota hai. Yeh unit kaafi badi hoti hai aur mukhya roop se khet ki naap mein use hoti hai.

The Concept of Kattha (1,361.25 Sq. Ft.)

Bihar ke shehri ilakon jaise Patna, Bhagalpur, aur Muzaffarpur mein zameen 'Kattha' ke hisaab se bechi jati hai. 1 Bigha = 20 Kattha. Ek Kattha ka area 1,361.25 Square Feet hota hai. Aksar log zameen kharidte waqt confusion mein rehte hain ki 1 Kattha kitna bada hota hai, toh aap ise aise samajh sakte hain ki yeh lagbhag 40 feet lambi aur 34 feet chaudi zameen hoti hai.

Dhur: The Micro Precision

Jab baat chote plots ki ho, toh Dhur ka istemal hota hai. 1 Kattha = 20 Dhur. Ek Dhur lagbhag 68.06 Square Feet hota hai. Bihar mein Amin (Land Surveyor) aksar Dhur tak ki naap nikalte hain taaki boundary sahi se set ho sake.

3. The Laggi System: Why It Varies?

Bihar mein land measurement ka ek aur pehlu hai 'Laggi'. Laggi ek baans (bamboo) ka danda hota hai jiske hisaab se Dhur ka size tay hota hai. Standard Bihar survey mein 5.5 haath ki laggi ka istemal hota hai. Lekin kuch districts mein yeh 4 haath ya 6 haath ki bhi ho sakti hai. Agar laggi ki lambai badal jaye, toh aapka Kattha aur Dhur ka area bhi badal jayega. Isliye humesha apne registry paper par laggi ki lambai zaroor check karein.

5. Step-by-Step Calculation Guide

Agar aapke paas zameen ka naap (Length and Width) hai, toh aap Bihar ke units mein aise convert kar sakte hain:

  • Step 1: Zameen ki total Square Feet nikaalein (Length x Width).
  • Step 2: Agar Square Feet 27,225 se zyada hai, toh use 27,225 se divide karein. Jo result aayega wo Bigha hoga.
  • Step 3: Remaining area ko 1,361.25 se divide karein, wo Kattha hoga.
  • Step 4: Bache hue hisse ko 68.06 se divide karein, wo Dhur hoga.

6. Legal Documents to Verify in Bihar

Sirf zameen naapna kafi nahi hai, Bihar mein registry se pehle ye dastawez zaroor check karein:

  1. Khatiyan: Yeh zameen ka sabse purana record hota hai jisme vanshavali ki jankari milti hai.
  2. Lagan Rasid: Sarkar ko diye gaye tax ki receipt, jo ownership proof ke liye zaroori hai.
  3. Dakhil-Kharij (Mutation): Zameen apne naam karwane ki sabse mahatvapurna prakriya.

7. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Bihar mein 1 bigha mein kitna decimal hota hai?
Ans: Bihar mein 1 Bigha lagbhag 62.5 Decimal ke barabar hota hai. 1 Decimal = 435.6 sq. ft.
Q2: Kya Bihar mein sabhi jagah 20 Kattha ka bigha hota hai?
Ans: Haan, Bihar ka standard yahi hai. Lekin Kattha ka square feet area laggi ke badalne se badal sakta hai.
Q3: 1 Acre mein kitna bigha hota hai Bihar mein?
Ans: 1 Acre barabar 1.6 Bigha (Pucca) hota hai.
