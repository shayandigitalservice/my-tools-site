1. Introduction: The Legacy of Land in Bihar

Bihar, jo Bharat ke itihas mein ek pramukh sthan rakhta hai, wahan bhoomi (land) hamesha se shakti aur sampatti ka prateek rahi hai. Bihar mein zameen ki naap-jokh ke liye aaj bhi vahi purane aur bharosemand units ka istemal hota hai jo British kal se chale aa rahe hain. Jab hum Bihar ke dehati ya shehri ilakon mein zameen kharidne ki baat karte hain, toh Bigha, Kattha, aur Dhur jaise shabd sabse zyada sunne ko milte hain.

2. Understanding Bihar's Local Units: The B-K-D System

Bihar mein land measurement mukhya roop se teen units par tiki hai. Inhe samajhna isliye zaroori hai kyunki Bihar Sarkar ke Bhulekh (Land Records) portal par sara data isi format mein hota hai.

1. What is 1 Bigha in Bihar?

Bihar mein 1 Bigha lagbhag 27,225 Square Feet hota hai. Agar hum ise meter mein badlein, toh yeh 2,530 Square Meters ke barabar hai. Yeh dhyaan dena zaroori hai ki Bihar ka Bigha "Pucca Bigha" ki category mein aata hai, jo kafi vishal hota hai.

2. The Power of 20 Kattha

Aksar log puchte hain—"Bihar mein 1 bigha mein kitne kattha hote hain?" Bihar mein standard system ke hisaab se 1 Bigha = 20 Kattha hota hai. Har kattha lagbhag 1,361.25 sq. ft. ka hota hai. Patna aur uske aas-paas ke shehron mein zameen aksar Kattha ke bhav se hi bechi jati hai.
