1. Introduction: Understanding Bengal's Land Records

West Bengal, jo apni kala aur sanskriti ke liye duniya bhar mein mashhoor hai, wahan zameen ki naap-jokh ka system kaafi satik (accurate) aur purana hai. Bengal mein zameen ko mukhya roop se Bigha, Katha, aur Chatak mein naapa jata hai. Jab aap Kolkata, Siliguri, ya Durgapur mein property ki baat karte hain, toh yahi units use hoti hain.

2. The Core Units: Bigha, Katha, and Chatak

West Bengal mein zameen ki calculation India ke baaki rajyon se thodi alag hai. Niche iska breakdown diya gaya hai:

1. What is 1 Bigha in West Bengal?

West Bengal mein 1 Bigha barabar 14,400 Square Feet hota hai. Agar aapne Assam Land Calculator use kiya hai, toh aapko pata hoga ki Assam ka Bigha bhi itna hi hota hai. Lekin divisions mein antar hai.

2. The Katha System (720 Sq. Ft.)

Bengal mein 1 Bigha = 20 Katha hota hai. Ek Katha ka area 720 Square Feet hota hai. Yeh unit ghar banwane wale plots ke liye sabse zyada lokpriya hai.

3. Chatak: The Unique Unit

West Bengal ki sabse khas unit Chatak hai. 1 Katha mein 16 Chatak hote hain. Iska matlab hai ki 1 Chatak barabar 45 Square Feet hota hai ($720 / 16$). Chote dukaan ya micro-plots ki registry Chatak mein hi hoti hai.
