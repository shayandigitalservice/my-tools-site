West Bengal Land Measurement Units (Standard Table)

West Bengal mein 1 Bigha barabar 14,400 Square Feet hota hai. Niche di gayi table se aap asani se units ko samajh sakte hain:

Unit Name Area (Sq. Ft.) Logic
1 Chatak45 sq. ft.Smallest traditional standard unit blueprint
1 Katha720 sq. ft.16 Chatak = 1 Katha Structural Grid
1 Bigha14,400 sq. ft.20 Katha = 1 Bigha Standard Block
1 Acre43,560 sq. ft.3.025 Bigha (WB Standard Ratio)

Example: 10 Katha Zameen ki Naap

Maan lijiye aapne 7,200 square feet ka ek plot dekha hai. West Bengal ke hisaab se iska conversion aise hoga:

Total Area = 7,200 sq. ft.
Calculation = 7,200 ÷ 720 (1 Katha) = 10 Katha.
Iska matlab hai ki aapka plot poora 10 Katha ka hai, jo ki aadhe (0.5) Bigha ke barabar hai.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: West Bengal mein 1 Katha mein kitne square feet hote hain?

Ans: WB mein 1 Katha barabar 720 Square Feet hota hai.

Q2: 1 Katha mein kitne Chatak hote hain?

Ans: West Bengal mein 1 Katha mein total 16 Chatak hote hain (Each chatak size is equal to 45 Sq.Ft spatial block size).
