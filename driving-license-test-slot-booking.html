Driving License Test Slot Booking Online 2026

Step by Step Official RTO Guide

Driving license test ke liye online slot book karna ab bahut easy hai. Agar aap learner license ya permanent driving license ke test ke liye slot book karna chahte ho, to ye guide step by step aapko help karegi.

Driving License Test Slot Booking Kya Hai

Driving license test slot booking ek official process hai jisme aap RTO portal se apne preferred date aur time ka driving test slot book karte ho. Isse aapko RTO office me wait nahi karna padta aur test hassle-free hota hai.

Required Details / Documents

  • Learner / Existing License Number (agar Permanent DL ke liye)
  • Registration Number (vehicle, if applicable)
  • Applicant Name, DOB aur Address Proof
  • Mobile Number linked with Aadhaar
  • Payment method (Debit/Credit/UPI) for fee
  • Passport size photo (optional)

Step 1 – RTO Official Portal Open Kare

Browser me Parivahan Sewa Official Portal open kare. Ye Government of India ka official portal hai for all RTO services.

Step 2 – Login / Registration Kare

“Online Services → Driving License” section me login kare. Agar pehli baar hai, to “New User Registration” kare aur registered mobile number OTP verify kare.

Step 3 – Driving License Test Type Select Kare

  • Learner License Test Slot – Agar nayi learner license ke liye test dena hai
  • Permanant DL Test Slot – Agar learner se permanent license convert karna hai

Step 4 – Slot Booking Steps

  1. “Slot Booking / Appointment” option select kare
  2. RTO office select kare apne district/state ke liye
  3. Available date aur time slot choose kare
  4. Required documents aur learner/permanent license number enter kare
  5. Submit details aur proceed to payment (if applicable)

Step 5 – OTP / Payment Verification

Registered mobile number pe OTP aayega, usse verify kare. Online fee payment karna ho to payment gateway se complete kare.

Step 6 – Slot Confirmation Aur Print

Booking complete hone ke baad confirmation page aayega. Iska PDF print kare ya mobile me save kare. Ye aapko test ke din RTO me dikhana hoga.

Important Tips

  • Official RTO portal hi use kare
  • Registered mobile number OTP ke liye active rakhe
  • Documents aur learner license number accurate hone chahiye
  • Slot cancel / reschedule karna ho to portal se hi kare
  • Test ke din slot confirmation PDF ya mobile screenshot saath leke jaaye

Benefits of Online Slot Booking

  • RTO office me wait nahi karna padta
  • Fast and hassle-free official process
  • 24/7 online booking facility
  • Slot reschedule possible in case of emergency
  • Official confirmation PDF available for reference

Conclusion

Driving license test slot booking online karna ab easy aur official process hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apna slot easily book kar sakte ho aur test hassle-free de sakte ho.
