EPF Balance Check Karne Ka Complete Guide

India me private job karne wale employees ke liye PF yani Provident Fund ek important savings scheme hai. Har month salary ka kuch part PF account me deposit hota hai.

Agar aap job karte ho to aapko apna PF balance check karna zaruri hota hai. Good news ye hai ki ab aap mobile phone se hi PF balance check kar sakte ho.

PF Balance Kya Hota Hai

PF balance wo amount hota hai jo employee aur employer dono milkar EPF account me deposit karte hain.
