3. Landmark Identifiers in Haryana Fard: Khewat, Khatauni, and Khasra

Zameen ka absolute mathematical layout areas nikaalne ke alawa dastawez me likhe teen administrative identity elements ko clear karna behad zaroori hai. Haryana digital database me har ek land entry in teen pillars par rest karti hai:

A. Khewat Number (खेवट नंबर): Ise ownership identity ledger kehte hain. Yeh number un sabhi owners (sah-maalik) ke pure collective panel ya single individual ownership record ko track karta hai jinke naam par wo land holding registry me registered hoti hai. Jab kisi parivar me partition deed execute hoti hai ya share sell out kiya jata hai, toh circle inspector is Khewat number ko automatic modify kar deta hai.

B. Khatauni Number (खतौनी नंबर): Yeh operational cultivation ledger hota hai. Is identification code se revenue inspector pata lagate hain ki us physical plot asset par actually kheti ya occupancy kiski hai. Agar maalik swayam wahan operations manage kar raha hai toh wahan technical terms me 'Khud Kasht' darj milta hai, anyatha leased tenants ka description is Khatauni section me rest karta hai.

C. Khasra Number (खसरा नंबर): Yeh numerical identifier sabse primary physical core hai. State boundary maps (Sajra Map) par har ek dynamic geographic piece ko ek clear identity serial code diya jata hai jise Khasra number kehte hain. Is serial reference key ke bina aap zameen ki physical ground position ya real boundary corners locate nahi kar sakte.
