2. The Complex Math of Vigha and Guntha Explained

Zameen ke map (survey system) ko samajhne ke liye hume Gujarat ke local parameters ko gaur se dekhna hoga. Gujarat me do tarah ke Vigha ka prayog purane samay se chala aa raha hai - Chota Vigha aur Bada Vigha. Lekin jab hum official revenue computations, land records ya bank loan files ki baat karte hain, toh hamesha Bada Vigha (Standard Government Vigha) ka hi istemal standard mana jata hai.

Standard rule ke anusar:
1 Vigha = 24 Guntha
Iska matlab hai ki agar aapke paas do alag plots hain; pehla plot 1 Vigha 18 Guntha ka hai aur doosra plot 2 Vigha 12 Guntha ka hai, toh unka total jod karte waqt 18 + 12 = 30 Guntha hota hai. Ab chunki 24 Guntha milkar 1 poora Vigha banate hain, isliye 30 Guntha me se 24 Guntha aage forward ho jayenge aur residual balance ke roop me sirf 6 Guntha bachega. Total answer aayega 4 Vigha 6 Guntha. Yeh advanced mechanism hamara calculator automatic back-end standard control node me perform karta hai.

Step 2: Carry-Forward Engine Conversion: 47 Guntha ko jab hum 24 se divide karenge, toh hamare paas 1 Full Vigha nikal kar aayega aur bache huye remaining counts honge 23 Guntha. (47 - 24 = 23).

Step 3: Vigha Ka Master Sum: 3 (Plot A) + 1 (Plot B) + 4 (Plot C) + 1 (Jo carry forward se aaya) = 9 Vigha.
