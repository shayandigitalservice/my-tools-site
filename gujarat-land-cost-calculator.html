3. Important Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Gujarat me 1 Vigha me kitne square yards ya var hote hain?
Ans: Gujarat revenue ledger maps ke anusar 1 traditional Vigha exact 2,844 Square Yards (ખેતીલાયક વાર) કે બરાબર હોતા હૈ.

Q2. Kya mahilaon (Female buyers) ke liye Gujarat me registry me relaxation milta hai?
Ans: Haan, mahila khariddaaro ke liye 1% ki poori Registration Fee exempt (maaf) hoti hai, unhe sirf 4.9% stamp duty deni padti hai.

Q3. Jantara rate kya hota hai?
Ans: Jantara government dwara set ki gayi minimum legal guideline circle rate list hoti hai jisse kam par property document generate nahi ho sakta.
