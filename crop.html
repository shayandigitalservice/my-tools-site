About Image Crop Tool

The Image Crop Tool allows you to quickly cut unwanted parts from a photo and focus on the most important area of an image. Cropping images is commonly used for profile pictures, social media posts, website graphics, and product images. With this free online image cropper, you can easily upload a photo, select the desired crop area, and download the final cropped image instantly.

This tool works directly in your browser using modern web technology. That means your images are processed locally on your device, ensuring faster performance and better privacy. Whether you are editing a profile picture, preparing images for a blog, or adjusting product photos for an online store, this simple tool helps you crop photos within seconds.

How to Use the Image Crop Tool
