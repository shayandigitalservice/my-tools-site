Jan Dhan Account Online Activate Kaise Kare 2026

Step by Step Official Guide

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) ke under, har citizen ko bank account open karne ka option diya gaya hai. Agar aapne Jan Dhan account khola hai lekin activate nahi kiya, to ye guide aapko step by step online activate karne me help karegi.

Jan Dhan Account Activation Kya Hai

Activation matlab ki aapka account fully operational ho gaya hai aur aap transactions kar sakte hain, including ATM withdrawal, UPI transfer, direct benefit transfer (DBT) aur online payments.

Required Documents

  • Bank passbook ya account number
  • Registered mobile number linked with Jan Dhan account
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID)
  • Address Proof (Aadhaar, Ration Card, Voter ID)
  • Debit Card / ATM card (agar issue hua hai)

Jan Dhan Account Online Activate Kaise Kare

Step 1 – Official Bank Portal Open Kare

Apni bank ki official website ya app open kare. Jan Dhan account mostly SBI, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank ya Axis Bank me hota hai.

Step 2 – Account Login / Customer Section

Login kare ya “Activate Account” option select kare. Agar app hai to “Services → Activate Account” select kare.

Step 3 – Enter Required Details

Account number, registered mobile number, identity & address proof details enter kare. OTP verification complete kare.

Step 4 – Set MPIN / UPI

ATM / Debit card ke liye MPIN set kare. UPI ke liye linked mobile number se registration complete kare.

Step 5 – Confirmation

Submit karte hi aapke Jan Dhan account activate ho jayega. Bank confirmation SMS ya email receive hoga.

Important Tips

  • Official bank app ya website hi use kare
  • OTP aur MPIN details safe rakhe
  • Mobile number update nahi hai to pehle update kare
  • Account activate karte hi debit card aur UPI activate kar le

Benefits of Activating Jan Dhan Account

  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) receive kar sakte hain
  • ATM withdrawal aur online payment easy
  • UPI transactions possible
  • Banking services fully operational

Conclusion

Jan Dhan account online activate karna official bank portal ya app se easy hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apna account instantly activate kar sakte hain aur sari banking services ka use kar sakte hain.
