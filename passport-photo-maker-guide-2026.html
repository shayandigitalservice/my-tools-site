Mobile se Photo Banane ka Step-by-Step Tarika

  1. Sabse pehle hamare Passport Photo Maker page par jayein.
  2. Apni ek saaf (clear) photo upload karein jisme aapka chehra seedha ho.
  3. Hamara tool apne aap background remove kar dega aur standard blue/white background laga dega.
  4. Aap standard sizes (Jaise 3.5cm x 4.5cm) select kar sakte hain jo sabhi forms mein chalta hai.
  5. "Download" par click karein aur aapki professional photo ready hai!

Khaas Tips Sahi Photo ke Liye:

  • Lighting: Photo hamesha achhi roshni mein khinchein taaki chehra saaf dikhe.
  • Pose: Seedha dekhein aur camera ke level par rahein.
  • Background: Agar peeche diwar (wall) plane ho toh results aur bhi behtar aate hain.
