Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. MP mein zameen ki registry par mahilaon ko kya discount milta hai?
Ans: Madhya Pradesh government female empowerment ko badhava dene ke liye mahilaon ko base stamp duty par 1% ki standard relaxation pradan karti hai.

Q2. Kya MP mein registration fee ki koi maximum limit hai?
Ans: Nahi, MP revenue rules ke mutabik registry panjiyan fees hamesha property cost ka flat 3% hoti hai, isme koi upper capping nahi hai.

Q3. MP mein 1 Bigha ka size kya mana jata hai?
Ans: Sarkari registries aur Bhulekh records ke liye MP Revenue Department standard **27,225 Square Feet** ka Bigha paimana hi manya karta hai.
