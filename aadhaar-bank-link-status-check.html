Aadhaar Bank Link Status Check 2026

Direct Official Method (Working Guide)

👉 Aadhaar Bank Link Status Check Kare
Quick Info:
• Process Time: 2-5 minutes
• Required: Aadhaar + Mobile OTP
• Mode: Online (Official UIDAI Portal)

Aadhaar Bank Link Status Kya Hai

Aadhaar bank link status se aap check kar sakte ho ki aapka Aadhaar number bank account se linked hai ya nahi. Ye DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) ke liye zaruri hota hai.

Step by Step Aadhaar Link Status Check

Step 1 – Upar Diye Gaye Button Par Click Kare

Direct official UIDAI website open ho jayegi.

Step 2 – Aadhaar Number Enter Kare

Apna 12 digit Aadhaar number enter kare.

Step 3 – OTP Verification

Registered mobile par OTP aayega, use enter kare.

Step 4 – Status Check Kare

Aapka bank linking status screen par show hoga.

Status Ka Matlab Kya Hota Hai

Active

Aapka Aadhaar bank se linked hai aur DBT ready hai.

Inactive

Aadhaar bank se linked nahi hai.

Last Bank Name

Jo bank Aadhaar se linked hai wo show hota hai.

Agar Link Nahi Hai To Kya Kare

1. Bank Branch Visit Kare

Apne bank me Aadhaar link karwaye.

2. ATM / Online Banking

Kuch banks online linking allow karte hain.

3. Form Fill Kare

Aadhaar seeding form bank me submit kare.

Important Tips

  • Mobile number Aadhaar se linked hona chahiye
  • OTP kisi ke sath share na kare
  • Sirf official website use kare
  • Details sahi enter kare

Conclusion

Ab aap easily Aadhaar bank link status check kar sakte ho. Upar diya gaya button direct official website open karta hai jahan se aap apna status turant check kar sakte ho.
