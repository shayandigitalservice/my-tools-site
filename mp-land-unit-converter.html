How to use MP Unit Converter? (उपयोग करने का तरीका)

  1. Apne zameen ke dastawezo (Bhulekh) ke hisab se Bigha, Katha aur Decimal boxes mein bhariye.
  2. Agar aapke paas sirf ek hi unit hai, toh baaki boxes ko khali chod dein.
  3. "Convert Units Now" button par click karein.
  4. Tool turant aapko Sq Ft, Acre aur Hectare ka sateek breakdown dikha dega.
