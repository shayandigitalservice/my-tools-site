Introduction

Bihar mein zameen ki paimaish (measurement) ke liye sadiyon se local units ka upyog hota aa raha hai. Bharat ke anya rajyon ki tulna mein Bihar ka land measurement system thoda alag hai, kyunki yahan "Lagga" (Standard Pole) ki lambai har kuch kos par badal sakti hai.

Hamara Bihar Land Unit Converter Shayan Smart Tools dwara develop kiya gaya hai. Yeh tool Bihar ke sabse prachalit 5.5 Haath ke Lagga standard par aadharit hai, jo ki Bihar ke adhiktat Revenue records mein istemal hota hai.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Bihar mein 1 Kattha mein kitne Dhur hote hain?
Ans: Bihar ke har zile mein 1 Kattha hamesha 20 Dhur ke barabar hota hai.
Q2. Lagga kya hota hai?
Ans: Lagga ek purana paimana hai jo zameen napne ke liye pole ki tarah use hota hai. Bihar mein 5.5 haath ka lagga sabse prachalit hai.
Q3. 1 Bigha mein kitne Square Feet hote hain?
Ans: Bihar standard ke anusar 1 Bigha 27,225 Sq Ft hota hai.
Q4. Kya ye calculator mobile par chalta hai?
Ans: Ji haan, ye tool fully responsive hai aur mobile browsers par smoothly kaam karta hai.
Q5. Acre to Bigha conversion kya hai?
