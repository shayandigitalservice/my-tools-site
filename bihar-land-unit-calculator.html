Bihar Land Measurement Guide

Bihar mein zameen ki maap ke liye Bigha, Kattha, aur Dhur sabse prachalit units hain. Bihar ka system thoda complex hota hai kyunki yahan zameen ki maap "Lagga" (pole length) par nirbhar karti hai. Humne is calculator mein Bihar ke standard 5.5 haath ke lagga ka upyog kiya hai, jo adhiktat zilon mein manya hai.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Bihar mein 1 Kattha mein kitna square feet hota hai?
Ans: Standard 5.5 haath ke lagga ke hisaab se 1 Kattha 1,361.25 Sq Ft hota hai.

Q2. Kya Bihar ke har zile mein Bigha ki value same hai?
Ans: Nahi, Lagga ki lambai badalne se value badal sakti hai. Kripya apne sthaniye ameen se sampark karein.
