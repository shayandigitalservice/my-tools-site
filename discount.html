What is a Discount Calculator?

A discount calculator is an online tool used to calculate the reduced price of a product after applying a discount percentage. It also shows how much money you save.

  • Original Price – The actual price of the product
  • Discount Percentage – The percentage reduction
  • Final Price – Price after discount

How to Use Discount Calculator

  1. Enter the original price of the product
  2. Enter the discount percentage
  3. Click on calculate button
  4. View discount amount and final price instantly

The tool is designed for simplicity and works on all devices including mobile phones and desktops.

Discount Formula Explained

Discount Amount Formula

Discount = (Original Price × Discount %) ÷ 100

Final Price Formula

Final Price = Original Price – Discount Amount
