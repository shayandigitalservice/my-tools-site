Why Bengal's Logic is Different?

Bihar aur UP mein 20 Dhur ka 1 Katha hota hai, lekin West Bengal mein **16 Chatak ka 1 Katha** mana jata hai. Isliye agar aap kisi dusre state ka calculator use karenge toh result galat aayega. Humara tool Bengal ke official revenue rules ko follow karta hai, jisme 1 Bigha 14,400 Square Feet ke barabar hota hai.

5 Practical Examples: WB Land Logic

  1. Ex 1: 10 Katha + 12 Katha = **1 Bigha 2 Katha** (20 Katha = 1 Bigha).
  2. Ex 2: 12 Chatak + 6 Chatak = **1 Katha 2 Chatak** (16 Chatak = 1 Katha).
  3. Ex 3: 1 Bigha 15 Katha + 10 Katha = **2 Bigha 5 Katha**.
  4. Ex 4: 8 Chatak + 8 Chatak = **1 Katha 0 Chatak**.
  5. Ex 5: 2 Bigha 19 Katha 15 Chatak + 1 Chatak = **3 Bigha**.
