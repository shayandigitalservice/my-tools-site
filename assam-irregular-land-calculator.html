Assam Irregular Land Calculation: Tedi-Medi Zameen Kaise Naapein?

Doston, Assam mein zameen hamesha ekdam square nahi hoti. Pahari ilaka aur nadiyon ki wajah se khet aksar tedi-medi (Irregular shapes) mein hote hain. Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **Assam Irregular Land Area Calculator** Heron’s Formula ka use karke ekdam accurate results deta hai.

How to Use: Step-by-Step Guide

1. Apne khet ke chaaro sides (A, B, C, D) ko feet mein naapein.
2. Beechon-beech ek tirchi line (Diagonal) naapein.
