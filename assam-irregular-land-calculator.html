2. Technical Core: The Mathematics of Diagonal Division and Heron's Formula

Tedi-medi 4-konne wali zameen ka sateek blueprint area map calculate karne ka sabse ancient aur 100% scientifically certified path banta hai pure asset space ko ek corner to corner line use karke do clear triangles me divide kar dena. Is intersection center line ko legal metric terms me hum Diagonal / Tirchi Line (কর্ণ) bolte hain. Jaise hi aap nakshe ya field maps coordinates par diagonal track complete kar lete hain, complex four-sided entity layout do individual operational triangles sections me divide ho jati hai. Geometry laws ke criteria me jab dono individual triangles ki complete dimensions (Side A, Side B, aur common diagonal Side E) sateek mil jayein, toh double processing loops me Heron’s Formula apply kiya jata hai:

$$s_1 = \frac{a + b + \text{diag}}{2}, \quad \text{Area}_1 = \sqrt{s_1(s_1 - a)(s_1 - b)(s_1 - \text{diag})}$$

$$s_2 = \frac{c + d + \text{diag}}{2}, \quad \text{Area}_2 = \sqrt{s_2(s_2 - c)(s_2 - d)(s_2 - \text{diag})}$$
