About PDF Compressor

This free online PDF compressor tool helps you reduce the size of large PDF files quickly and easily. Many PDF documents can become very large, especially when they contain images, graphics, or scanned pages. Large files are difficult to upload, share, or send through email. With this PDF compression tool, you can reduce file size directly in your browser without installing any software.

The tool works entirely on your device, meaning your files are not uploaded to any external server. This makes the compression process fast and secure while protecting your privacy. It is ideal for students, professionals, and anyone who needs to optimize PDF documents for easier sharing.

How to Use This Tool

Using this PDF compressor is very simple. First, upload the PDF file from your device. Next, enter your desired output size in KB or MB. After that, click the compress button and the tool will process your document. Once compression is complete, you can download the optimized PDF file instantly.

Features of the Tool

This online PDF compressor includes several helpful features. It works directly in your web browser and does not require installation. The interface is simple and mobile friendly so it works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. It also processes files locally, keeping your data safe and private.
