Getting Started with Udhaar Manager PRO

Follow these simple steps to start managing your customer credit efficiently:

  1. Enter your shop name, address, and phone number and click Save Shop.
  2. Enter a customer’s name and mobile number and click Add Customer.
  3. Select a customer to view their profile, total udhaar, paid amount, and outstanding balance.
  4. Enter the transaction amount and choose Add Udhaar or Add Payment to update records.
  5. Click Print to generate a professional receipt for the transaction.
  6. Use WhatsApp button to send payment reminders instantly.
  7. Filter customer history by today, last week, or last month for detailed tracking.
  8. Click Download All Data to export records as an Excel file for bookkeeping.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I print receipts on any printer?

Yes, receipts are printable on standard printers or POS receipt printers in suitable formats.

Is WhatsApp reminder secure?

Yes, reminders are sent via WhatsApp link and do not expose sensitive data publicly.

Can I track daily, weekly, or monthly transactions?

Absolutely. Use the filter options in customer profile to see transactions for today, last week, or current month.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is needed. Udhaar Manager PRO runs entirely in your browser.
