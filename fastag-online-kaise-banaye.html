FASTag Online Kaise Banaye 2026

Complete Step by Step Guide

FASTag ek electronic toll collection system hai jo India ke highways par toll payment ko easy aur fast banata hai. Agar aap car ya vehicle use karte ho to FASTag lagana mandatory hai.

Is article me hum aapko batayenge ki **FASTag online kaise banaye**, kaun se documents chahiye aur kaise apply kare.

FASTag Kya Hai?

FASTag ek RFID technology based sticker hai jo vehicle ke windshield par lagaya jata hai. Jab vehicle toll plaza cross karta hai to automatically payment deduct ho jata hai.

FASTag Ke Benefits

  • Toll par rukna nahi padta
  • Fuel saving hoti hai
  • Online payment automatic hota hai
  • SMS alert milta hai
  • Cash carry karne ki jarurat nahi

FASTag Banane Ke Liye Documents

  • Vehicle RC
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport size photo
  • Mobile number

FASTag Online Kaise Banaye

Step 1

FASTag ke liye official website open kare:

NHAI Official Website

Step 2

FASTag apply option par click kare aur apna account create kare.

Step 3

Personal details fill kare:

  • Name
  • Mobile number
  • Vehicle number
  • Address

Step 4

Required documents upload kare.

Step 5

Online payment kare aur application submit kare.

FASTag Bank Se Kaise Banaye

India me kai banks FASTag provide karte hain. Example:
  • HDFC Bank
  • ICICI Bank
  • SBI Bank
  • Axis Bank
Bank website se bhi FASTag apply kar sakte hain.

FASTag Paytm Se Kaise Banaye

Paytm app se bhi FASTag apply kar sakte hain. Steps:
  • Paytm app open kare
  • FASTag option select kare
  • Vehicle details enter kare
  • Payment complete kare

FASTag Balance Kaise Check Kare

FASTag balance check karne ke liye:
  • Bank app open kare
  • FASTag wallet check kare
  • SMS alerts check kare

FASTag Recharge Kaise Kare

FASTag recharge karne ke options:
  • UPI
  • Debit card
  • Net banking
  • Wallet

Conclusion

FASTag online banana bahut easy hai aur highway travel ko fast aur convenient bana deta hai. Agar aapke vehicle par FASTag nahi hai to jaldi apply kare.
