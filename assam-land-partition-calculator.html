Assam Land Partition: Sateek Batwara aur Sahi Hisab

Assam mein zameen ka batwara (মাটিৰ ভাগ কৰা) hamesha se ek mahatvapurna vishay raha hai. Assam Dharitree records mein zameen aksar Hectare mein hoti hai, lekin parivarik batwara hamesha Bigha, Katha aur Lessa mein kiya jata hai. **Shayan Smart Tools** ka ye **Assam Mixed Land Partition Calculator** vishesh roop se Assam ke sarkari standards (1 Bigha = 14,400 Sq Ft) par adharit hai.

How to Use (ব্যৱহাৰৰ নিয়ম)

  1. Apne zameen ke kaagazat ke anusar Bigha, Katha, Acre ya Hectare sahi box mein bhariye.
  2. Hissedaro (Shares) ki kul sankhya enter karein.
  3. "Divide Land Now" par click karte hi sateek hisab aapke screen par hoga.
  4. Result ko family ke saath share karne ke liye WhatsApp button ka use karein.

5 Practical Examples of Assam Land Partition

1. **Joint Family:** 1 Bigha aur 10 Lessa zameen ko 2 bhaiyon mein barabar baantna.
2. **Registry Check:** 1 Hectare zameen ko 4 hissedaro mein Bigha-Katha tak sateek divide karna.
3. **Small Plot:** 1 Katha zameen ko 3 logo mein Lessa tak barabar baantna.
4. **Mixed Units:** Acre aur Bigha ko jod kar hissedaro mein divide karna.
5. **Industrial Land:** Badi zameen ko Sq Ft aur Bigha ke mix hisab se partition karna.
