FAQs: Tamil Nadu Land Records

Q1. Tamil Nadu mein 1 Ground kitne square feet ka hota hai?
Ans: Tamil Nadu mein standard 1 Ground **2,400 Square Feet** ka hota hai.

Q2. 1 Cent mein kitne square feet hote hain?
Ans: 1 Cent mein sateek **435.6 Square Feet** hote hain.

Q3. 1 Acre mein kitne Ground hote hain?
Ans: 1 Acre mein lagbhag **18.15 Ground** hote hain.

Q4. Ankanam unit kahan use hoti hai?
Ans: Ankanam mukhya roop se Nellore aur AP border se lage TN ke ilakon mein use hoti hai (1 Ankanam = 72 sq ft).
