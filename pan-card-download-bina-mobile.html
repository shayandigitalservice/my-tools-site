PAN Card Kaise Download Kare Bina Registered Mobile Number 2026

Aaj ke digital zamane me PAN Card ek bohot important document hai. Lekin agar aapka registered mobile number change ho gaya hai ya available nahi hai, to bhi aap easily apna PAN Card online download kar sakte ho. Ye step by step Hinglish guide aapko puri process batayegi.

Important: Apne PAN number aur Date of Birth ready rakhein. Aadhaar linked email ya number use kar ke OTP verify kar sakte hain.

Step by Step PAN Card Download

  1. 1 Visit kare NSDL PAN Service website.
  2. 2 “Download PAN” option par click kare.
  3. 3 Form me apna PAN Number, Full Name, Date of Birth enter kare.
  4. 4 “I don’t have registered mobile number” checkbox/option select kare.
  5. 5 Aadhaar linked email/mobile par aaya hua OTP enter karke verify kare.
  6. 6 OTP verify hone ke baad PAN card PDF download kare.
  7. 7 PDF ko apne device me save kare future ke liye.

Important Tips

  • Aadhaar linked email/mobile number ka use kare.
  • Form me exact spelling aur date enter kare.
  • Download karne ke baad soft copy aur print copy dono rakhe.

Common Problems

  • OTP nahi aata? → Aadhaar linked mobile/email dubara check kare.
  • Incorrect details? → DOB/Name carefully verify kare.
  • Website slow hai? → Kuch time baad dubara try kare.
