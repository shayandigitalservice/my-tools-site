Privacy Policy

Transparency and security are our top priorities. Learn how we safeguard your digital information.

H2: 1. Introduction to Our Privacy Standards

At Shayan Smart Tools, we are committed to maintaining the highest level of privacy and security for our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, handle, and protect your data when you use our 70+ free online utilities, calculators, and PDF services.

We operate from Assam, India, and our platform is designed to comply with global data protection standards, including the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and the Information Technology Act, 2000 of India.

By accessing Shayan Smart Tools, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. We ensure that your "Privacy by Design" is never compromised.

H2: 2. Information Collection & Zero-Storage Policy

One of the core features of Shayan Smart Tools is our Zero-Storage Commitment. For most of our digital tools, such as the PDF Compressor, Image Resizer, and Background Remover, we utilize "Client-Side Processing."

H3: Non-Personal Data Collection

We may collect non-personal data such as browser type, operating system, IP addresses (anonymized), and the time of your visit. This data is used solely for analyzing website performance and improving tool efficiency.

H3: Personal Information

We do not require users to register or provide personal information to use our tools. Personal data is only collected if you voluntarily contact us via email, where we may store your name and email address to respond to your inquiry.

H2: 3. Use of Cookies and Web Beacons

Shayan Smart Tools uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files that allow us to remember your preferences and recognize you on return visits. These cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information (PII).

H3: Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

To keep our platform 100% free, we display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (like the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visit to Shayan Smart Tools and other sites on the internet.

Users may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy. You can also manage your cookie preferences through your individual browser settings.

H2: 4. Data Security and Encryption

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Our website uses SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption to ensure that all data transmitted between your browser and our platform remains encrypted and private.

While we strive for absolute security, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. However, since most of our tools process files locally in your browser, your risk is significantly minimized.

H2: 5. Children's Privacy Protection

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), Shayan Smart Tools does not knowingly collect or solicit any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our tools are designed for a general audience. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal info, we will delete it immediately.

H2: 6. External Links & Third-Party Sites

Our website may contain links to other third-party websites. Please be aware that Shayan Smart Tools is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each website that collects personally identifiable information.

We reserve the right to update this policy to reflect changes in our services or legal obligations (such as updated 2026 IT rules). Any changes will be updated on this page with an "Effective Date." We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
