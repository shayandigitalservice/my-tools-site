Madhya Pradesh Land Measurement: Complete Guide 2026

Madhya Pradesh (MP) mein zameen ki mapi (Land Measurement) ke liye vividh tareeke apnaye jate hain. MP ke kuch hisso mein (jaise Gwalior division) UP se milta-julta Bigha chalta hai, wahi Indore aur Bhopal division mein standard sarkari mapi Hectare aur Acre mein hoti hai. FAQs: Madhya Pradesh Land Records & Bhulekh

Q1: MP mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Katha hote hain?
Ans: Madhya Pradesh ke adhiktar districts mein 1 Bigha mein 20 Katha (Biswa) hote hain.

Q2: Decimal unit ka kya upyog hai?
Ans: Decimal unit ka use chote plots ya residential area ki mapi ke liye hota hai. 100 Decimal milkar 1 Acre bante hain.

Q3: Kya MP mein Bigha ka size badalta hai?
Ans: Haan, local riwaaj ke hisaab se kacha aur pakka bigha ho sakta hai, lekin sarkari registry mein Acre/Hectare hi valid hai.

Q4: 1 Hectare mein kitne Bigha hote hain?
Ans: Standard mapi ke hisaab se 1 Hectare mein lagbhag 3.95 Bigha hote hain.
