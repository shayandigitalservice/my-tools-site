5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Odisha Land Registry & Stamp Duty

Q1. Odisha me land stamp duty kitni hai?
Ans: Odisha me male/general buyers ke liye stamp duty 5%, female ke liye 4%, aur joint registration par 4.5% lagti hai.

Q2. Odisha land registry fees kitni percent hoti hai?
Ans: Odisha government flat 2% registration fee charge karti hai, jisme koi maximum limit (capping) nahi hoti.

Q3. Commercial property par Odisha me kitna stamp duty tax lagta hai?
Ans: Commercial lands/properties par regular benchmark rate ke upar +1% additional surcharge loading lagti hai.

Q4. Bhulekh Odisha land calculation units kya hain?
Ans: Bhulekh record maps me zameen ko mainly Acre aur Decimal parameters par hi track kiya jata hai. 1 Acre me 100 Decimal hote hain.

Q5. Odisha benchmark circle rate online kaise check kare?
Ans: Odisha government ke official IGR portal (igrodisha.gov.in) par jakar 'Guidelines Register Value' option se kisi bhi mouza/village ka benchmark rate check kar sakte hain.
