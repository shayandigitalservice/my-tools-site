Odisha Land Registry Legal Framework FAQs

What are the precise stamp duty concessions for women in Odisha?

To promote female property asset ownership, Odisha offers a 1% concession. Women pay 4% Stamp Duty, whereas male individual buyers pay 5%. Joint registration (Male + Female) is assessed at 4.5%.

Is there any upper cap on Registration Fees in Odisha?

No. Unlike some other states that put a monetary limit on registration fees, Odisha charges a flat 2% of the absolute market/benchmark value without any upper capping limit.

How is land measured and converted across Odisha tehsils?

Traditionally, Odisha land records (Bhulekh) leverage Acres and Decimals. 1 Acre is exactly equivalent to 100 Decimals, or 43,560 square feet. Hectare is predominantly used for massive agricultural or rural evaluation metrics.

What is the legal remedy if a property is undervalued?

Under Section 47-A of the Indian Stamp Act (Odisha Amendment), any document suspected of undervaluation is flagged and referred to the District Collector for correct valuation adjustment and collection of deficit penalties.
