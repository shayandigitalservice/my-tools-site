About This PPF Calculator

This free PPF calculator helps you estimate the maturity value of your Public Provident Fund investment. The Public Provident Fund (PPF) is one of the most popular long-term savings schemes in India backed by the government. It offers safe investment options, attractive interest rates, and tax benefits under Section 80C.

Using this calculator, you can quickly estimate how much your yearly investment will grow over time. It automatically calculates the maturity amount based on the investment amount, interest rate, and number of years.

How to Use the PPF Calculator

Using this tool is simple and requires only three inputs:

  • Enter your yearly investment amount.
  • Enter the interest rate of the PPF scheme.
  • Enter the number of years you want to invest.
  • Click the calculate button.

The calculator will instantly display the maturity value of your PPF investment.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is PPF?
Public Provident Fund is a long term government backed savings scheme available in India.

What is the lock in period of PPF?
The lock in period of a PPF account is 15 years.

Is PPF investment tax free?
Yes, PPF investments qualify for tax deduction under Section 80C and the maturity amount is tax free.
