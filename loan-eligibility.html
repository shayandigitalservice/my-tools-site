What is Loan Eligibility?

Loan eligibility refers to the maximum loan amount that a lender is willing to offer you based on your financial profile. Banks and financial institutions evaluate your income, expenses, credit history, and repayment capacity before approving a loan.

Using a loan eligibility calculator helps you estimate this amount instantly without visiting a bank or filling out long forms.

How Loan Eligibility Calculator Works

The calculator works by analyzing your monthly income and subtracting your expenses to calculate your disposable income. A portion of this disposable income is considered safe for EMI payments.

Based on this EMI capacity, along with interest rate and tenure, the calculator estimates the total loan amount you are eligible for.

Basic Formula Concept

  • Disposable Income = Income – Expenses
  • Maximum EMI = 40% to 50% of Disposable Income
  • Loan Eligibility = EMI-based calculation using interest and tenure
