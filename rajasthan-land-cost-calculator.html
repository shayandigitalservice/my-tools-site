How to Use Rajasthan Land Cost Calculator?

Is calculator ka istemal karna behad saral hai:

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Rajasthan mein 1 Bigha mein kitna Biswa hota hai?
Ans: Rajasthan revenue standards ke anusar 1 Pucca Bigha mein 20 Biswa hote hain.

Q2. Pucca Bigha aur Kucha Bigha mein kya antar hai?
Ans: Pucca Bigha bada hota hai (27,225 Sq Ft), jabki 1 Pucca Bigha barabar 3 Kucha Bigha hota hai.

Q3. Kya DLC rate registry ke liye use hota hai?
Ans: Ji haan, Rajasthan mein registry ke waqt DLC rate (Sarkari Rate) par Stamp Duty deni hoti hai.

Q4. 1 Biswa kitne Square Feet ka hota hai?
Ans: Rajasthan mein 1 Biswa exactly 1,361.25 Square Feet ka hota hai.
