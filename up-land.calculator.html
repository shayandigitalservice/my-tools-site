1. Introduction: UP Mein Zameen Ka Mahtva

Uttar Pradesh, Bharat ka sabse ghan-abaadi wala aur rajnaitik roop se sabse prabhavshali rajya hone ke saath-saath, ek bada krishi pradhan (agricultural) desh bhi hai. Yahan ki arthvyavastha mukhya roop se Gangetic plains ki upjau mitti par tiki hui hai. Yahi wajah hai ki UP mein zameen ki paimaish (measurement) ka system sadiyon purana hai aur aaj bhi wahi paramparagat units jaise Bigha, Biswa, aur Biswansi ka istemal hota hai.

2. Understanding "Pucca Bigha" vs "Kucha Bigha"

UP mein 'Bigha' shabd sunte hi sabse bada confusion yeh hota hai ki wo Pucca hai ya Kucha. Is antar ko samajhna 2500 words ki is guide ka sabse mahatvapurna hissa hai.

What is Pucca Bigha?

Sarkari dastawezon, registry, aur court ke kaamo mein humesha Pucca Bigha ka istemal hota hai. 1 Pucca Bigha barabar 27,225 Square Feet ($165 \text{ ft} \times 165 \text{ ft}$) hota hai. Ise hi standard mana jata hai aur humara calculator isi par kaam karta hai.

What is Kucha Bigha?

Dehati ilakon mein kisan aapsi vyavhar ke liye Kucha Bigha ka use karte hain. Yeh Pucca Bigha ka thik ek-tihaayi ($1/3$) hota hai, yaani lagbhag 9,075 Square Feet. Google par log aksar in dono ke beech confuse ho jate hain, isliye humesha "Pucca Bigha" par hi bharosa karein.

3. Detailed Hierarchy of UP Land Units

UP mein zameen naapne ka system ek chain ki tarah kaam karta hai. Niche humne isse micro-level tak explain kiya hai:

  • 1 Bigha (Pucca) = 20 Biswa
  • 1 Biswa = 20 Biswansi (Jise Dhur bhi kaha jata hai)
  • 1 Biswansi = 20 Kachwansi
  • 1 Kachwansi = 20 Unwansi

Yeh hierarchy itni purani hai ki aaj bhi patwari isi ka istemal karte hain. Agar aapne 1 Bigha zameen li hai, toh iska matlab aapke paas 20 Biswa zameen hai.

5. The "Laggi" System in Uttar Pradesh

Jaise Bihar mein Laggi chalti hai, UP mein bhi local level par Laggi ka use hota hai. UP mein standard 99-inch ki laggi ya 5.5 haath ki laggi ka prayog kiya jata hai. Dhur ya Biswansi ka square area isi laggi par nirbhar karta hai. Agar aap West UP (Meerut, Saharanpur) mein hain, toh wahan ki units East UP (Ballia, Ghazipur) se thodi variant ho sakti hain, lekin sarkari record 27,225 sq. ft. hi rahega.

7. How to Check Bhulekh UP Records

Agar aapne UP mein zameen kharidi hai, toh aapko **Bhulekh UP** ki official website par ja kar apna "Khatauni" check karna chahiye. Wahan area "Hectare" mein likha hota hai. 8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: UP mein 1 Biswa mein kitne square feet hote hain?
Ans: Uttar Pradesh mein 1 Biswa barabar 1,361.25 Square Feet hota hai.
Q2: UP mein 1 Acre mein kitna bigha hota hai?
Ans: UP ke Pucca Bigha ke hisaab se 1 Acre mein 1.6 Bigha hota hai.
Q3: Biswansi kya hota hai?
Ans: Biswansi UP ki ek choti unit hai, jise kai jagahon par 'Dhur' bhi kaha jata hai. 20 Biswansi milkar 1 Biswa banate hain.
