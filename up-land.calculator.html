Uttar Pradesh Land Measurement Guide 2026: Bigha, Biswa, and Dhur Explained

1. Introduction: UP Land Records and Measurement

Uttar Pradesh, Bharat ka sabse bada rajya hone ke saath-saath land records ke maamle mein bhi kaafi vishal hai. Yahan zameen ki naap-jokh (paimaish) mukhya roop se Pucca Bigha, Biswa, aur Biswansi par adharit hai. Jab aap Lucknow, Kanpur, Noida ya Varanasi mein zameen ki baat karte hain, toh local units ko samajhna bahut zaroori ho jata hai.

2. The Core Units: Pucca Bigha, Biswa, and Dhur

UP mein zameen ko mukhya roop se teen hisson mein baata gaya hai. Inhe samajhna isliye zaroori hai kyunki sarkari Khatauni aur Jamabandi mein data isi format mein hota hai.

What is 1 Pucca Bigha in UP?

UP mein 1 Bigha (Standard Pucca) barabar 27,225 Square Feet hota hai. Agar hum ise meter mein badlein, toh yeh 2530 Square Meters hota hai. Dhyaan rahe, UP ke kuch pashchimi (Western) ilakon mein 'Kucha Bigha' bhi chalta hai, jo Pucca Bigha ka 1/3 hota hai.

The Concept of 20 Biswa

Bhai, UP ka sabse basic formula hai—"1 Bigha mein kitna Biswa hota hai?" Iska jawab hai **20 Biswa**. Har Biswa ka size 1,361.25 square feet hota hai. Jab aap plot kharidte hain, toh rate aksar 'Per Biswa' ke hisaab se hi tay hota hai.

Biswansi (Dhur): The Micro Unit

Biswansi UP ki micro-unit hai, jise kai jagahon par 'Dhur' bhi kaha jata hai. 1 Biswa mein 20 Biswansi hote hain. Iska matlab hai ki 1 Biswansi barabar 68.06 square feet hota hai.
