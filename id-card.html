About School ID Card Generator

School ID Card Generator is a powerful online tool designed to create professional PVC style student identity cards easily. Schools, coaching institutes and training centers can generate high quality ID cards with student photo, school logo, class details and contact information within seconds. This tool helps institutions save time and printing costs while producing modern ATM-style ID cards suitable for PVC printing.

How to Use

Enter school name, year, student name, parent name, class, section and roll number. Upload student photo, school logo and principal signature. After entering the details click preview card to generate the design. Choose one of the available professional templates to customize the card appearance. Finally download the card as PNG or PDF for PVC printing.

Features

  • Professional PVC ID card size (ATM style)
  • Front and back side card design
  • Upload school logo and student photo
  • Principal signature support
  • 20 professional design templates
  • Download PNG or PVC PDF
  • Direct print option
