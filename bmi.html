What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation used to determine whether a person has a healthy body weight based on their height and weight.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30+
