What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation used to check whether your body weight is healthy according to your height.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30+

Benefits

  • Quick health check
  • Helps in fitness planning
  • Easy and fast
