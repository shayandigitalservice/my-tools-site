What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a simple calculation that helps determine whether a person has a healthy body weight based on their height and weight. BMI is commonly used by health professionals to identify weight categories such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or higher
