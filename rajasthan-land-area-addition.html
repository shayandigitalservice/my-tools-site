Rajasthan Land Area Addition: Bigha, Biswa aur Biswansi ka Total Jod Kaise Karein?

Rajasthan mein zameen ka hisab-kitab kaafi dilchasp hai. Yahan **Apna Khata (E-Dharti)** portal par records Hectare mein hote hain, lekin jab baat kharid-farokht ki aati hai, toh log hamesha **Bigha aur Biswa** mein hi baat karte hain. Rajasthan mein alag-alag zilon mein Bigha ka size alag ho sakta hai (Pucca Bigha vs Kuchha Bigha), lekin calculation ka formula hamesha same rehta hai.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **Rajasthan Land Area Addition Calculator** kisanon aur property dealers ke liye ek vardan hai. Yahan **20 Biswansi milkar 1 Biswa** banti hai aur **20 Biswa milkar 1 Bigha**. Agar aap manually jodenge toh galti hone ka darr rehta hai, lekin hamara tool automatic logical carry-forward apply karta hai.

Is Calculator Ko Use Kaise Karein? (Step-by-Step)

  • Step 1: Sabse pehle apne pehle plot (Plot #1) ka Bigha, Biswa aur Biswansi input boxes mein bharein.
  • Step 2: Dusre khet ki details fill karein.
  • Step 3: Agar 2 se zyada plots hain, toh "+ Add More Plot" button click karke naye rows jodein.
  • Step 4: "Calculate Total Area" par click karte hi result screen par aa jayega.

5 Practical Examples: Rajasthan Land Logic

  1. Ex 1: 15 Biswa + 10 Biswa = **1 Bigha 5 Biswa** (20 Biswa = 1 Bigha).
  2. Ex 2: 18 Biswansi + 5 Biswansi = **1 Biswa 3 Biswansi**.
  3. Ex 3: 2 Bigha 18 Biswa + 1 Bigha 5 Biswa = **4 Bigha 3 Biswa**.
  4. Ex 4: 5 Bigha 19 Biswa aur 1 Biswa milkar **6 Bigha** ban jayenge.
  5. Ex 5: 10 Biswansi + 15 Biswansi = **1 Biswa 5 Biswansi**.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Rajasthan mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Biswa hote hain?
Ans: Rajasthan ke standard revenue records ke mutabik 1 Bigha mein 20 Biswa hote hain.

Q2. Kya ye tool Kuchha Bigha ke liye kaam karega?
Ans: Ji haan, calculation logic (20 unit wala) same rehta hai, bas square feet value badal jati hai.

Q3. Kya main result WhatsApp par bhej sakta hoon?
Ans: Bilkul! Humne special WhatsApp button diya hai.
