Why Accuracy Matters in Age Calculation

The Gregorian calendar is inherently complex. With February alternating between 28 and 29 days every four years and other months varying between 30 and 31 days, a simple subtraction of years is never enough for official documentation. For instance, being 18 years old "as of" a certain date is a strict requirement for Indian banking and government sectors. Our Online Age Finder utilizes a specialized logical sequence to ensure that every leap day is accounted for, providing a result that is legally and mathematically sound.
