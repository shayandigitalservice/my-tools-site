Supported Image Formats

1. JPG (Joint Photographic Experts Group)

JPG is one of the most commonly used image formats. It is best for photographs and images with many colors. It provides good quality with smaller file size, making it ideal for websites.

2. PNG (Portable Network Graphics)

PNG is perfect for images that require transparency. It is commonly used for logos, icons, and graphics. PNG files are higher quality but larger in size compared to JPG.

3. JPEG

JPEG is similar to JPG and is widely used for digital images. It offers a balance between quality and compression.
