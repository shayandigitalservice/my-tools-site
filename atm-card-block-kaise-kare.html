ATM Card Block Kaise Kare Online 2026

Lost ya Stolen Debit Card Ko Turant Block Karne Ka Guide

ATM card ya debit card aaj ke time me banking ka sabse important tool ban chuka hai. Iski help se aap ATM se cash nikal sakte ho, online payment kar sakte ho aur shopping bhi kar sakte ho.

Lekin agar aapka ATM card kho jaye ya chori ho jaye to turant card block karna bahut zaruri hota hai. Agar card block nahi kiya gaya to koi bhi aapke bank account se paise nikal sakta hai.

Is article me hum aapko batayenge ki **ATM card block kaise kare online**, bank app se card block kaise kare aur customer care se card block kaise kare.

ATM Card Block Karna Kyu Zaruri Hai

Agar aapka debit card kho jata hai to kisi aur ke haath me pad sakta hai. Aise me fraud hone ka risk badh jata hai.

  • Bank account safe rakhne ke liye
  • Fraud transaction rokne ke liye
  • Online payment misuse se bachne ke liye
  • Unauthorized ATM withdrawal rokne ke liye

ATM Card Block Karne Ke Tarike

ATM card block karne ke kai tarike hote hain:

  • Bank mobile app se
  • Internet banking se
  • Customer care call karke
  • Bank branch visit karke

Online ATM Card Block Kaise Kare

Step 1

Sabse pehle apne bank ki official mobile banking app open kare.

Step 2

Login kare using mobile number ya customer ID.

Step 3

Card services ya debit card option select kare.

Step 4

Block card ya hotlist card option par click kare.

Step 5

Confirm button par click kare aur card block ho jayega.

Customer Care Se ATM Card Block Kaise Kare

Agar aap online card block nahi kar pa rahe ho to bank customer care par call karke bhi card block kar sakte ho.

Example:

  • SBI Customer Care: 1800-1234
  • HDFC Customer Care: 1800-202-6161
  • ICICI Customer Care: 1800-1080

Internet Banking Se ATM Card Block Kare

Agar aap internet banking use karte ho to website ke through bhi card block kar sakte ho.

Steps:
  • Bank website open kare
  • Internet banking login kare
  • Debit card services open kare
  • Block card option select kare

ATM Card Block Hone Ke Baad Kya Kare

Card block hone ke baad aap new ATM card apply kar sakte ho.

  • Bank branch visit kare
  • Online debit card request kare
  • New ATM card receive kare

ATM Card Safety Tips

  • ATM PIN kisi ke saath share na kare
  • Unknown website par card details enter na kare
  • Transaction alerts enable rakhe
  • Suspicious activity ho to bank ko inform kare
