Why Choose PNG Format for Your PDF Pages?

While JPG is common, PNG (Portable Network Graphics) is the superior choice for document conversion in 2026. PNG is a "lossless" format, meaning it does not lose quality every time the file is opened or saved. This is crucial for PDFs that contain text, fine lines, or logos. By converting your PDF to PNG, you ensure that every pixel remains sharp and every word stays legible.

Key Advantages of PNG Conversion:

  • Lossless Compression: Keep your document's original quality intact without blurriness.
  • Transparency Support: Perfect for extracting logos or signatures from PDF backgrounds.
  • Better Text Rendering: Unlike JPG, PNG does not create "artifacts" around text characters.
  • Web Compatibility: PNGs are natively supported by all browsers, apps, and presentation software like PowerPoint and Keynote.
