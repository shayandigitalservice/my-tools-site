About This Tool

The Profit Loss Calculator is a simple online tool that helps you calculate profit, loss and percentage instantly. It is useful for shop owners, traders, small business owners and students who want to quickly determine whether a product sale results in profit or loss.

By entering the cost price and selling price, the calculator automatically calculates the profit or loss amount and the percentage. This removes the need for manual calculations and provides accurate results in seconds.

The tool works directly in your web browser and does not require any download or registration. It is fully compatible with mobile phones, tablets and desktop devices, making it easy to use anytime.

How to Use

  • Enter the cost price of the product.
  • Enter the selling price of the product.
  • Click the Calculate button.
  • The tool will instantly display profit or loss and the percentage.

FAQ

What is profit?
Profit occurs when the selling price of a product is higher than its cost price.

What is loss?
Loss occurs when the selling price is lower than the cost price.

Is this calculator free?
Yes, this tool is completely free to use.

Can I use it on mobile?
Yes, the calculator works on smartphones, tablets and desktop devices.
