What is Profit Loss Calculator?

A profit loss calculator is an online tool that calculates the difference between cost price and selling price. It helps you determine whether you made a profit or a loss and also calculates the percentage.

  • If Selling Price > Cost Price → Profit
  • If Cost Price > Selling Price → Loss
  • If both are equal → No Profit No Loss

How to Use Profit Loss Calculator

  1. Enter the cost price of your product
  2. Enter the selling price
  3. Click on the calculate button
  4. Instantly view profit/loss and percentage

This tool is designed for simplicity, so even beginners can easily use it without any confusion.

Profit and Loss Formula

Profit Formula

Profit = Selling Price – Cost Price

Loss Formula

Loss = Cost Price – Selling Price

Profit Percentage Formula

Profit % = (Profit ÷ Cost Price) × 100

Loss Percentage Formula

Loss % = (Loss ÷ Cost Price) × 100
