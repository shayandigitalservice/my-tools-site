2. The Core Units: Bigha, Katha, and Lessa

Assam ke revenue department ke anusar, zameen ko teen pramukh hisson mein baata gaya hai. Inhe samajhna isliye zaroori hai kyunki jab aap Jamabandi ya Dharitree portal par apna record check karte hain, toh data isi format mein hota hai.

What is 1 Bigha in Assam?

Assam mein 1 Bigha standard roop se 14,400 Square Feet hota hai. Agar hum ise yard (gaj) mein badlein, toh yeh 1,600 Square Yards hota hai. Aksar log padosi rajya West Bengal se confuse ho jate hain jahan calculation alag hai.

The Concept of 5 Katha

Bhai, sabse bada sawal jo log puchte hain wo hai—"Assam mein 1 bigha mein kitna katha hota hai?" Iska seedha jawab hai **5 Katha**. Har katha ka size 2,880 square feet hota hai.

5. How to Check Land Records in Assam (Dharitree)

Digital India ke antargat, Assam sarkar ne **Dharitree Portal** shuru kiya hai. Is portal par aap apne Dag Number aur Patta Number ke zariye zameen ka area dekh sakte hain. Aksar wahan area "B-K-L" (Bigha-Katha-Lessa) format mein hota hai.
