UPI Payment Limit Increase Kaise Kare 2026

Step by Step Official Bank Guide

UPI payments India me sabse popular digital payment method hai. Kabhi-kabhi users ko higher transaction amount bhejna ya receive karna padta hai. Iske liye aapko UPI daily/transaction limit increase karni hoti hai.

UPI Payment Limit Kya Hai

UPI payment limit matlab maximum amount jo aap ek din ya ek transaction me transfer kar sakte ho. Ye limit bank aur app ke upar depend karti hai.

Reasons To Increase UPI Limit

  • High value payment karna ho
  • Business transactions
  • Medical bills ya education fees pay karna ho
  • Official payments

Required Details

  • Registered mobile number linked with bank account
  • Bank account details
  • UPI app installed (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM)
  • Debit card or MPIN ready

UPI Payment Limit Increase Kaise Kare

Step 1 – Bank/UPI App Open Kare

Apni UPI app open kare. Agar multiple apps hain, to jis app se high payment karna hai wahi choose kare.

Step 2 – Bank Account Link Verify Kare

Bank account properly linked hai ya nahi check kare. KYC complete hona chahiye.

Step 3 – Transaction Limit Option Select Kare

App settings me “Transaction Limit / UPI Limit” ya “Payment Settings” option select kare.

Step 4 – New Limit Set Kare

Bank allowed maximum amount ke according new limit set kare. Daily aur per transaction limit set ki ja sakti hai.

Step 5 – MPIN/OTP Verification

Debit card/MPIN/OTP verify kare. Limit successfully update ho jayega.

Important Tips

  • Official bank apps hi use kare
  • MPIN ya debit card details safe rakhe
  • Fake apps ya phishing sites se bache
  • Daily limit dheere-dheere increase kare, zyada sudden increase avoid kare

Benefits of Increasing UPI Limit

  • High value payments instantly kar sakte ho
  • Business aur official payments easy ho jati hain
  • Bank branch jane ki zarurat nahi

Conclusion

UPI payment limit increase karna official bank app ya portal se easy hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apni limit safely badha sakte ho aur hassle-free digital payments kar sakte ho.
