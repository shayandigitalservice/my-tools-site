Rajasthan Land Calculation: Tedi-Medi Zameen ki Napai Rajasthan Mein Kaise Karein?

Rajasthan ka bhaugolik ilaka kafi bada hai aur yahan khet aksar retile tilon ya purani seemao ki wajah se tede-mede hote hain. Rajasthan mein **Apna Khata (E-Dharti)** portal par zameen hectare mein hoti hai, lekin aam log ise Bigha aur Biswa mein hi samajhte hain. Jab zameen tedi-medi ho, toh average nikalne se hamesha galti hoti hai.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **Rajasthan Irregular Land Calculator** kisanon ki isi samasya ka samadhan hai. Ye tool Heron’s Formula ka use karta hai, jo zameen ko do parts mein baant kar ekdam accurate total area nikalta hai.

उपयोग कैसे करें (How to Use)

  1. Khet ki charo sides (Side A, B, C, D) ko feet mein naapiye.
  2. Ek kone se tirche kone tak ki lambai (Diagonal) naapiye.
  3. In 5 numbers ko calculator mein bhariye aur "Calculate Area" dabaiye.
