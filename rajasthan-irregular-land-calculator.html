Rajasthan Irregular Land Calculation: Tedi-Medi Zameen Kaise Naapein?

Rajasthan mein aksar retile ilakon ya purane khet ki boundaries ki wajah se zameen ekdam seedhi nahi hoti. **Apna Khata Rajasthan** par area hectare mein hota hai, lekin kisan ise Bigha mein samajhte hain. Shayan Smart Tools ka ye calculator Rajasthan ke standard (1 Bigha = 20 Biswa) par base hai.

Important Measurement Rules:

Rajasthan mein hamesha **Pucca Bigha (27,225 sq ft)** ka hi use hota hai sarkari records mein. Hamara tool isi accurate measurement par result deta hai.
