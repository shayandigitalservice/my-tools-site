Haryana Registration Fee Slabs Explained

Haryana mein flat 1% logic ke bajay strict structural slabs hote hain:

  • ₹5 Lakh tak ki property par: Flat ₹1,000
  • ₹5 Lakh se ₹10 Lakh tak: Flat ₹2,000
  • ₹10 Lakh se ₹25 Lakh tak: Flat ₹5,000
  • ₹25 Lakh se ₹50 Lakh tak: Flat ₹10,000
  • ₹50 Lakh se ₹1 Crore tak: Flat ₹25,000
  • ₹1 Crore se upar ki sabhi properties par: Maximum **₹50,000** boundary cap lagti hai.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Haryana mein registry stamp duty par mahilaon ko kya discount milta hai?
Ans: Haryana government male buyers ke mukable female buyers ko stamp duty par pure 2% ki standard relaxation pradan karti hai.

Q2. Kya Haryana mein registration fee unlimited hoti hai?
