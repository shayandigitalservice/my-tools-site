UP Irregular Land Calculation: Uttar Pradesh Mein Tedi-Medi Zameen Kaise Naapein?

Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein khet ya plot aksar ekdam square ya rectangle shape mein nahi hote. Chakbandi ke baad bhi kai baar boundaries tedi-medi reh jati hain. Aise mein kisan bhai aksar "Average Method" (Lambaai × Chaudai) ka use karte hain, jo ki galat hai. Isse ya toh aapka nuksan hota hai ya fir padosi ka.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **UP Irregular Land Area Calculator** "Heron’s Formula" ka upyog karta hai. Is formula se tedi zameen ko do triangles (tribhuj) mein baant kar ekdam sateek (100% accurate) area nikala jata hai. Chahe aapka plot Lucknow mein ho ya Varanasi mein, ye tool UP Bhulekh ke standard Bigha, Biswa, aur Biswansi ke hisab se perfect result deta hai.

How to Use: Step-by-Step Guide (उपयोग करने का तरीका)

  1. Sabse pehle apne tedi-medi plot ke chaaro sides (A, B, C, D) ko feet mein naapein.
  2. Khet ke ek kone se dusre kone tak ek tirchi line (Diagonal/Vikarn) naapein, jaisa diagram mein dikhaya gaya hai.
  3. In 5 values ko upar diye gaye boxes mein bharein.
  4. "Calculate Area" button par click karein. Tool turant aapko Bigha, Biswa aur Biswansi mein result dikha dega.
