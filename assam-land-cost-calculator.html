Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Assam mein 1 Bigha mein kitna Katha hota hai?
Ans: Assam revenue standards ke anusar 1 Bigha mein 5 Katha hote hain.

Q2. Lessa kya hota hai?
Ans: Lessa Assam ki sabse choti land unit hai. 1 Katha mein 20 Lessa hote hain.

Q3. Kya Registry fees total price mein shamil hai?
Ans: Nahi, yeh calculator sirf zameen ki market value dikhata hai. Registry fees (Stamp Duty) aapko alag se deni hogi.

Q4. 1 Katha kitne Square Feet ka hota hai?
Ans: Assam mein 1 Katha exactly 2,880 Square Feet ka hota hai.

Q5. Kya ye tool Guwahati ke liye accurate hai?
Ans: Ji haan, yeh pure Assam ke standard units par based hai.
