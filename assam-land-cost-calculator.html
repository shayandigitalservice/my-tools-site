3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Assam Land Registry

Q1. Assam me property registry charge kitna percent hai?
Ans: Assam me land registry ke liye standard Stamp Duty rate Male ke liye 5%, Female ke liye 3% hai aur Registration Fee flat 1% apply hoti hai.
