3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Assam Land Registry

Q1. Assam me property registry charge kitna percent hai?
Ans: Assam me land registry ke liye standard Stamp Duty rate Male ke liye 5%, Female ke liye 3% hai aur Registration Fee flat 2% rules baseline apply hoti hai.

Q2. Kya Assam me women registry par discount milta hai?
Ans: Haan, Assam me mahilaon ke naam par registry karwane par standard stamp duty me 2% ki choot milti hai, jisse unka stamp slab strictly 3% ho jata hai.

Q3. Assam me 1 Bigha me kitne square feet hote hain?
Ans: Assam benchmark framework metrics systems layout instructions data sheets me 1 Bigha hamesha 14,400 Sq.Ft ke barabar save kiya jata hai.

Q4. Online Dharitri Assam land records check karne ki portal link kya hai?
Ans: Official land records ko securely track karne ke liye authorized platform dharitri.assam.gov.in land revenue portal operational hai.
