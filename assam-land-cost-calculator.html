1. Introduction to Assam Property Valuation Framework

Assam me bhoomi (land) kharidte ya bechte samay exact registry budget aur government charges ka pata lagana behad zaroori hai. Assam Sarkar ke **Revenue and Disaster Management Department** ke niyamawali ke anusar zameen ki valuation local Circle Rates ke aadhar par hoti hai. Assam me Registration Fee poore desh me sabse zyada **8.5%** hai, jiske sath gender-wise Stamp Duty lagti hai. Shayan Smart Tools ka yeh advanced automated calculator aapko sateek metrics provide karta hai jisse aap pure land core capital bina kisi manual mathematical error ke seconds me calculate kar sakte hain.

2. How to Use the Assam Land Registry Budget Calculator

  • Step 1 (Land Area Value): Sabse pahle apni zameen ka area input box me fill karein (jaise 1, 2, 5).
  • Step 2 (Area Unit Selection): Dropdown menu se sahi unit select karein (Bigha, Katha, Lessa, Acre, ya Square Feet).
  • Step 3 (Price Per Unit / Circle Rate): Apne area ka official Govt Circle Rate price enter karein.
  • Step 4 (Region & Ownership Type): Property ka zone (Urban/Rural) chunein aur Buyer ke gender ke anusar Ownership standard select karein.
  • Step 5 (Calculate Budget): "Calculate Budget Now" button par click karte hi detailed ledger framework report open ho jayegi.

3. Important Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Assam Land Records

Q1. Assam me registration fee kitni hai?
Ans: Assam me property registration fee flat 8.5% hai, jo pure India me sabse zyada slabs me se ek hai.

Q2. Online Assam land registration records check karne ke liye kaun sa portal hai?
Ans: Assam government ka official portal Dharitree (revenueassam.nic.in/dharitree/) land records aur tracking ke liye authorized platform hai.

Q3. Assam me 1 Bigha me kitna Katha aur Square Feet hota hai?
Ans: Assam me 1 Bigha me 4 Katha (या 80 Lessa) hote hain, aur total square feet area 14,400 Sq. Ft. hota hai.
