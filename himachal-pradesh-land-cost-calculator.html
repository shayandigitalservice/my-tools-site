3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Himachal Pradesh Land Registry

Q1. Himachal Pradesh me property registry charge kitna percent hai?
Ans: Himachal Pradesh me land registry ke liye standard Stamp Duty rate Male ke liye 5%, Female ke liye 4% hai aur Registration Fee flat 2% apply hoti hai.

Ans: Haan, Himachal me mahilaon ke naam par registry karwane par standard stamp duty me 1% ki choot milti hai, jisse unka stamp slab 4% ho jata hai.

Q3. Himachal Pradesh me 1 Bigha me kitne square feet hote hain?

Q7. Property registries circle rate base par kyu calculated hoti hai?
