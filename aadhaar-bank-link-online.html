Aadhaar Bank Link Online Kya Hai

Aadhaar bank link online ka matlab hai apne bank account ko Aadhaar number se internet banking ya mobile app ke through connect karna.

Kya Aadhaar Bank Link Pure Online Ho Sakta Hai?

✔ Haan (Bank Dependent)

Kuch banks net banking ya mobile app se Aadhaar linking allow karte hain.

❌ Nahi (Sab Banks Me Nahi)

Kuch banks me aapko branch visit karna padta hai.

Method 1 – Net Banking Se Aadhaar Link Kare

Step 1 – Bank Website Open Kare

Apne bank ki official website open kare.

Step 2 – Login Kare

User ID aur password se login kare.

Step 3 – Profile / Services Section

“Aadhaar Linking” ya “Update Aadhaar” option select kare.

Step 4 – Aadhaar Number Enter Kare

Apna Aadhaar number enter kare.

Step 5 – OTP Verification

Registered mobile par OTP aayega, use enter kare.

Step 6 – Submit Kare

Submit karte hi request complete ho jayegi.

Method 2 – Mobile Banking App Se Link Kare

Step 1 – Bank App Open Kare

Apne bank ka official app open kare.

Step 2 – Login Kare

MPIN ya password se login kare.

Step 3 – Aadhaar Linking Option

Services me Aadhaar link option select kare.

Step 4 – Aadhaar Enter Kare

Aadhaar number enter karke submit kare.

Important Tips

  • Mobile number Aadhaar se linked hona chahiye
  • Sirf official bank website/app use kare
  • OTP kisi ke sath share na kare
  • Details sahi enter kare

Conclusion

Aadhaar bank link online karna possible hai lekin bank par depend karta hai. Agar aapka bank online facility deta hai to aap ghar baithe linking kar sakte ho, warna branch visit karna padega.
