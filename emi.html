What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the amount paid by a borrower to a lender every month until the loan is fully repaid. It includes both principal amount and interest.

  • Principal Amount – The loan amount you borrow
  • Interest Rate – The cost of borrowing
  • Loan Tenure – Duration of repayment

How EMI Calculator Works

The EMI calculator uses a mathematical formula to calculate the monthly installment based on your inputs.

EMI Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1]

  • P = Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Number of Months

Our tool automatically calculates EMI using this formula and shows instant results.
