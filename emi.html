What is EMI Calculator?

An EMI Calculator is a free online financial tool that helps you calculate your monthly loan installment easily. EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment which is the fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender every month.

How EMI is Calculated

EMI depends on three important factors:

  • Loan Amount
  • Interest Rate
  • Loan Tenure

Our online EMI calculator automatically calculates your monthly EMI based on these inputs. It is useful for home loans, car loans, personal loans and other types of loans.

  • Helps plan your monthly budget
  • Compare different loan options
  • Free and easy to use
