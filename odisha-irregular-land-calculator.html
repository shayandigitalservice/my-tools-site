1. Introduction to Irregular Land Dynamics in Odisha (Jami Mapa Tedi-Medi)

Odisha mein agricultural fields aur residential plots ka survey records kafi traditional aur irregular boundaries ke saath maps me darj hota hai. Coastal belts (Cuttack, Puri, Balasore) se lekar Western Odisha (Sambalpur, Bargarh) tak geometric structures uneven lengths hold karte hain. Jab property patches straight rectangle nahi hote, toh use mathematical configuration me hum **Irregular Quadrilateral** bolte hain.
