How to Use: Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Apne tedi zameen ke charo sides (Side A, B, C, D) ko feet mein naapein.
  2. Zameen ke ek kone se dusre kone tak ek tirchi line (Diagonal) naapein (jaisa diagram mein dikhaya gaya hai).
  3. In 5 values ko calculator mein bharein.
  4. Tool aapko turant Bigha, Kattha, Dhur aur Dhurki mein result bata dega.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Bihar mein 1 Bigha mein kitna Kattha hota hai?

Ans: Bihar mein 1 Bigha standard scale system parameters ke mutabik 20 Kattha hota hai.

Q2. Dhurki kya hoti hai?

Ans: Dhur se bhi choti unit ko Dhurki kehte hain. 20 Dhurki milkar 1 Dhur banti hai (Each dhurki size is equal to 3.40 Sq.Ft spatial block size).

Q3. Kya bina diagonal ke area nikal sakta hai?

Ans: Nahi, tedi-medi zameen ke liye diagonal (tirchi line) bisection calculation ke liye naapna 100% zaruri hai.
