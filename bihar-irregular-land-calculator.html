Bihar Land Area Addition: Tedi-Medi Zameen Kaise Naapein?

Bihar mein zameen ki napai karna koi asan kaam nahi hai, khas kar jab khet ya plot **Tedi-Medi (Irregular)** shape mein ho. Bihar ke kisan aur property dealers aksar average nikal kar zameen naapte hain, jisme dhur ya dhurki ka nuksan ho jata hai. Ek dhurki ki keemat bhi aaj ke zamane mein bohot hoti hai.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **Bihar Irregular Land Calculator** "Heron’s Formula" par kaam karta hai. Isme aapko zameen ke 4 sides aur ek tirchi line (**Diagonal**) naapni hoti hai. Ye tool automatic zameen ko do triangles mein baat kar ekdam sateek result deta hai, jo **Bihar Bhulekh** records ke liye 100% sahi hai.

How to Use: Step-by-Step Guide

  • Apne tedi zameen ke charo sides (Side A, B, C, D) ko feet mein naapein.
  • Zameen ke ek kone se dusre kone tak ek tirchi line (Diagonal) naapein (jaisa diagram mein dikhaya gaya hai).
  • In 5 values ko calculator mein bharein.
  • Tool aapko turant **Bigha, Kattha, Dhur aur Dhurki** mein result bata dega.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Bihar mein 1 Bigha mein kitna Kattha hota hai?
Ans: Bihar mein 1 Bigha = 20 Kattha hota hai.

Q2. Dhurki kya hoti hai?
Ans: Dhur se bhi choti unit ko Dhurki kehte hain. 20 Dhurki milkar 1 Dhur banti hai.

Q3. Kya bina diagonal ke area nikal sakta hai?
Ans: Nahi, tedi-medi zameen ke liye diagonal (tirchi line) naapna 100% zaruri hai.
