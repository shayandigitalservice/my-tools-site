2. Geometric Methodology: Multi-Triangle Slicing and Heron's Formula

Kisi bhi tedi-medi zameen ka exact area map nikalne ke liye use separate smaller triangles me traverse karna sabse certified process hai. Jab aap geometric layout par boundaries cross diagonals join karte hain, to pure plot ka structure independent components me split ho jata hai. Agar kisi triangle ki teeno sides criteria values (Side A, Side B, Side C) feed ki jayein, to mathematical systems me standard Heron's Formula run hota hai:

s = (a + b + c) / 2

Area = √(s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c))
