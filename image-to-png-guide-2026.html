PNG Format ke Faide

  • High Resolution: PNG mein photo ke colors aur details hamesha sharp rehte hain.
  • Transparency: Agar aap logo ya icon bana rahe hain, toh PNG hi best choice hai.
  • Lossless: Kitni baar bhi save karein, photo ki quality kam nahi hoti.
  • Professional Use: Printing aur web designing ke liye sabse zyada use hone wala format.
