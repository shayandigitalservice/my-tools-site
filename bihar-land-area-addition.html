Bihar Land Area Addition: Zameen Ka Jod Kaise Karein?

Bihar mein zameen ka lenden aur registry hamesha Bigha, Kattha aur Dhur ke aadhar par hoti hai. Lekin kisanon aur aam aadmi ko tab dikkat aati hai jab unhe apne alag-alag khet ya plots ka kul jod (Total Sum) nikalna hota hai. Bihar ka land conversion system "Base 20" par chalta hai, jiske karan manual calculation mein galti hone ke kafi chances rehte hain.

Shayan Smart Tools ka yeh Bihar Land Area Addition Calculator isi samasya ka hal hai. Yeh tool Bihar ke official revenue units ke anusaar banaya gaya hai, jo automatic "Carry-Forward" logic apply karke aapko sateek parinaam deta hai.

Bihar Land Addition Tool Ke Fayde:

  • 20-Kattha Logic: Yeh tool Bihar ke standard (1 Bigha = 20 Kattha) ko follow karta hai.
  • Dhur to Kattha Conversion: Jaise hi Dhur ka jod 20 hota hai, yeh automatic usey 1 Kattha maan leta hai.
  • User Friendly: Aap jitne chahen utne plots ek click par jod sakte hain.

How to use:

1. Plot #1 mein Bigha, Kattha aur Dhur ki value bharein.
2. Plot #2 mein dusre khet ki jankari likhein.
3. Naye plot jodne ke liye "+ Aur Plot Jodein" ka use karein.
4. Result dekhne ke liye "Total Jod Nikalein" par click karein.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Bihar mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Kattha hote hain?
Ans: Bihar mein 1 Bigha barabar 20 Kattha hota hai.

Q2. Dhur aur Kattha ka kya rishta hai?
Ans: Bihar mein 20 Dhur milkar 1 Kattha banate hain.

Q3. Kya ye tool khet aur plot dono ke liye hai?
Ans: Ji haan, ye har tarah ki zameen ke jod ke liye upyogi hai.

Q4. Kya ye registry mein help karega?
Ans: Haan, ye aapko total area samajhne mein madad karega taaki aap registry fee ka anuman laga sakein.
