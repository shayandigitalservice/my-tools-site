3. Mandatory Documents Checklist for Land Registry in Tamil Nadu

TNREGINET registry process sub-registrar office me securely finalise karne ke liye khariddaar (buyer) aur bechne wale (seller) ke paas niche diye gaye valid certificates aur reports hone anivarya hain:

  • Patta Chitta Copy: Digitally verified land ownership copy extracted from eservices.tn.gov.in portal.
  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC): Minimum 15-30 years history log sheet verifying clear asset transaction layout backgrounds.
  • Parent Documents: Previous sale deed record chains tracing original developer allocation tracking matrices.
  • FMB Map Sketch: Field Measurement Book mapping vectors illustrating land coordinate scale boundary layout details.
  • Identity Records: Government verification proofs including PAN Card, Voter ID, or relevant official documents.

5. 15 Important Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Tamil Nadu Land Records & Registry

Q1. What is the current stamp duty rate in Tamil Nadu 2026?
Ans: Tamil Nadu me current property registration par Stamp Duty flat 7% hai.

Q2. What are the registration fees for land in Tamil Nadu?
Ans: Tamil Nadu government property ke guideline value par flat 4% registration fee charge karti hai.

Q3. How much is 1 Cent in Square Feet in Tamil Nadu?
Ans: Tamil Nadu standard measurement ke mutabik 1 Cent exact 435.6 Sq.Ft. hota hai.

Q4. How many Square Feet make 1 Ground in Chennai?
Ans: Urban layout or Chennai regions me 1 Ground barabar 2,400 Sq.Ft. hota hai.

Q5. Is there a registration fee concession for women in Tamil Nadu?
Ans: Nahi, Tamil Nadu me male, female ya joint registry par ek saman (7% + 4%) charges lagte hain.

Q6. Which official website is used to check TN guideline value?
Ans: Tamil Nadu land guideline value check karne ke liye official portal tnreginet.gov.in hai.

Q7. How can I download Patta Chitta online in Tamil Nadu?
Ans: Aap TN Land Records ke official platform eservices.tn.gov.in par jaakar Patta Chitta download kar sakte hain.

Q8. What is total registration cost percentage in TN?
Ans: Tamil Nadu me total overhead registry tax 11% (7% Stamp Duty + 4% Registration Fee) hai.

Q9. What is Guideline Value vs Market Value in Tamil Nadu?
Ans: Guideline Value government ke revenue records ka fixed rate hai, jabki Market Value wo demand price hai jis par deal hoti hai.

Q10. How many Cents are there in one Acre?
Ans: South India land measurement standard ke anusar 1 Acre me pure 100 Cents hote hain.

Q11. Minimum how many years Encumbrance Certificate (EC) is required?
Ans: Safe and clean property deal ke liye sub-registrar office me 15 se 30 years tak ka EC check kiya jata hai.

Q11. Minimum how many years Encumbrance Certificate (EC) is required?
Ans: Safe and clean property deal ke liye sub-registrar office me 15 se 30 years tak ka EC check kiya jata hai.

Q12. Can we register land below guideline value in TN?
Ans: Nahi, Tamil Nadu rule ke mutabik guideline value se kam rate par registry parakriya illegal mani jati hai.

Q13. What is FMB map sketch in Tamil Nadu registry?
Ans: FMB (Field Measurement Book) map ek visual sketch hota hai jo zameen ke exact coordinates aur boundary lines ko darshata hai.

Q14. What documents are needed for land registration in Tamil Nadu?
Ans: Patta Chitta copy, Encumbrance Certificate (EC), Parent Deeds, FMB Sketch, aur ID Proofs (PAN, Aadhaar) anivarya hain.

Q15. Is legal documentation or lawyer fees included in this calculator?
Ans: Nahi, yeh calculator sirf government fees (Stamp Duty & Reg Fees) calculate karta hai; local handling charges alag se hote hain.
