Weight to Price Calculator Online

The Weight to Price Calculator is a free online tool that helps users quickly calculate the total price of a product based on its weight and price per kilogram. This calculator supports multiple weight units including Gram (g), Kilogram (Kg), Quintal (Qt), Ton, and Pound (lb). It automatically converts the selected unit into kilograms and calculates the accurate total price instantly.

This tool is extremely useful for shopkeepers, traders, wholesalers, farmers, and buyers who deal with weight-based products. Instead of doing manual calculations, users can simply enter the weight and price per kilogram to get the correct price within seconds.

How to Use the Weight to Price Calculator

  • Enter the weight of the product
  • Select the unit (Gram, Kg, Quintal, Ton, or Pound)
  • Enter the price per kilogram
  • Click the Calculate Price button

The calculator will instantly display the total price based on the weight and price per kilogram.

Supported Weight Units

  • Gram (g)
  • Kilogram (Kg)
  • Quintal (Qt)
  • Ton
  • Pound (lb)

Weight Conversion Formula

  • 1 Kilogram = 1000 Gram
  • 1 Quintal = 100 Kilogram
  • 1 Ton = 1000 Kilogram
  • 1 Pound = 0.453592 Kilogram
