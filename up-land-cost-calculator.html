Introduction (Dumdar Prastavana)

Uttar Pradesh (UP) Bharat ka sabse bada rajya hai, jahan property aur real estate market hamesha garam rehta hai. Lucknow, Noida, Kanpur ya Varanasi—kahin bhi zameen kharidne par sabse badi tension hoti hai keemat ka sahi hisab lagana. UP mein zameen zyadatar "Per Bigha" ya "Per Biswa" ke hisaab se bechi jati hai.

Hamara UP Land Cost Calculator Shayan Smart Tools dwara UP Revenue standards ko dhyan mein rakh kar banaya gaya hai. Yeh calculator aapko bina kisi galti ke total land price batata hai, taaki aap apna budget sahi se plan kar sakein aur mol-bhav (negotiation) mein koi dhokha na ho.

How to Use UP Land Cost Calculator?

Is calculator ka upyog karna behad aasan hai:

  1. Dropdown menu se unit select karein (Pucca Bigha, Biswa, ya Biswansi).
  2. "Enter Area" mein zameen ki quantity bharein (Example: 2.5 Bigha).
  3. "Rate per Unit" mein us area ka market rate likhein.
  4. "Calculate Total Price" button par click karein.
  5. Result box mein aapko total Rupees aur Words mein price dikh jayegi.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. UP mein 1 Bigha mein kitna Biswa hota hai?
Ans: Uttar Pradesh standard ke anusar 1 Pucca Bigha mein 20 Biswa hote hain.

Q2. Pucca Bigha aur Kucha Bigha mein kya antar hai?
Ans: Pucca Bigha bada hota hai (27,225 Sq Ft), jabki 1 Pucca Bigha barabar 3 Kucha Bigha hota hai.

Q3. Kya Registry fees calculator mein shamil hai?
Ans: Nahi, yeh sirf zameen ki keemat batata hai. Stamp Duty aur Registration fees UP mein lagbhag 5% to 7% alag se lagti hai.

Q4. 1 Biswa kitne Square Feet ka hota hai?
Ans: UP mein 1 Biswa exactly 1,361.25 Square Feet ka hota hai.

Q5. Kya ye tool Noida ke liye manya hai?
Ans: Ji haan, yeh pure UP ke standard units par based hai.
