Uttar Pradesh Land Registration: Detailed Overview

Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein land registration aur property valuation ek strict regulatory framework ke tahat aati hai, jise State Government ka Stamp and Registration Department (IGRSUP) manage karta hai. Jab bhi aap UP mein koi zameen, plot ya residential property khareedte hain, toh aapko state ke circle rates ke mutabik registry budget ready karna hota hai. Yeh interactive portal aapko transparent system provide karta hai taaki aap automated tareeqe se accurate tax calculation aur total acquisition metrics evaluate kar sakein bina kisi manual error ke.

Required Documents for Property Registry in Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh IGRSUP portal ya Sub-Registrar Office (SRO) par registry document execution ke time in documents ki requirement hoti hai:

  • Original Sale Deed Draft (Vikray Patra).
  • Identity Proof documents (Jaise Passport, Voter ID Card, ya Driver's License).
  • Address Proof Documents Buyer aur Seller dono ke liye.
  • Khane/Zameen ka official map aur UP Bhulekh ROR (Khatauni) details.
  • Stamp Duty aur Registration online payment receipt printout.
  • Two passport size photographs of all parties (Buyer, Seller, and Witnesses).
  • NOC (No Objection Certificate) agar property disputed boundary area mein aati hai toh.
