Bihar Land Cost Calculation Guide: Zameen Ka Sahi Mol Nikalein

Bihar mein zameen kharidne se pehle uska total budget nikalne ka sabse aasan tarika.

Introduction (Dumdar Prastavana)

Bihar mein zameen ka karobaar bohot tezi se badh raha hai. Chahe aap Patna mein plot le rahe hon ya Darbhanga ke gramin ilakon mein khet, sabse pehla sawal hota hai— "Zameen ki total keemat kya hogi?" Bihar mein zameen ka rate aksar "Per Kattha" ya "Per Dhur" ke hisaab se bataya jata hai. Lekin jab area points mein ho (jaise 2.5 Kattha), toh aam aadmi ke liye calculation mushkil ho jati hai.

Hamara Bihar Land Cost Calculator isi samasya ko hal karne ke liye banaya gaya hai. Yeh tool Shayan Smart Tools ki ek vishesh peshkash hai jo Bihar ke revenue standards (5.5 Haath ka Lagga) ko dhyan mein rakhte hue banaya gaya hai. Is calculator se aap seconds mein zameen ka total value nikal sakte hain.

How to Use Bihar Land Cost Calculator?

Is calculator ka upyog karna behad saral hai:

  1. Sabse pehle dropdown menu se apni unit chunein (Kattha, Bigha, ya Dhur).
  2. "Enter Area" box mein zameen ki sankhya likhein jo aap kharidna chahte hain.
  3. "Rate per Unit" mein us area ka current market rate ya sarkari rate (MVR) bharein.
  4. "Calculate Total Price" button par click karein.
  5. Tool aapko total keemat ₹ (Rupees) mein aur Words mein bhi dikha dega.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Bihar mein zameen ka rate kaise tay hota hai?
Ans: Bihar mein do tarah ke rate hote hain—Sarkari Rate (MVR) aur Market Rate. Registry hamesha MVR ya usse zyada par hoti hai.

Q2. 1 Kattha mein kitna Dhur hota hai?
Ans: Bihar mein hamesha 1 Kattha mein 20 Dhur hote hain.

Q3. Kya Registry fees land cost mein include hoti hai?
Ans: Nahi, land cost ke upar aapko lagbhag 6% to 9% stamp duty aur registration fee alag se deni hoti hai.

Q4. 1 Bigha mein kitna Kattha hota hai?
Ans: Bihar standard ke anusar 1 Bigha mein 20 Kattha hota hai.

Q5. Kya ye tool khet aur plot dono ke liye hai?
Ans: Ji haan, ye tool har tarah ki zameen ke mol-bhav ke liye upyogi hai.
