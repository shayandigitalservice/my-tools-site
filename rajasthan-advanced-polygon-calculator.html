Rajasthan Advanced Polygon Land Calculator: Tedi-Medi Zameen ki Sateek Napai Rajasthan Mein

Rajasthan ka bhaugolik kshetra (Geographical area) bohot bada hai aur yahan zameen ki boundaries aksar retile tilon, purani medon ya pahadi rasto ki wajah se ekdam seedhi nahi hoti. Rajasthan mein jab kisi plot ya khet ke 4 se zyada konne (corners) hote hain, toh use "Polygon" shape kaha jata hai. Aisi tedi-medi zameen ka area nikalne ke liye sadharan lambaai aur chaudai ka formula hamesha galat result deta hai.

Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **Rajasthan Advanced Land Tool** Heron's Formula aur Multi-Triangle method ka upyog karke aapko 100% accurate result deta hai. Ye tool khas taur par Rajasthan ke kisanon ke liye design kiya gaya hai jo apne khet ki sateek napai **Pucca Bigha, Biswa aur Biswansi** mein chahte hain.

Rajasthan Mein Zameen Kaise Naapein? (Step-by-Step Guide)

Tedi zameen ko naapne ka sabse sahi tarika hai use triangles (trivhuj) mein baant lena. Agar aapka plot kafi teda hai, toh use kai triangles mein divide karein (Diagram dekhein). Har triangle ki teeno sides ka map feet mein lekar is calculator mein bharein.

Agar aapko sirf simple conversion chahiye, toh aap hamara Rajasthan Land Unit Converter use kar sakte hain. Lekin complex shapes ke liye ye advanced tool hi sabse behtar hai.

How to Use This Advanced Tool?

  1. Apne khet ko triangles mein divide karein aur har side ka map feet mein lein.
  2. "Triangle #1" ki teeno sides (A, B, C) ko calculator mein enter karein.
  3. Agar zameen badi hai, toh "+ Add Triangle" button se aur triangles jodiye.
  4. "Calculate" par click karte hi aapko Bigha, Biswa, Biswansi, Acre aur Hectare mein sateek jankari mil jayegi.
