1. Introduction to Irregular Land Formations in Jharkhand (Zameen Maapi Background)

Jharkhand ke vibhinn zilon jaise Ranchi, Dhanbad, Bokaro, aur Jamshedpur ka bhougolik paridrishya (terrain) kafi pathrila aur tedi-medi simao (boundaries) wala hai. Sadiyon purane gramin kshetro ke batwaare aur pahadi dhalano ke karan, yahan ki lagbhag 85% se zyada jameen ke plots aur khet perfectly rectangle ya square aakar me nahi hain. Jab kisi plot me char se zyada corners hote hain, to use geometry me Polygon (बहुकोण) kehte hain. Aisi tedi-medi zameen par keval lambai $\times$ choudai lagane se area humesha galat nikalta hai, jisse aage chalkar dastaweji ya registry me vivad paida hota hai.

Jharkhand Rajaswa Vibhag (JharBhoomi portal guidelines) ke anusar, mutation aur zameen naapi ke samay sateek math ka upyog hona anivary hai. Is samasya ko door karne ke liye professional ameen aur engineers Multi-Triangle Polygon Geometric Mapping ka upyog karte hain. 2. Geometric Methodology: Heron's Formula and Multi-Triangle System

Kisi bhi tedhe-medhe aakar ke plot ka sateek area nikalne ka sabse vishwasniya tarika use chhote aur swatantra right/irregular triangles me baat dena hai. Jab aap khet ke aamne-saamne ke kono par diagonal lines (vikarn) kheenchte hain, to complex polygon chhote triangles me badal jata hai. Har ek triangle ki teeno sides (Side A, Side B, Side C) ka naap lekar hum geometry ka sabase prasiddh Heron’s Formula lagate hain:

$$s = \frac{a + b + c}{2}$$

$$\text{Area} = \sqrt{s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c)}$$

3. Important Documents: JharBhoomi, Register-II, and Khata Number

Jharkhand me zameen ki naapi aur uske sateek record ke liye in technical terms ko janna bohot zaroori hai:

A. JharBhoomi Portal: Yeh Jharkhand sarkar ka official land records portal hai jahan se aap apna digital Khatian aur Register-II online check kar sakte hain.

B. Register-II (पंजी-II): Yeh kisi bhi mauza (village) ka jamabandi register hota hai jo zameen ke malikana haq aur lagan (tax) ke bhugetan ko certified karta hai.

C. Khata and Khesra Number (खाता और खेसरा नंबर): Khata number parivar ya maalik ke pure ledger ko darshata hai, jabki Khesra number har ek specific plot (tukde) ka unique identifier dynamic code hota hai.

Q1. Jharkhand mein 1 Kattha me kitna Square Feet hota hai?
Ans: Official Jharkhand revenue standards ke anusar 1 Kattha me sateek 720 Square Feet area hota hai.

Q2. 1 Bigha me kitne Kattha hote hain?
Ans: Jharkhand me 1 Bigha poore 20 Kattha ke barabar hota hai, jisme kul 14,400 Square Feet hote hain.
