Common LIC Premium FAQs

How is LIC Premium Calculated?

Premium depends on age, policy term, sum assured, bonuses, and optional riders.

Can I Calculate LIC Premium Online?

Yes, use our LIC Premium Calculator to calculate online in seconds.

Why is LIC Premium Important?

Knowing your premium ensures timely payments and proper financial planning.

Which LIC Plans Can I Calculate?

You can calculate premiums for Term, Endowment, Money Back, and Whole Life Plans.

Tips to Reduce LIC Premium

  • Choose longer policy terms to reduce annual premiums.
  • Select pure Term Insurance for affordable premiums.
  • Avoid optional riders to keep costs low.
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle to lower premium rates.
