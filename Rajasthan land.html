Rajasthan Land Measurement: Pucca vs Kucha Bigha

Rajasthan mein zameen ka maapan ek dilchasp vishay hai. Yahan "Pucca Bigha" sarkari dastawezon mein use hota hai, jabki "Kucha Bigha" local kisan aapsi vyavhar mein use karte hain. Shayan Smart Tools ke is guide mein hum Rajasthan Bhulekh ke har ek unit ko detail mein samjhayenge.

1. What is 1 Pucca Bigha?

Rajasthan mein 1 Pucca Bigha $27,225 \text{ sq. ft.}$ hota hai. Yeh bilkul UP aur Bihar ke standard jaisa hai. Isme 20 Biswa hote hain.

2. What is 1 Kucha Bigha?

Kucha Bigha chota hota hai, jo lagbhag $17,424 \text{ sq. ft.}$ ka hota hai. Rajasthan ke kai districts mein log aaj bhi Kucha Bigha mein hi baat karte hain, isliye humare calculator mein dono options diye gaye hain.
