3. Mandatory Documents Checklist for Land Registry in Telangana

IGRS Telangana registry process sub-registrar office (SRO) me securely finalise karne ke liye khariddaar (buyer) aur bechne wale (seller) ke paas niche diye gaye valid certificates aur reports hone anivarya hain:

  • Dharani Portal Documents: Digitally updated **Patta Kadar Passbook** aur land tracking allocation slips for agricultural sectors.
  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC): Minimum 30 saal ka structural track records statement proving clear land title coordinates.
  • Official Land Map / Layout: HMDA (Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority) ya DTCP approved layout copy configurations.
  • Identity Proof Verification: Dono party (Buyer, Seller & Witness) ka PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID ya valid photo documentation.
  • Latest Property Tax Receipts: Non-agricultural blocks ke context me updated municipal property assessment tax logs statement.
  • Online Payment Challans: IGRS portal par stamp duty, transfer duty aur registration fees pre-pay karne ke baad generated official e-challan layer copies.

5. 15 Important Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Telangana Land Records & Rate Dynamics

Q1. Telangana me land registration charges kitne percent hai?
Ans: Telangana me total land registration charges 7.5% lagte hain. Isme 5.5% Stamp Duty, 1.5% Transfer Duty aur 0.5% Registration Fee shamil hai.

Q2. Kya Telangana me female property registration par discount milta hai?
Ans: Nahi, Telangana me male aur female dono ke liye property registration aur stamp duty rates bilkul barabar (7.5% total) hain.

Q3. Telangana land measurement ke liye 1 Gunta kitne Sq Yards hota hai?
Ans: Telangana standard revenue rules ke mutabik 1 Gunta land exact 121 Square Yards (ya 1089 Square Feet) ke barabar hota hai.

Q4. Dharani Portal par market value check kaise kare?
Ans: Official Dharani portal (dharani.telangana.gov.in) par jaakar 'View Market Value' link par click kare, phir apna District, Mandal aur Village select karke kisi bhi survey number ka rate dekh sakte hain.

Q5. IGRS Telangana portal ka main use kya hai?
Ans: IGRS Telangana portal ka use non-agricultural property ki details check karne, Encumbrance Certificate (EC) nikalne aur online stamp duty payment challan generate karne ke liye kiya jata hai.

Q6. 1 Acre me kitne Gunta aur Sq Yards hote hain?
Ans: Telangana measurement system me 1 Acre me full 40 Guntas hote hain, jo ki mathematical total 4840 Square Yards ke barabar hota hai.

Q7. Property market value aur structural price me kya farq hai?
Ans: Market value government dwara side circle rates hoti hai jo registry cost taye karti hai, jabki structural price construction quality ke calculation ke baad nikalta hai.

Q8. Patta Kadar Passbook agricultural land entry ke liye kyu zaroori hai?
Ans: Patta Kadar passbook zameen ke pakke malikana haq ka saboot hai, iske bina Dharani system me slot booking aur registry transfer freeze rehta hai.

Q9. Property registry ke baad Mutation hone me kitna time lagta hai?
Ans: Dharani agricultural portal par registry ke sath automatic instant mutation ho jata hai, jabki non-agricultural properties me CDMA registry se lagbhag 15 din lagte hain.

Q10. Encumbrance Certificate (EC) kitne saal ka check karna chahiye?
Ans: Telangana me kisi bhi safe property purchase deed se pehle IGRS portal se kam se kam 30 saal purana digital Encumbrance Certificate track record verify karna chahiye.

Q11. Prohibited properties list Telangana me kaise check kare?
Ans: Dharani aur IGRS portals par Section 22-A tab ke andar jakar aap government dwara block ya disputed prohibited lands ki direct update download karke read kar sakte hain.

Q12. HMDA approved plots par additional registry charges hote hain?
Ans: Nahi, local development infrastructure changes development layouts par registration process same rehta hai par unka circle base valuation rates highly double set hota hai.

Q13. Kya is land calculator tool ka data mobile desktop responsive hai?
Ans: Haan, Shayan Smart Tools ka yeh advance core CSS pipeline automation fully optimized responsive layout design system standard par programmed hai.

Q14. Telangana sub-registrar challan valid kab tak rehta hai?
Ans: Online portal se submit kiya gaya e-challan generation transaction verification system successfully clear hone ke baad generalized 90 days tak active rehta hai.

Q15. Ghar baithe zameen ke documents verify kaise kare?
Ans: Agri plots ke liye Dharani and non-agri residential structures ke liye IGRS Telangana analytics portal select karke verification identity status checks online print kar sakte hain.
