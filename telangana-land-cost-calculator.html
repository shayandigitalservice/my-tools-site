3. Mandatory Documents Checklist for Land Registry in Telangana

IGRS Telangana registry process sub-registrar office (SRO) me securely finalise karne ke liye khariddaar (buyer) aur bechne wale (seller) ke paas niche diye gaye valid certificates aur reports hone anivarya hain:

  • Dharani Portal Documents: Digitally updated **Patta Kadar Passbook** aur land tracking allocation slips for agricultural sectors.
  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC): Minimum 30 saal ka structural track records statement proving clear land title coordinates.
  • Official Land Map / Layout: HMDA (Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority) ya DTCP approved layout copy configurations.
  • Identity Proof Verification: Dono party (Buyer, Seller & Witness) ka PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID ya valid photo documentation.
  • Latest Property Tax Receipts: Non-agricultural blocks ke context me updated municipal property assessment tax logs statement.
  • Online Payment Challans: IGRS portal par stamp duty, transfer duty aur registration fees pre-pay karne ke baad generated official e-challan layer copies.

5. 15 Important Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Telangana Land Records & Rate Dynamics

Q1. Kya Telangana mein plots aur agricultural land ko measure karne ke alag standards hain?
Ans: Haan, Telangana me commercial plots ko strictly Square Yards (गज) ya Sq.Ft me evaluate kiya jata hai, jabki agricultural farmsteeds ko Acres aur Guntas me calculate kiya jata hai.

Q2. June 2026 ke naye dynamic tax rules ke anusar Telangana me total registry charges kitne hain?
Ans: Telangana sarkar ke structural revision ke tehat total capital levy 7.50% hai, jisme 5.50% Stamp Duty, 1.50% Transfer Duty aur 0.50% Registration Fees shamil hain.

Q3. Telangana me online plot registration check karne ke liye kaun sa official framework kaam karta hai?
Ans: Non-agricultural zones ki structural tracking ke liye **IGRS Telangana (registration.telangana.gov.in)** portal aur agricultural lands ke liye official **Dharani Portal** deploy kiya gaya hai.

Q4. Kya Telangana registration framework me female ownership par koi concessional stamp duty relief milta hai?
Ans: Nahi, Chhattisgarh ke viprit, Telangana state regulation me male, female aur joint categories ke liye standard slab base uniform (7.50% total) set kiya gaya hai.

Q5. Telangana Land Budget Calculator engine me 1 Square Yard me kitne Square Feet embedded hote hain?
Ans: Is calculation panel me mathematical precision maintain karne ke liye 1 Square Yard (Yard) ko exact 9 Square Feet (Sq.Ft) ke baseline equation par scale kiya jata hai.

Q6. Collector Guideline Value (Sarkari Rate) aur Market Value me variance kyun aata hai?
Ans: Collector value minimum layout floor budget matrix hai jo state compute karti hai tax sourcing ke liye, jabki high-intent corporate infrastructure hubs ke karan actual transaction market price badh jaata hai.

Q7. Dharani portal status dashboard par Patta Kadar Passbook ka kya relevance hai?
Ans: Patta Kadar Passbook transparent ownership code layout card hai. Iske bina sub-registrar parameters agricultural assets transfer transaction record ko finalise nahi karte.

Q8. Kya urban HMDA-approved layouts par koi specific local cess parameters applicable hote hain?
Ans: Transfer duty (1.50%) municipal areas aur urban execution systems ke stabilization framework layer me already default tracking infrastructure layout me embedded rehti hai.

Q9. Property Registry execute karne ke baad structural Mutation (Dakhil-Kharij) me kitna samay lagta hai?
Ans: Agricultural deals me Dharani dynamic database auto-mutation strictly slot selection timeline ke andar (instantaneously) finalise karta hai, jabki non-agri assets me CDMA channels se 15 din lagte hain.

Q10. Agar verification ledger me Encumbrance Certificate (EC) missing ho toh kya impact hoga?
Ans: Bina clear EC layer logs ke absolute title ownership mapping dispute zone me chali jaati hai, jisse legal compliance checks fail ho sakte hain aur registration block ho sakti hai.

Q11. Kya Telangana land calculator tool me sub-registrar scanning sheets aur stamp processing layer charges alag se include hain?
Ans: Nahi, yeh engine data frameworks strictly fundamental components (Base Cost, Stamp duty structure, Registration processing slab, and Transfer duty) ko map karta hai.

Q12. Telangana me 1 Gunta land measure exact kitne standard values scale ko evaluate karta hai?
Ans: Standard Telangana conversion parameters rules ke tehat 1 Gunta land area precisely 121 Square Yards (equivalent to 1,089 Sq.Ft structural tracking) hota hai.

Q13. Kya is metrics matrix tool ka processing pipeline real-time mobile responsive layouts ko dynamic support karta hai?
Ans: Haan, is responsive CSS code structure design ecosystem ko complete adaptive framework rules par render kiya jata hai jo dynamic display configurations output deta hai.

Q14. Telangana me disputed lands ya prohibited plots properties status check karne ka kya rule hai?
Ans: Buyers official IGRS ya Dharani portal tracking list dashboards par jaakar **Prohibited Properties Section (Section 22-A)** log inputs filter evaluate kar sakte hain.

Q15. HMDA aur DTCP layouts plots calculations metrics me valuation matrices ka main diff kya hai?
Ans: HMDA structural development metrics high-scale connectivity tracks standard par design hone ki wajah se inside metro peripheral zones general collector valuations multipliers double set hote hain.
