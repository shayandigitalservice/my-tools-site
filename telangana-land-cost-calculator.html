Telangana Land Cost & Registry Valuation Framework (All 33 Districts Covered)

Telangana state me land aur real estate ki registry, stamp duty calculations, aur legal procedures ko properly execute karne ke liye area classification ko samajhna zaroori hai. **Telangana Revenue Act** ke mutabik state me Male, Female, ya Joint registration holdings par alag se koi concession ya tax chhoot nahi di jaati hai. Sabhi gender profiles ke liye flat **7.5% official registry matrix** apply hota hai.

Lekin calculation metrics me sabse bada factor **Urban vs Rural area classification** hota hai. Urban areas (Municipal Corporations/Municipalities) me transactions primarily **Square Yards (Sq Yds / गज)** me measure hote hain, jabki Rural areas (Gram Panchayats) me agricultural and residential properties ka evaluation hamesha **Acres aur Guntas** me kiya jata hai jise official Dharani database track karta hai.
