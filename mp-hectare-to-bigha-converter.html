2. Decoding MP Land Metrics Hierarchy: Pakka Bigha, Biswa, and Kattha

Madhya Pradesh me different divisions (jaise Indore, Bhopal, Gwalior, Jabalpur aur Ujjain) ke revenue guidelines ko analyze karne par pata chalta hai ki state me standard registries ke liye **Pakka Bigha** system ko hi basic core assessment benchmark mana gaya hai. Agar aap is systematic division sequence ko samajh lete hain, toh aap bina kisi professional map maker (Ameen ya Patwari) ke kisi bhi Khasra portal ledger copy ko easily trace out kar sakte hain.

MP government conversion scales ke mutabik, zameen ki mathematical standard positioning is prakar rests karti hai: State me 1 standard Pakka Bigha sateek **27,225 Square Feet** ke dimensional plot space ke equal hota hai. Agar hum is independent block dimensions ko square yards me trace out karein toh ye pure 3,025 Square Yards (वर्ग गज) banta hai, jo ki 165 feet lambi aur 165 feet chaudi zameen ka square segment hota hai ($165 \times 165 = 27225$). Is scale ke andar administrative operations chalane ke liye 20 symmetric internal sections banaye gaye hain jise 1 Biswa (ya kuch divisions me Kattha) kaha jata hai. Yaani ki, 1 Pakka Bigha me sateek 20 Biswa space matrix hotey hain. Is logic se 1 Biswa ka absolute mathematical design banta hai: $27,225 / 20 = \mathbf{1,361.25\text{ Square Feet}}$.
