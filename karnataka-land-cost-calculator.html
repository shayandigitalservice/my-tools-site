2. Practical Mathematical Computation Examples

Karnataka structural accounting systems ke configuration workflows ko chaliye do simple scenarios ke mathematical examples se samajhte hain:

Example A: Urban Region Acquisition (BBMP Zone - Standard Range)

  • Input Dimensions: 2000 Sq.Ft area valued at ₹3,000 per Sq.Ft baseline rate.
  • Pure Land Cost Base: 2000 * ₹3,000 = ₹60,00,000 (Valued above ₹45L, thus 5% Stamp Duty applies).
  • Base Stamp Duty (5%): 5% of ₹60,00,000 = ₹3,00,000.
  • BBMP Surcharge Deployment: 10% computed on the calculated Stamp Duty (10% of ₹3,00,000) = ₹30,000.
  • Registration Charges (1%): ₹60,00,000 * 1% = ₹60,00,000.
  • Total Estimated Net Budget: ₹60,00,000 + ₹3,00,000 + ₹30,000 + ₹60,000 = ₹63,90,000.
