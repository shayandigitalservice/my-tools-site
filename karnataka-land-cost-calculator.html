1. Required Documents for Land Registration in Karnataka

Property registry process ko successfully execute karne ke liye Karnataka Stamps and Registration Department ke rules ke mutabik following documentation complete hona mandatory hai:

  • Bhoomi RTC (Record of Rights, Tenancy and Crops): Current year ka digital land matrix record extract.
  • Sale Deed Document: Main structural financial assignment draft copy.
  • Khata Certificate & Extract: Local corporation standard layout log record (BBMP/E-Khata).
  • Property Encumbrance Certificate (EC): Form 15 ya Form 16 logs minimum past 13 to 30 years ka.
  • Government ID Proofs: Buyer aur Seller dono ka Aadhaar Card aur PAN Card framework ledger tags.
  • Sanction Plan / Layout Approval: BDA/BMRDA ya competent authority standard verification certificate layout.
