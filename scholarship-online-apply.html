School / College Scholarship Online Apply 2026

Step by Step Official Guide for Students & Parents

School aur college scholarship online apply karna ab simple aur official process hai. Ye guide aapko step by step process batayegi, taaki aap eligibility check karke apna application official portal se submit kar sakein.

Scholarship Kya Hai

Scholarship ek financial aid hai jo students ko study continue karne ke liye government ya private institutions provide karte hain. Ye merit, income aur category based ho sakti hai.

Required Documents / Details

  • Student Aadhaar / ID proof
  • Income certificate (Parent/Guardian)
  • Previous class marksheet / grade
  • Bank account details for scholarship transfer
  • Passport size photo
  • School / College enrollment details

Step 1 – Official Scholarship Portal Open Kare

Browser me relevant state / central scholarship portal open kare. Examples:

  • National Scholarship Portal (NSP)
  • State scholarship portal (e.g., Maharashtra – Mahadbt Portal)

Step 2 – Registration / Login

“New Registration / Login” option select kare. Student aur parent details fill kare:

  • Name, DOB, Gender
  • Aadhaar / ID proof
  • Mobile number / email verification
  • Set username & password for portal login

Step 3 – Eligibility Check

Portal me eligibility criteria select kare:

  • School / College level
  • Category (SC/ST/OBC/General)
  • Income group
  • State / Central scholarship scheme

Step 4 – Online Application Form Fill Kare

Step by step form fill kare:

  • Student personal details
  • School / College details
  • Parent income & bank details
  • Upload required documents
  • Review form and submit

Step 5 – Acknowledgement / Reference Number

Form submit karne ke baad ek unique reference number milega. Isko note kare for future tracking.

Step 6 – Track Application Status

“Track Application Status” section me jaake:

  • Reference number aur DOB enter kare
  • Status check kare – Pending / Approved / Rejected
  • Official receipt / confirmation PDF save kare

Official Scholarship Links

  • National Scholarship Portal (NSP)
  • Mahadbt Portal – Maharashtra
  • State-wise portal official link check kare

Important Tips

  • Official government portal hi use kare
  • All documents latest aur valid ho
  • Reference number safe rakhe
  • Multiple schemes ke liye alag-alag application submit kare
  • Deadline dhyan me rakhe

Benefits of Online Scholarship Apply

  • Direct government scholarship apply without agent
  • Track application status online
  • Secure aur transparent process
  • Financial aid for studies
  • Save time aur hassle-free process

Conclusion

School aur college scholarship online apply karna ab fast aur easy hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apna application official portal se submit aur track kar sakte ho aur scholarship ka benefit le sakte ho.
