Birth Certificate Online Kaise Banaye 2026

Complete Step by Step Hinglish Guide

Birth Certificate ek official document hai jo aapki birth details jaise name, date of birth, place of birth aur parents ke naam prove karta hai. Ye document school admission, passport apply, government schemes aur legal proofs ke liye bahut important hai.

Birth Certificate Kya Hai?

Birth Certificate ek government issued document hai jo proof karta hai ki kisi individual ki birth kab aur kahan hui. India me ye **Municipal Corporation / Panchayat / State Government** ke under issue hota hai.

Birth Certificate Ke Types

  • Hospital Birth Certificate: Ye certificate hospital se milta hai jahan birth hua ho.
  • Municipal / Nagar Nigam Birth Certificate: Ye official government document hai jo local authorities issue karte hain.
  • Online Birth Certificate: Aaj kal kai states me aap easily online portal se apply kar sakte ho.

Birth Certificate Ke Benefits

  • School admission ke liye proof
  • Passport aur PAN card ke liye identity proof
  • Government schemes aur benefits ke liye mandatory
  • Legal aur inheritance purposes ke liye zaruri

Birth Certificate Banane Ke Documents

  • Parents ka Aadhaar Card
  • Hospital birth slip ya medical certificate
  • Parents ki identity proof (PAN/Aadhaar/Passport)
  • Residential proof (Electricity bill/Ration card/Voter ID)
  • Form filled from local municipal portal

Birth Certificate Online Apply Kaise Kare

Step 1 State official portal open kare

Har state ka apna portal hota hai. Jaise:

  • CRS (Central Registration System) Portal
  • Maharashtra: MahaOnline Birth Certificate
  • Delhi: Delhi e-District

Step 2 New user registration kare

State portal me account create kare using email or mobile number.

Step 3 Login kare

Registered email/mobile number aur password se login kare.

Step 4 Birth Certificate apply option select kare

Dashboard me “Apply for Birth Certificate” ya similar option select kare.

Step 5 Form fill kare

  • Child Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Place of Birth (Hospital / Home)
  • Parents Name
  • Address Details

Step 6 Documents upload kare

Required documents upload kare jaise Aadhaar, hospital slip, address proof.

Step 7 Fees pay kare (if applicable)

Online payment karne ke liye net banking, UPI ya card use kar sakte ho. Fees generally ₹50-₹200 hoti hai depending on state.

Step 8 Application submit kare

Form submit karne ke baad confirmation aur application number milega. Ye number future reference ke liye save kare.

Step 9 Birth certificate download kare

Application approve hone ke baad aap PDF format me certificate download kar sakte ho ya physical copy ghar receive kar sakte ho.

Birth Certificate Status Kaise Check Kare

  • State portal open kare
  • Application number enter kare
  • Status check kare

Common Problems

  • Form incomplete submission
  • Document mismatch
  • Delay in approval
  • Wrong details in certificate
