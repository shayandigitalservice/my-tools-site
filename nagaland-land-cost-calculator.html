3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Nagaland Land Registry

Q1. Nagaland me property registry charge kitna percent hai?
Ans: Nagaland me land registration ke liye standard Stamp Duty rate Male aur General category ke liye 5%, Female indigenous tribe ke liye 3.50% hai aur Registration Fee flat 2% compute hoti hai.

Q2. Article 371A ke tahat Nagaland me land rules kya hain?
Ans: Article 371A ke anusar non-indigenous aur outsiders Nagaland me land buy nahi kar sakte. Zameen par strictly native tribes aur village councils ka permanent dynamic operational customary framework right rehta hai.

Q3. Nagaland me 1 Bigha me kitne square feet hote hain?
Ans: Nagaland verification and survey parameters me 1 Bigha benchmark standard exactly 14,400 Square Feet rectangular layout grid space hold karta hai.

Q4. Online Land Records tracking ke liye Nagaland me kya system chal raha hai?

Ans: Commercial regions jaise Dimapur me regular town layouts ke adhar par system metrics run hote hain, jabki Kohima aur interior hilly hubs me dynamic village standard authorities directly verification records map karti hain.

Q6. Nagaland property valuation metrics processing validation criteria kya hai?

Q7. Joint ownership properties me discount parameters split hote hain?
