Income Tax e-Verification / ITR Verification Online 2026

Step by Step Official Income Tax e-Filing Guide

Income Tax e-Verification / ITR verification online karna ab simple aur official process hai. Ye guide aapko step by step process batayegi, jaise ki e-Filing portal login, Aadhaar verification, net banking aur other methods se ITR verify karna.

Income Tax e-Verification Kya Hai

e-Verification ek process hai jisse aap apna ITR (Income Tax Return) officially verify karte ho government ke system me. Verification ke bina ITR processing complete nahi hoti.

Required Details / Documents

  • PAN Number aur Aadhaar
  • Registered mobile number aur email ID linked with PAN
  • Bank account details (for net banking verification)
  • ITR acknowledgement number (if filed already)
  • Login credentials for Income Tax e-Filing portal

Step 1 – Official Income Tax e-Filing Portal Open Kare

Browser me Income Tax e-Filing Portal open kare.

Step 2 – Login / Register Kare

  • “Login” option select kare
  • PAN aur password enter kare
  • Captcha / OTP verification complete kare
  • Dashboard successfully open hoga

Step 3 – ITR Verification Section

Dashboard me “e-Verify Return” option select kare:

  • Filed ITR list dikhegi
  • Return select kare jo verify karna hai
  • Verification methods available honge

Step 4 – Verification Methods

Official portal me multiple options hai:

  • Aadhaar OTP: Registered mobile par OTP aayega, enter kare aur verify kare
  • Net Banking: Linked bank account ke through verify kare
  • Debit / Credit Card: Instant verification ke liye
  • Digital Signature: DSC holder verify kar sakte hain
  • Bank ATM: ATM based ITR verification available hai kuch banks me

Step 5 – Verification Acknowledgement

  • Verification successful hone par acknowledgment PDF generate hoga
  • Download aur save kare future reference ke liye
  • e-Verification status “Verified” dashboard me dikhega

Important Tips

  • Official e-Filing portal hi use kare
  • Registered PAN aur Aadhaar details correct ho
  • Mobile / email OTP secure rakhe
  • Verification acknowledgment safe rakhe
  • Errors avoid karne ke liye portal instructions follow kare

Benefits of Online ITR e-Verification

  • Secure aur official government process
  • Immediate acknowledgment aur processing start
  • No branch visit required
  • Track ITR status easily
  • Reduce errors aur compliance easy

Conclusion

Income Tax e-Verification / ITR verification ab fast aur simple hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apna ITR official portal se verify kar sakte ho aur acknowledgement download karke secure rakh sakte ho.
