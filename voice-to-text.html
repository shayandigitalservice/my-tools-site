About Voice to Text Converter

The Voice to Text Converter is a powerful online speech recognition tool that allows you to convert spoken words into written text instantly. This free tool uses modern browser technology to recognize voice input and transform it into text with high accuracy. It supports multiple languages including English, Hindi, Assamese, Bengali and many other Indian languages. This makes it a useful tool for students, bloggers, writers, and professionals who want to type faster using their voice.

How to Use This Tool

Using the speech to text tool is very simple. First select your preferred language from the language dropdown menu. After selecting the language, click on the “Start Voice Typing” button and begin speaking clearly into your device microphone. The system will automatically detect your speech and convert it into text inside the text box. You can stop the recording anytime using the Stop button. After completing your speech typing, you can copy the generated text or clear the text area to start again.
