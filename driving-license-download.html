What is Digital Driving License?

Digital Driving License (DL) ek electronic version hai aapke physical driving license ka. Ye official and legally valid hai, aur aap mobile par download karke use kar sakte ho, especially during verification by traffic police or government authorities.

Eligibility Criteria

  • You must have a valid permanent or learner driving license issued by the RTO.
  • Mobile number should be registered with the RTO database.
  • Email address (optional for some portals) for receipt and verification.

Required Documents

  • Driving License Number / DL Number
  • Registered Mobile Number (for OTP)
  • Aadhaar Number (if linked for verification)
  • RTO issued details (optional but recommended)

Step by Step Guide to Download Driving License Online

Step 1 – Visit Official Parivahan / State RTO Portal

👉 Official Parivahan Sewa Portal

Select your state and go to the “Driving License / DL Download” section.

Step 2 – Enter DL Number

Input your 15-digit DL Number and date of birth as registered with RTO.

Step 3 – Verify via OTP

An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number. Enter it to verify your identity.

Step 4 – Download DL

After successful verification, download your digital Driving License in PDF format. You can also save it in DigiLocker if available.

Step 5 – Optional – Save in DigiLocker

Login to DigiLocker and upload / sync your DL for easy access anytime.

Common Issues and Fixes

  • OTP not received – Ensure your mobile number is registered and active.
  • DL not found – Check state RTO portal or verify DL number correctness.
  • Mismatch details – Ensure DOB, name, and DL number match exactly with RTO records.
  • Portal down – Try again later, some state portals may have temporary downtime.

FAQs

1. Is Digital DL legally valid?

Yes, digital DL issued via Parivahan portal or DigiLocker is legally valid across India.

2. Can I download Learner License as well?

Yes, learner license holders can download digital LL using the same portal.

3. Is there any fee for online DL download?

No, downloading digital DL is free of cost.

4. Can I use digital DL on phone without printing?

Yes, digital DL is valid on mobile devices. Keep it accessible during traffic checks.

5. What if DL is not linked with Aadhaar?

You can still download using DL number and DOB, but linking Aadhaar makes verification faster.

Tips for Successful DL Download

  • Use official Parivahan portal only.
  • Keep registered mobile number active.
  • Check DL number and DOB carefully.
  • Save PDF in device and optionally in DigiLocker for easy access.
  • Track RTO portal updates for any downtime or maintenance.
