EPF / Pension Withdrawal Online 2026

Step by Step Official EPFO Guide

EPF / Pension withdrawal online ab simple aur official process ke through possible hai. Ye guide aapko step by step process batayegi, jaise ki UAN login, claim submission, pension withdrawal aur online status tracking.

EPF / Pension Withdrawal Kya Hai

Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF) aur pension fund se eligible employees apna accumulated balance ya pension withdraw kar sakte hain. Withdrawal ke liye online claim submit karna aur e-Verification karna mandatory hai.

Required Details / Documents

  • UAN Number aur Password
  • Registered mobile number linked with UAN
  • Bank account linked with EPF
  • PAN / Aadhaar linked with EPF
  • Claim form details (online submission)

Step 1 – Official EPFO Member e-Sewa Portal Open Kare

Browser me EPFO Member e-Sewa Portal open kare.

Step 2 – Login via UAN

  • UAN number enter kare
  • Password enter kare
  • Captcha / OTP verification complete kare
  • Dashboard successfully open hoga

Step 3 – Claim / Withdrawal Section

Dashboard me “Online Services → Claim (Form-31, 19, 10C)” option select kare:

  • Form type select kare – PF withdrawal / Pension withdrawal
  • Member details auto-fetch honge
  • Bank account aur Aadhaar verify kare

Step 4 – Submit Claim Online

  • Withdrawal amount enter kare (full / partial as per eligibility)
  • Reason select kare – Retirement / Resignation / Medical / Housing / Pension
  • Declaration checkbox select kare
  • Submit button click kare
  • OTP verification on registered mobile complete kare
  • Claim successfully submit hoga aur reference number generate hoga

Step 5 – Track Claim Status

“Track Claim Status” section me jaake:

  • Reference number / UAN enter kare
  • Status check kare – Pending / Processed / Approved / Rejected
  • Approved hone par amount directly bank account me transfer hoga

Official EPFO Links

Important Tips

  • Official EPFO portal hi use kare
  • Bank account aur UAN details correct ho
  • Registered mobile active ho
  • OTP aur acknowledgement safe rakhe
  • Partial / full withdrawal rules carefully follow kare

Benefits of Online EPF / Pension Withdrawal

  • Secure aur official method
  • No branch visit required
  • Track claim status online
  • Direct bank transfer – fast aur hassle-free
  • Reduce errors aur compliance easy

Conclusion

EPF / Pension withdrawal ab fast aur simple hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apna claim online submit kar sakte ho, status track kar sakte ho aur approved amount directly bank account me receive kar sakte ho.
