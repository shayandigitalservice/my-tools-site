Calculation Example (Sahi Naap Kaise Nikalein)

Maan lijiye aapne Tezpur mein ek plot dekha jiski lambai 120 feet hai aur chaudai 60 feet hai. Toh uski calculation kuch is tarah hogi:

Total Square Feet = 120 ft × 60 ft = 7,200 sq. ft.
Ab isse Bigha mein badalne ke liye:
7,200 / 14,400 = 0.5 Bigha (Yaani ki 2 Katha aur 10 Lessa).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Assam mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Katha hote hain?

Ans: Assam ke standard revenue system ke anusar, 1 Bigha mein total 5 Katha hote hain.

Q2: Lessa unit ka kya matlab hai?

Ans: Lessa Assam ki sabse choti naapne ki ikai (unit) hai. 20 Lessa milkar 1 Katha banate hain aur 100 Lessa milkar 1 Bigha.
