3. Deep Insight: How to Read Dharitree Records?

Dharitree Assam portal se jab aap 'Fard' download karte hain, toh wahan Hectare values hoti hain. Hectare ko Assam ke local units mein badalne ke liye hume constants ki zaroorat hoti hai. 1 Hectare mein lagbhag 7.47 Bigha hote hain. Humara calculator automatically in values ko internal factors se multiply karke aapke liye output nikalta hai.

Zameen ki kharidari mein 'Mutation' (Namantaran) process ka bohot bada role hai. Agar aapka calculation galat hai, toh mutation ke waqt file reject ho sakti hai. Isliye, registry se pehle hamesha hamare tool se area double-check karein.
