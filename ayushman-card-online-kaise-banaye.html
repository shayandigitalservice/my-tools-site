Ayushman Card Online Kaise Banaye 2026

Complete Step by Step Hinglish Guide

Ayushman Card, ya Ayushman Bharat Yojana Card, ek government health insurance card hai jo free medical facilities aur health insurance benefits ke liye use hota hai. Ye card low-income families aur eligible citizens ke liye mandatory hai.

Ayushman Card Kya Hai?

Ayushman Card ek official document hai jo aapko Ayushman Bharat Scheme ke benefits provide karta hai. Iske through aap hospitals me free treatment aur insurance claim ke liye eligible ho jate ho.

Ayushman Card Ke Benefits

  • Free treatment up to ₹5 lakh per year
  • Cashless hospitalization in empanelled hospitals
  • Online verification for hospitals
  • Government health schemes ke liye required
  • Eligibility verification aur financial support

Required Documents for Ayushman Card

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Residential Proof (Electricity Bill, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Passport size photograph

Ayushman Card Online Apply Kaise Kare

Step 1 State official Ayushman Bharat portal open kare

Example:

  • Ayushman Bharat Official Portal
  • State wise portals: Kerala, Maharashtra, UP, etc. check kare.

Step 2 New user registration kare

Portal me apna mobile number aur email se account create kare.

Step 3 Login kare

Registered credentials se login kare.

Step 4 Ayushman Card apply option select kare

Dashboard me “Apply for Ayushman Card” option select kare.

Step 5 Form fill kare

  • Full name of head of family
  • Date of birth & family members details
  • Residential address
  • Aadhaar or ration card details

Step 6 Documents upload kare

Required documents upload kare jaise Aadhaar, photos, residential proof.

Step 7 Form submit kare

Submit karne ke baad confirmation aur application number milega. Save kare for future reference.

Step 8 Ayushman Card download kare

Verification ke baad aap PDF download kar sakte ho ya physical card receive kar sakte ho.

Ayushman Card Status Kaise Check Kare

  • Official portal open kare
  • Application number enter kare
  • Status check kare

Common Issues

  • Incomplete form submission
  • Document mismatch
  • Delay in approval
  • Wrong details in card
