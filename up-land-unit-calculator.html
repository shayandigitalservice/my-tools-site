Understanding Land Measurement in Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein zameen ki napai ke liye Pucca Bigha aur Kucha Bigha ka upyog kiya jata hai. Sarkari dastawezon mein adhiktat Pucca Bigha ka istemal hota hai. Ek Pucca Bigha mein 20 Biswa hote hain. Hamara yeh tool UP ke standard measurements par aadharit hai taaki aapko sahi jankari mil sake.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. UP mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Biswa hote hain?
Ans: UP mein 1 Pucca Bigha mein 20 Biswa hote hain.

Q2. Pucca Bigha aur Kucha Bigha mein kya antar hai?
Ans: Pucca Bigha Kucha Bigha se teen guna bada hota hai (1 Pucca Bigha = 3 Kucha Bigha).
