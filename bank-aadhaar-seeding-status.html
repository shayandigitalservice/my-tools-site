Bank Aadhaar Linking / Seeding Status 2026

Step by Step Official Guide for Checking Status

Important:
Aadhaar linking with your bank account is mandatory for government schemes. Follow this guide to check your status online or via SMS.

What is Aadhaar Seeding / Linking?

Aadhaar seeding or linking means connecting your 12-digit Aadhaar number with your bank account. This ensures that government subsidies, DBT payments, and other benefits are directly transferred to your bank account.

Why is it Important?

  • Direct benefit transfer (DBT) for subsidies and pensions.
  • Mandatory for schemes like PM Kisan, LPG subsidy, etc.
  • Prevents rejection of government payments.
  • Allows banks to maintain updated KYC records.

Methods to Check Aadhaar Linking Status

1. Online Method via Bank Portal / NPCI

Most banks provide a login portal or NPCI website to check if Aadhaar is linked. Follow these steps:

Step 1 – Visit Official Bank or NPCI Portal

Go to your bank’s official site or check NPCI official website for Aadhaar status checking.

Step 2 – Enter Bank Account Details

Enter your account number, IFSC code, and registered mobile number.

Step 3 – Enter Aadhaar Number

Input your 12-digit Aadhaar number to verify seeding status.

Step 4 – OTP Verification

An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number. Enter it to authenticate.

Step 5 – Check Status

The portal will show whether your Aadhaar is already linked, pending, or not linked.

2. SMS Method

All major banks allow checking Aadhaar linking via SMS. The general format:

  • Send UID(space)12-digit Aadhaar to a designated number (e.g., 567678 or bank specific number).
  • You will receive a reply showing the linking status.
  • Ensure SMS is sent from your registered mobile number.

3. Bank Branch / Customer Care

If online or SMS method fails, visit your bank branch or call customer care to verify Aadhaar seeding status.

Common Issues and How to Fix

  • Incorrect Aadhaar number: Recheck and re-submit at bank.
  • OTP not received: Ensure your mobile number is registered with bank and Aadhaar.
  • Pending verification: Bank may take 2–7 working days for processing.
  • Mismatch of details: Ensure your name, DOB, and gender match between Aadhaar and bank record.

FAQs

1. How long does Aadhaar linking take?

Typically 2–7 working days after submitting request at bank.

2. Is it mandatory to link Aadhaar with bank account?

Yes, for receiving government benefits, DBT, pensions, and subsidies.

3. Can I link multiple bank accounts?

Yes, but each account must be individually linked with Aadhaar.

4. How can I update bank details after linking?

Update details online via bank portal or visit bank branch with supporting documents.

5. Is there any fee for Aadhaar linking?

No, linking Aadhaar with bank account is free of cost.

Tips for Successful Linking

  • Use official bank / NPCI portals only.
  • Keep Aadhaar and bank details accurate.
  • Use registered mobile number to receive OTP.
  • Check status regularly until confirmed linked.
  • Maintain records of submission and confirmation messages.

Conclusion

Bank Aadhaar linking ensures smooth receipt of government payments. By following the above steps, you can check your linking status online or via SMS, resolve common issues, and ensure your bank account is properly seeded with Aadhaar.
