About Online Notepad

Online Notepad is a free and simple web tool that allows users to write, edit, and save notes directly in their browser. It works like a digital notebook where you can quickly type ideas, reminders, study notes, or important information. This tool is perfect for students, bloggers, writers, and professionals who need a fast and lightweight note-taking solution without installing any software.

The Online Notepad automatically saves your notes in your browser using local storage. This means your text remains available even if you refresh the page or close the browser. You can also download your notes as a TXT file for backup or sharing. Because it works completely online, you can use it on mobile phones, tablets, or desktops anytime.

How to Use the Online Notepad

  • Start typing your notes in the text area.
  • Click the Save Note button to store your notes in the browser.
  • If you want to remove everything, click the Clear button.
  • You can also download your notes using the Download TXT button.
  • The saved notes will automatically appear when you reopen the page.
