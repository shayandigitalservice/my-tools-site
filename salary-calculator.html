Understanding Salary Components

Your total salary includes various components. Knowing each one helps in accurate calculation and planning:

  • Basic Salary: The fixed portion of your salary.
  • HRA: House Rent Allowance, partially tax-exempt depending on rent.
  • Other Allowances: Conveyance, medical, special allowances, etc.
  • Deductions: Taxes, PF, insurance, or other contributions.
