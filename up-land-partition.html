Uttar Pradesh Land Partition Calculator: UP Mein Zameen Ka Sateek Batwara

Uttar Pradesh (UP) ek aisa prant hai jahan ki arthvyavastha mukhya roop se krishi (agriculture) par adharit hai. Yahan zameen sirf ek sampatti nahi, balki parivarik viraasat aur samman ka prateek hai. Lekin, jab parivarik batware (Family Partition) ki baat aati hai, toh aksar sateek mapi na hone ke karan bhaiyon aur hissedaro ke beech vivad paida ho jate hain.

UP Mein Land Partition (Batwara) Kyun Zaroori Hai?

Jab kisi buzurg ki mrityu hoti hai ya parivar bada ho jata hai, toh zameen ka kanooni taur par alag hona zaruri ho jata hai. Bina kanooni batware ke, hissedar apni zameen par loan nahi le sakte aur na hi use asani se bech sakte hain. UP mein batwara do tariko se hota hai:

  • Aapsi Sam सहमति (Mutual Consent): Jahan parivar ke log mil-baithkar sateek calculation ke saath zameen baant lete hain[span_2](start_span)[span_2](end_span). Hamara tool isme sabse bada sahayak hai.
  • Kanooni Batwara (Partition Suit): Jab vivad ho, toh SDM court mein case kiya jata hai[span_3](start_span)[span_3](end_span). Isme sateek mathematical data bohot kaam aata hai.

UP Land Laws aur SDM Court ki Bhumika

UP Revenue Code 2006 ke tahat, batware ka case Section 116 ke andar SDM court mein darj kiya jata hai. Is prakriya mein Tehsildar aur Patwari zameen ka 'Kurra' (khaka) taiyar karte hain. Hamara tool aapko pehle se hi sateek data de deta hai taaki Patwari ki report mein koi galti na ho sake.
