UP Land Partition: Uttar Pradesh Mein Zameen ka Sateek Batwara

Uttar Pradesh (UP) mein zameen ka batwara hamesha se ek gambhir mudda raha hai. UP Bhulekh records mein zameen aksar Hectare mein hoti hai, lekin gaon-dehat mein kisan use Bigha aur Biswa mein hi samajhte hain[span_0](start_span)[span_0](end_span). Jab ek parivar mein hissedaro ke beech zameen baantni ho, toh Hectare, Acre aur Bigha ko mix karke sateek hisab nikalna namumkin ho jata hai. **Shayan Smart Tools** ka ye **UP Mixed Land Partition Calculator** isi mushkil ko hal karne ke liye banaya gaya hai. Ye tool UP ke standard (1 Pucca Bigha = 27,000 Sq Ft) par adharit hai[span_1](start_span)[span_1](end_span)[span_2](start_span)[span_2](end_span).

How to Use (उपयोग कैसे करें?)

  1. Apne zameen ke kaagazat (Bhulekh/Registry) se Hectare, Acre ya Bigha ki value sahi boxes mein bhariye[span_3](start_span)[span_3](end_span)[span_4](start_span)[span_4](end_span).
  2. Hissedaro (shares) ki kul sankhya darj karein.
  3. Tool pehle sabhi units ko Square Feet mein badal kar jode-ga aur fir hissedaro mein barabar baant dega.
  4. Result aapko UP ke standard Bigha, Biswa aur Biswansi mein milega[span_5](start_span)[span_5](end_span).
