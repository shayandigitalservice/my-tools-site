West Bengal Land Partition: Accurate Jomir Hisab Guide

West Bengal mein zameen ka batwara (জমি ভাগ করা) ek bohot purani aur kanooni prakriya hai. Bengal mein zameen ki napai ke liye Bigha, Kottah, aur Chatak ka use kiya jata hai. Shayan Smart Tools ka ye **West Bengal Mixed Land Partition Calculator** vishesh roop se Bengal ke standard standards (1 Kottah = 720 Sq Ft) par adharit hai. Chahe aap Kolkata mein ho ya Siliguri mein, ye tool sateek hisab nikalne mein madad karega.

How to Use (ব্যবহারের নিয়ম)

  1. Zameen ke papers ke hisab se Bigha, Kottah, Acre ya Hectare sahi boxes mein darj karein.
  2. Hissedaro (Shares) ki sankhya enter karein.
  3. "Divide WB Land Now" button par click karte hi har person ka hissa screen par hoga.
  4. Result ko copy karke ya WhatsApp ke zariye family ke saath share karein.

5 Practical Examples: WB Mixed Batwara

1. **Joint Family:** 1 Bigha aur 5 Kottah zameen ko 2 bhaiyon mein barabar baantna.
2. **Registry Data:** 1 Hectare zameen ko 4 hissedaro mein Kottah tak sateek divide karna.
3. **Small Plot:** 10 Kottah zameen ko 3 logo mein Chatak tak barabar baantna.
4. **Mixed Units:** Acre aur Kottah ko jod kar hissedaro mein divide karna.
5. **Land Sale:** Badi zameen ka ek-tihaai hissa nikalne ke liye sateek calculation.
