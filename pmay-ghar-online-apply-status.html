PMAY Ghar Online Apply / Status Check 2026

Step by Step Official Guide

PMAY (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana) ke liye ghar online apply karna aur apna status check karna ab simple ho gaya hai. Ye guide aapko step by step official portal se apply aur status track karne ka process batayegi.

PMAY Kya Hai

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana ek government scheme hai jisme low aur middle income group ke liye affordable housing provide ki jati hai. Eligible applicants subsidy ke saath ghar purchase ya construction kar sakte hain.

Required Documents / Details

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank account details (passbook/IFSC)
  • Income Certificate
  • Residential Address Proof
  • Mobile Number
  • Passport size photo (optional)

Step 1 – Official PMAY Portal Open Kare

Browser me PMAY Official Portal open kare. Ye Government of India ka official portal hai.

Step 2 – Citizen Login / Registration Kare

“Citizen Assessment” ya “New User Registration” option select kare. Apna Aadhaar number aur mobile number verify kare.

Step 3 – Apply for PMAY Ghar

“Apply Online” section me jaake:

  • Scheme type select kare (CLSS / Beneficiary Led / In-situ)
  • Personal details, income & address fill kare
  • Bank account aur documents upload kare
  • Submit kare aur confirmation receipt download kare

Step 4 – PMAY Application ID Note Kare

Application submit hone ke baad ek unique Application ID milega. Is ID ko note kare future tracking ke liye.

Step 5 – PMAY Status Check Kaise Kare

“Citizen Assessment → Check Your Status” me jaake:

  • Application ID ya Aadhaar number enter kare
  • Status check kare – Pending / Approved / Subsidy Released
  • Receipt ya confirmation PDF save kare

Official PMAY Links

Important Tips

  • Official portal hi use kare
  • All documents correct aur latest ho
  • Application ID safe rakhe
  • Income aur residential proof accurately upload kare
  • Subsidy approval me time lag sakta hai, regular portal check kare

Benefits of PMAY Online Apply

  • Direct government scheme apply without agent
  • Track application status online
  • Secure and official process
  • Time save aur transparency
  • Digital receipt aur confirmation available

Conclusion

PMAY ghar online apply aur status check karna ab safe aur easy hai. Upar diye steps follow karke aap apna application successfully submit aur track kar sakte ho, aur government subsidy ka benefit le sakte ho.
