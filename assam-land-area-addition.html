Assam Land Area Addition: Zameen Ka Jod Kaise Nikalein?

Assam Dharitree Fard ke mutabik multiple plots ka kul rakba calculate karne ka sateek tareeka.

1. Introduction: Why Land Area Addition is Critical in Assam?

Assam mein zameen ki napai (Measurement) baaki Bharat ke rajyon se kafi alag hai. Yahan revenue records aur Dharitree Jamabandi portal mein area hamesha Hectare mein darj hota hai, lekin local star par sauda hamesha Bigha, Katha aur Lessa mein kiya jata hai. Ek kisan ya property buyer ke liye sabse badi chunauti tab aati hai jab unke paas ek se zyada plots hote hain aur unhe uska 'Kul Rakba' ya 'Total Jod' nikalna hota hai.

Assam government revenue manual ke anusar, zameen ka jod nikalna seedha math nahi hai. Yahan 1 Bigha mein 5 Katha hote hain aur 1 Katha mein 20 Lessa hote hain. Agar aap manual jod karte hain, toh galti ki sambhavna 90% hoti hai. Shayan Smart Tools ka yeh Assam Land Area Addition Calculator isi galti ko khatam karne ke liye banaya gaya hai. Yeh tool 5-Katha logic par kaam karta hai, jo Assam ka official standard hai.

2. How to Use Assam Land Area Addition Tool?

  1. Plot Input Details: Sabse pehle apne pehle plot (Plot #1) ka Bigha, Katha aur Lessa input boxes mein bharein.
  2. Add Multiple Plots: Agar aapke paas do se zyada plots hain, toh "+ Add Plot" button par click karke naye rows jodein.
  3. Calculation: Sari jankari bharne ke baad "Calculate Total Jod" button par click karein.
  4. Result: Tool automatic carry-forward logic apply karke aapko total Bigha, Katha aur Lessa ka sateek jod dikha dega.

4. Practical Calculation Logic (Assam Revenue Standards)

Assam Revenue system mein carry-forward logic bahut zaruri hai. Jaise hi 'Lessa' ki sankhya 20 hoti hai, wo automatic 1 'Katha' mein badal jata hai. Isi tarah 5 'Katha' hote hi wo 1 'Bigha' mein convert ho jata hai. Shayan Smart Tools ka engine isi mathematical rule (5-Katha Carry Forward) par kaam karta hai, jisse aapko har bar manual conversion nahi karna padta.
