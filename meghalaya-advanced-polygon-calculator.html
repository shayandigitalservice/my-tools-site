Irregular polygon plots ka sateek framework map evaluate karne ka sabse ancient aur globally certified system hai bada asset patch ko chhote independent right/irregular triangles me convert kar dena. 5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Ki Jingkylli Ba Ju Kylli Bha (Khasi Language)

Q1. Kiei ki standard units ba pyndonkam ha Meghalaya ban khein ia ka jingjngai/jingheh khyndew?

Q2. Ha kano ka unit ba pynskhem ia ka jingheh khyndew ha ki dkhot/kot Patta ki Autonomous District Councils?
Ans: Ha KHADC, JHADC, bad GHADC dkhot revenue records, ia ka jingheh khyndew barabor pynkynriah bad pynskhem ha ki Acres, Square Yards, bad Square Feet metrics.

Q3. Kumno ban kylla (convert) Hectare sha Acre katkum ki rukom khein Meghalaya?
Ans: 1 Hectare ka ryngkat bad 2.4711 Acres. Ban kylla ia ka Hectare sha Acre, pynkylla da kaba multiply ia ka Hectare value da 2.4711.

Q4. Katno ki Square Yards (Sq Yds) ba don ha kawei ka Acre ha Meghalaya?
Ans: Katkum ki rukom survey baroh kawei ka Acre ka don 4,840 Square Yards. Aiei ba pyndonkam ha ki cadastral surveys bad master planning.

Q5. Katno ka jingheh bigha katkum ka metric standard ne Assam-Meghalaya border standards?
Ans: Ha ki jaka ba pyniasoh ha border bad flat spaces, 1 Bigha ka katkum 14,400 Square Feet standard area context.

Q6. Balei ba pyndonkam ia ka Heron's Formula ha kaba khein ia ki khyndew bym beit (irregular plots)?

Q8. Katno ki Square Meters ba don ha kawei ka Hectare?
Ans: Katkum ka global metric benchmark scale, 1 Hectare ka katkum 10,000 Square Meters.

Q9. Bad kaia ka jingiasoh ha pyngkat Square Yard bad Square Feet ha Meghalaya?
Ans: 1 Square Yard ka katkum 9 Square Feet. Aiei ba pyndonkam bha ha ki urban building permissions bad municipal regulatory approvals.
