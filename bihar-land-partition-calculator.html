Dumdar Intro: Bihar Land Partition Tool

Bihar mein zameen ka batwara (Land Partition) hamesha se ek gambhir aur kanooni mudda raha hai. Parivarik batware mein aksar Dhur aur Kattha ke hisab ko lekar choti si galti bade vivad ka karan ban jati hai. Is samasya ko hal karne ke liye **Shayan Smart Tools** ne ye sateek **Bihar Land Partition Calculator** banaya hai. Ye tool Bihar ke standard land units (1 Bigha = 27,225 Sq Ft) ka upyog karke har hissedar ka barabar aur sateek hissa nikalta hai.

How to Use (बंटवारा कैसे करें?)

  1. Sabse pehle apne plot ka total area Square Feet mein calculator mein darj karein.
  2. Hissedaro (shares) ki kul sankhya enter karein.
  3. "Calculate" button par click karein.
  4. Result mein aapko har vyakti ka hissa Bigha, Kattha aur Dhur mein mil jayega.

5 Examples of Land Batwara in Bihar

1. **Equal Partition:** 1 Bigha zameen ko 4 bhaiyon mein baantna (Har kisi ko 5 Kattha).
2. **Small Area:** 2 Kattha zameen ko 3 hissedaro mein sateek Dhur tak divide karna.
3. **Partner Exit:** Jab koi partner apna 1/3rd hissa lekar nikalna chahe.
4. **Complex Ratio:** 15,000 sq ft ko 2 logo mein barabar divide karna.
5. **Hectare Share:** Sarkari records ke hectare ko feet mein badal kar hissedaro mein baantna.
