Bihar Land Partition Tool: Managing Mixed Units

Bihar mein zameen ki registry aksar Hectare mein hoti hai, lekin parivarik batwara hamesha Bigha aur Kattha mein kiya jata hai. Agar aapke paas aisi zameen hai jiska kuch hissa Hectare mein hai, kuch Acre mein aur kuch Bigha mein, toh use barabar baantna bohot mushkil ho jata hai. **Shayan Smart Tools** ka ye mixed unit calculator isi mushkil ko hal karta hai. Ye tool Bihar ke standard (1 Bigha = 27,225 Sq Ft) ka upyog karke sateek calculation deta hai.

How to Use (उपयोग कैसे करें?)

  1. Apne paas maujood zameen ka data (Hectare, Acre, Bigha etc.) sahi boxes mein bhariye.
  2. Hissedaro (shares) ki sankhya enter karein.
  3. Tool sabhi units ko pehle Square Feet mein jode-ga aur phir barabar divide kare-ga.
  4. Result aapko Bigha-Kattha-Dhur mein milega.
