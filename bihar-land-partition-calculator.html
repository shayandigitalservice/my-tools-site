Bihar Mixed Land Partition Tool: Sateek Batwara aur Sahi Hisab

Bihar mein zameen ki napai (Land Measurement) ek bohot hi pechida (complex) kaam hai. Aksar parivarik batware ya registry ke waqt hum dekhte hain ki zameen ka hisab alag-alag units mein hota hai. Jaise ki kisi ke paas **5 Bigha, 3 Kattha aur 1 Dhur** zameen hai, toh kisi aur ke paas **1 Hectare, 4 Acre aur kuch Bigha** ka mishrit (mixed) area hai. Aisi sthiti mein sabko barabar baantna bina kisi calculator ke namumkin hai.

**Shayan Smart Tools** ka ye **Bihar Mixed Unit Partition Calculator** isi mushkil ko asan karne ke liye banaya gaya hai. Ye tool Bihar ke standard (1 Bigha = 27,225 Sq Ft) aur sarkari Hectare to Sq Ft conversion ka upyog karke har hissedar ka sateek hissa nikalta hai.

How to Use (उपयोग कैसे करें?)

Is tool ka upyog karna bohot hi asan hai, bas niche diye gaye steps ko follow karein:

  1. Input Mixed Values: Aapke paas jitni bhi zameen hai, uska Hectare, Acre, Bigha, Kattha ya Dhur sateek boxes mein bhariye.
  2. Number of Shares: Kul kitne logo mein batwara karna hai (jaise 2 bhai ya 5 hissedar), wo sankhya darj karein.
  3. Calculate: Button par click karte hi calculator sabhi units ko pehle Square Feet mein jode-ga aur phir usey divide karke result **Bigha-Kattha-Dhur** mein dikha dega.
