Bihar Mixed Land Partition Calculator: Sateek Batwara aur Sahi Hisab

Bihar mein zameen ki napai (Land Measurement) ek bohot hi pechida (complex) kaam hai. Aksar parivarik batware ya registry ke waqt hum dekhte hain ki zameen ka hisab alag-alag units mein hota hai. Jaise ki kisi ke paas **5 Bigha, 3 Kattha aur 1 Dhur** zameen hai, toh kisi aur ke paas **1 Hectare, 4 Acre aur 5 Bigha** ka mishrit (mixed) area hai. Aisi sthiti mein sabko barabar baantna bina kisi professional calculator ke namumkin hai.

**Shayan Smart Tools** ka ye **Bihar Mixed Unit Partition Calculator** isi mushkil ko asan karne ke liye banaya gaya hai. Ye tool Bihar ke standard (1 Bigha = 27,225 Sq Ft) aur sarkari Hectare to Sq Ft conversion ka upyog karke har hissedar ka sateek hissa nikalta hai. Bihar ke kisanon ke liye ye batwara ab ungliyon par hoga.

Bihar Mein Land Partition ki Zaroorat Kyun?

Bihar ke gaon-dehat ho ya Patna jaise sheher, zameen ke batware ko lekar aksar parivaron mein vivad hota hai. Iska mukhya karan hai units ka sahi gyaan na hona. Jab hum Hectare se Bigha mein badalte hain, toh decimal points ki wajah se dhur aur dhurki mein fark aa jata hai. Ek choti si galti bhi lakhon ki zameen ka nuksan kar sakti hai. Hamara tool complex algorithms ka use karke pehle sabhi mixed units ko Square Feet mein badalta hai, fir hissedaro mein divide karta hai, aur ant mein aapko wahi result deta hai jo Bihar ke kisan samajhte hain.
