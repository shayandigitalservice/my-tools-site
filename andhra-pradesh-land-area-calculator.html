Andhra Pradesh Land Measurement: Ankanam, Gajam and Cent Guide

Andhra Pradesh (AP) mein zameen ki napai (భూమి కొలతలు) ke liye bohot hi purana aur sateek system chalta hai. Coastal Andhra mein **Ankanam** bohot lokpriya hai, jabki Rayalaseema aur shehri ilakon mein **Gajam (Square Yards)** aur **Cent** ka upyog kiya jata hai.
