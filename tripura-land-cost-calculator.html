3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Tripura Land Registry

Q1. Tripura me 1 Kani kitne Square Feet ke barabar hota hai? (১ কানি কত স্কয়ার ফুট?)
Ans: Tripura revenue unit measurement rule ke mutabiq 1 Kani (১ কানি) = 17,280 Square Feet hota hai. Yeh 20 Ganda ke barabar hota hai.

Q2. Tripura me land registration stamp duty kitna percent hai 2026 me?
Ans: Tripura me standard base Stamp Duty rate 5% hai aur Registration Fee flat 2% hai. Urban municipal areas me 1% additional local infrastructure surcharge lagta hai.

Q3. 1 Ganda me kitne Sq Ft hote hain Tripura me? (১ গণ্ডা = কত স্কয়ার ফুট?)
Ans: Tripura land measurement matrix ke anusar 1 Ganda (১ গণ্ডা) = 864 Square Feet hota hai (17,280 / 20).

Q4. Tripura online land record Khatian/Khasra kaise check karein?
Ans: Tripura Govt ki official Land Records portal 'Jarki Tripura' (jarki.tripura.gov.in) par jakar ROR, Khatian number aur land status online check kar sakte hain.

Q5. 1 Kranta me kitne Sq Ft hote hain? (১ ক্রান্তা = কত বর্গফুট?)
Ans: Tripura local land metric system me 1 Kranta = 288 Square Feet hota hai. 1 Ganda me 3 Kranta hote hain.

Q6. Agartala Municipal Corporation (AMC) area me extra registry charge lagta hai kya?
Ans: Haan, Agartala samet sabhi Tripura Urban / Municipal limits ke andar 1% local body development infrastructure surcharge (cess) extra charge hota hai.

Q7. 1 Acre me kitne Kani hote hain Tripura me?
Ans: 1 Acre (43,560 Sq.Ft) me Tripura metric scale ke mutabiq exactly 2.525 Kani (প্রায় ২.৫২৫ কানি) hote hain.

Q8. Land Registration valuation Government Circle Rate par calculate hoti hai ya Market Price par?
Ans: Government Circle Rate (Guideline Value) ya actual Agreement Market Value — jo bhi dono me se higher hoga, Stamp Duty aur Registration Fee usi value par calculate hogi.

Q9. Tripura me Jarki Portal se Mutation Charge calculate kar sakte hain?
Ans: Jarki portal ROR records aur land title trace karta hai. Final Stamp Duty & Registration calculation ke liye hamara calculator exact 2026 slabs provide karta hai.

Q10. Stamp Duty aur Registration Fee online pay ki ja sakti hai Tripura me?
Ans: Haan, e-Stamping aur e-GRAS portal integration ke zariye digital payment modes, e-Stamp certificates aur online treasury challan safe process hote hain.

Q11. Rural (Gramin) areas me kitna registry budget aayega?
Ans: Rural Tripura areas me 5% Base Stamp Duty + 2% Registration Fee = Total 7% Government Fee lagti hai (Gramin area me 1% Urban Cess nahi lagta).

Q12. Sub-Registrar Office me Kon-kon se documents le jana mandatory hai?
Ans: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Drafted Sale Deed, Jarki Khatian Copy (ROR), Stamp Duty Payment Receipt, aur Passport Size Photos mandatory hain.

Q13. Joint Property purchase me Registration fee me split discount milta hai?
Ans: General Tripura registry acts ke tehat registration 2% aur stamp duty standard percent hi rehti hai, ownership title deed me multiple buyers record kiye jaate hain.

Q14. Local Surcharge / Cess calculation automatic calculate hota hai?
Ans: Ji haan, Calculator me jab aap 'Urban Zone' Select karenge tab 1% Cess automatically add ho jayega.

Q15. Calculator ka output pdf ya print form me download kar sakte hain?
Ans: Haan, niche diye gaye 'Print' button par click karke aap official verification seal ke sath A4 PDF / Hard copy print nikal sakte hain.
