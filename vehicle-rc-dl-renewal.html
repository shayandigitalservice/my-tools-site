What is Vehicle RC & DL Renewal?

RC renewal means updating your vehicle registration certificate before expiry or after transfer of ownership. DL renewal means updating your driving license before expiry to continue driving legally.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Vehicle RC: Vehicle must be registered and not more than 15 years old (for private vehicles, state dependent).
  • DL Renewal: Existing Driving License must be valid or expired within renewal grace period.
  • Applicant must be physically fit for driving.
  • Registered mobile number and Aadhaar linked for verification.

Required Documents

  • Vehicle RC copy / DL copy
  • Form 26 / Form 9 (as per RTO requirement)
  • Address proof – Aadhaar / Passport / Utility Bill
  • Identity proof – Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport
  • Medical certificate for DL renewal (if applicable)
  • Insurance certificate & Pollution Certificate (for RC renewal)

Step by Step Guide to Renew Vehicle RC Online

Step 1 – Visit Official Parivahan / State RTO Portal

👉 Official Parivahan Sewa Portal

Select your state and go to “Vehicle Registration / RC Renewal” section.

Step 2 – Enter Vehicle Details

Input vehicle registration number, chassis number, engine number, and owner details.

Step 3 – Upload Documents

Upload RC copy, insurance certificate, pollution certificate, and address proof. Ensure clarity and proper format.

Step 4 – Pay Renewal Fees

Pay fees online via net banking, UPI, credit card, or debit card.

Step 5 – Schedule Vehicle Inspection

Book appointment for physical verification at RTO if required.

Step 6 – Submit Application

Review details and submit application. Save Application Reference Number (ARN) for tracking.

Step 7 – Receive Renewed RC

After approval, updated RC is sent to your address or can be downloaded digitally.

Step by Step Guide to Renew Driving License Online

Step 1 – Visit Official Parivahan / State RTO Portal

Select “Driving License / DL Renewal” section.

Step 2 – Fill Renewal Form

Enter DL number, date of birth, address, and contact details.

Step 3 – Upload Documents

Upload DL copy, medical certificate, and address proof.

Step 4 – Pay Renewal Fee

Fees vary by state. Pay online using official portal payment gateway.

Step 5 – Book RTO Appointment

Schedule appointment for verification / photograph update if required.

Step 6 – Submit Application & Verification

Submit form online and visit RTO for verification. Check ARN for updates.

Step 7 – Receive Renewed DL

Updated DL is issued digitally and can be downloaded from portal / DigiLocker.

Common Issues and Fixes

  • OTP not received – Ensure mobile number linked with Aadhaar.
  • Document rejected – Upload clear and correct document.
  • Portal down – Try during non-peak hours.
  • Verification pending – Allow 2–7 working days for RTO processing.

FAQs

1. Can I renew RC and DL together?

Yes, but submit separate forms for vehicle and driving license.

2. How long is DL renewal valid?

Typically 20 years or until age 50, state dependent.

3. Can I use digital RC / DL?

Yes, digital RC / DL is valid via Parivahan portal or DigiLocker.

4. Is there any fee for online renewal?

Yes, fees vary by state and vehicle type.

5. What if application is rejected?

Check reason, correct documents, and resubmit online.

Tips for Successful RC / DL Renewal

  • Use official Parivahan portal only.
  • Keep Aadhaar and mobile number updated.
  • Upload clear scanned documents.
  • Pay fees via official payment gateway.
  • Track application status regularly using ARN.
