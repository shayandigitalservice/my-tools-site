3. Mandatory Documents Checklist for Land Registry in Chhattisgarh

CG IGRS registry process sub-registrar office me securely finalise karne ke liye khariddaar (buyer) aur bechne wale (seller) ke paas niche diye gaye valid certificates aur reports hone anivarya hain:

  • Bhuiyan Portal Documents: Digitally signed **Khasra (B1)** and **P-II Khationi** copy jisse land ownership and classification validity trace hoti hai.
  • Official Land Map (नक्शा): Revenue department ਦੁਆਰਾ verified map copy tracking grid sheets showing layout structures.
  • Collector Guideline Certificate: Region ke standard valuation verification benchmark sheet copy.
  • Identity Proof Verification: Dono party (Buyer, Seller & Witness) ka Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, ya Voter ID documents.
  • Property Diversion Certificate: Agar zameen agricultural se residential/commercial purpose layout zone me badli gayi ho, toh Diversion clearance log receipt.
  • No-Objection Certificate (NOC): Mining parameters or industrial zoning areas rule check specifications limits logs clear documents sheet.

5. 15 Important Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Chhattisgarh Land Records & Rate Dynamics

Q1. Kya Chhattisgarh mein zameen ko napne ke liye official records me Hectare standard validation use hota hai?
Ans: Haan, Chhattisgarh Revenue and Bhuiyan tracking systems records data layers me zameen ka structural management strictly Hectare map standard mapping layers par hi store kiya jata hai.

Ans: Urban development zones ke anusaar property transactions me Municipal Corporations development balance layout banaye rakhne ke liye 1% Extra Infrastructure Cess apply hota hai jo rural plots me standard zero baseline map parameters par chalta hai.

Q3. Chhattisgarh land registration processing limits rules ke tehat baseline exemption metric kya hai?
Ans: Sub-registrar guidelines standard matrix layout updates ke mutabik registration fee configuration calculate karte waqt pure land value balance pricing threshold me se dynamic ₹50,000 threshold relaxation rule execution automatically active hota hai.

Q4. Kyun Chhattisgarh mein district-wise land rates achanak badal jaate hain?
Ans: Infrastructure development density, industrial facilities presence, commercial activity hubs, aur National Highway/Rail connectivity indicators badalne ki wajah se Collector Guideline and Market rates har district mein bilkul alag ho jaate hain.

Q5. Chhattisgarh mein online land record system details track karne ke liye kaun sa web application link use kiya jata hai?
Ans: Chhattisgarh Revenue department ka official portal Bhuiyan (bhuiyan.cg.nic.in) map layout layers aur B1-Khasra tracking data management handle karta hai.

Q6. Chhattisgarh mein sabse high land prices valuation limits kis belt mein active hain?
Ans: Economic corridors aur dense state capital infrastructure zones hone ke chalte Raipur Urban clusters, Naya Raipur (Atal Nagar), aur Durg-Bhilai transit metrics zone mein price curves highest hain.

Q7. Kya Raipur industrial layout zones aur Bastar tribal zones ke valuation rates barabar hain?
Ans: Nahi, Bastar divisional belts eco-protected forest corridors hone aur tribal ownership protection rules hone se wahan commercial market valuation minimal hai, jabki Raipur metallic centers aur commercial corridors rates exponential hain.
