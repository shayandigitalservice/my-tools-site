1. Understanding Property Registry & Evaluation Rules in Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh me Raipur, Bilaspur, Durg, Bhilai, ya Bastar jaise kshetron me zameen kharidte waqt total registry budget and stamp values compute karna bohot zaroori hota hai. Government of Chhattisgarh ka Commercial Tax Department (Registration and Stamps) and Revenue Department zameen ki sarkari rate (Collector Guideline Rate) ke mutabik registry structures ko deploy karta hai.

Is tool ko Chhattisgarh ke dynamic matrix laws ke tehat calibrate kiya gaya hai jahan Hectare, Acre aur Dismil (डिसमिल) me calculations hoti hain. Mahilaon (Female buyers) ke liye stamp duty parameters me vishesh chhoot ka provison bhi isme dynamically auto-process hota hai taaki calculations me koi galti na ho.
