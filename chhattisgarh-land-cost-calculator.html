1. Property Registry Rates in Chhattisgarh (2026)

Chhattisgarh me Raipur, Bilaspur, Durg, Bhilai ya Bastar jaise regions me jab aap kisi plot ya zameen ki registry karwate hain, toh government taxes calculation standard rules par work karti hain. Chhattisgarh Government me Stamp duty structural base format uniform **5.00%** rakha jata hai, lekin Rural Gram Panchayat me **1% Additional Janpad Upkar (Duty)** add ho jata hai.

Registration fee Chhattisgarh me flat **4.00%** lagti hai jo baaki states ki tulna me thodi zyada hai. Hamara dynamic calculator in saare factors ko automated mode me breakdown karke detailed reporting sheet generate karta hai.
