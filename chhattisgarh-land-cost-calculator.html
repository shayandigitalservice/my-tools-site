5. 15 Important Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Chhattisgarh Land Records & Rate Dynamics

Q1. Kya Chhattisgarh mein zameen ko napne ke liye Bigha aur Katha ka use hota hai?
Ans: Haan, Chhattisgarh ke local/rural areas (jaise Surguja aur Bilaspur divisions) mein zameen ka calculation Bigha aur Katha ke adhar par kiya jata hai, halanki sarkari nakshon mein Hectare ka prayog hota hai.

Q2. Chhattisgarh mein 1 Hectare mein kitne Bigha calculate hote hain?
Ans: Chhattisgarh state regional standard metrics ke anusar 1 Hectare mein lagbhag 2.47 Bigha area system validation scale chalta hai.

Q3. Chhattisgarh land conversion criteria mein 1 Bigha mein kitne Katha hote hain?
Ans: Chhattisgarh state division parameters verification guidelines ke tehat 1 Bigha strictly 20 Katha map boundary coordinates ko represent karta hai.

Q4. Kyun Chhattisgarh mein district-wise land rates achanak badal jaate hain?
Ans: Infrastructure development density, industrial facilities presence, commercial activity hubs, aur National Highway/Rail connectivity indicators badalne ki wajah se Collector Guideline and Market rates har district mein bilkul alag ho jaate hain.

Q5. Chhattisgarh mein online land record system details track karne ke liye kaun sa web application link use kiya jata hai?
Ans: Chhattisgarh Revenue department ka official portal Bhuiyan (bhuiyan.cg.nic.in) map layout layers aur B1-Khasra tracking data management handle karta hai.

Q6. Chhattisgarh mein sabse high land prices valuation limits kis belt mein active hain?
Ans: Economic corridors aur dense state capital infrastructure zones hone ke chalte Raipur Urban clusters, Naya Raipur (Atal Nagar), aur Durg-Bhilai transit metrics zone mein price curves highest hain.

Q7. Kya Raipur industrial layout zones aur Bastar tribal zones ke valuation rates barabar hain?
Ans: Nahi, Bastar divisional belts eco-protected forest corridors hone aur tribal ownership protection rules hone se wahan commercial market valuation minimal hai, jabki Raipur metallic centers aur commercial corridors rates exponential hain.

Q8. Collector Guideline Rate kya hota hai aur iska registry process se kya taluk hai?
Ans: Collector Guideline Rate state revenue registry department dwara design kiya gaya woh safe minimum benchmark evaluation threshold matrix hai, jisse niche values par properties mutation process register nahi ho sakti.

Q9. Chhattisgarh mein 1 Katha mapping area measurement square feet metrics framework kya hold karta hai?
Ans: Standard computational evaluation metrics scale calculations standard ke tehat 1 standard regional Katha structure near about 1,360 Square Feet horizontal area plot layout matrix hold karta hai.

Q10. Korba aur Raigarh districts ke prices valuation trends kis parameter par setup hote hain?
Ans: Korba aur Raigarh districts heavily heavy coal block energy generation reserves aur large steel setups plants structural clusters par base karte hain, isliye wahan industrial real estate layout pricing trend high setup rehta hai.

Q11. Bhuiyan portal records database structure mein land plot allocation validation map signature form mein trace kar sakte hain?
Ans: Ji haan, Bhuiyan online network service par user dynamic secure channel se digitally signed copies of Khata B1 aur digital nakshe online tracking output download kar sakte hain.

Q12. Chhattisgarh mein diversion laws (bhoomi vyavsayik parivartan) registry rules criteria kya hain?
Ans: Agricultural zameen ko residential ya corporate infrastructure processing scale par convert karne ke liye land owners ko premium tax metrics framework sub-registrar system verification updates rules compliance check karke apply karna hota hai.

Q13. Southern remote units jaise Sukma, Bijapur, aur Narayanpur mein acre valuations itni niche kyun hain?
Ans: Remote terrain landscapes, non-industrial expansion corridors, environmental forest ecosystem zones aur standard infrastructure development updates missing hone ke chalte sarkari boundary line validation indexes kafi low margin par execute hote hain.

Q14. Kya market transactions actual cash flows value guideline metrics threshold values se lower ho sakti hain?
Ans: Final physical deals customer levels par market factors base par lower level balance hold kar sakti hain par legal registry rules protocols ke dynamic enforcement layers ke tehat stamp calculation guideline standard baseline scale par hi complete karna mandatory hota hai.

Q15. Chhattisgarh mining zones areas borders property transactions checks kaise trace hote hain?
Ans: Kisi bhi layout conversion parameter system trace updates ko strictly revenue board aur district environment mining department clearance tracking matrix pass logs system verify karne ke baad hi registration ledger system validation grant kiya jata hai.
