1. Understanding Property Registry & Evaluation Rules in Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh me Raipur, Bilaspur, Durg, Bhilai, ya Korba jaise shehron ya rural gram panchayats me zameen kharidte waqt total land budget compute karna ek complex process hota hai. Government of Chhattisgarh ka Registration and Stamps Department zameen ki official evaluation cycle rate (Circle Rate) ke base par stamp duty fix karta hai. Agar aap bina proper assessment ke market deal finalize karte hain, toh registry ke samay unexpected high tax slab charges aa sakte hain.

Is tool ko Chhattisgarh ke dynamic matrix laws ke rules compliance checks ke tehat banaya gaya hai. Chhattisgarh me Dismil (डिसमिल), Acre (एकड़), aur Bigha (बीघा) zameen napne ke sabse lokpriya tarike hain. Hamara tool in local units ko automatic square feet processing engine me normalize karta hai, jisse human calculations me error ka khatra poori tarah khatam ho jata hai.
