2. Understanding the Concept of Collector Guideline Rate (Circle Rate)

Chhattisgarh me kisi bhi zameen ka sarkari rate, jise "Collector Guideline Rate" ya "Circle Rate" kaha jata hai, har saal district collector ki samiti dwara nirdharit kiya jata hai. Yeh rate har area, ward, aur khet ke survey number (Khasra) ke mutabik alag-alag hota hai. Government registry hamesha is baat par zor deti hai ki stamp duty calculation hamesha guideline rate ya fir actual selling market agreement rate (dono me se jo bhi zyada ho) par hi nikali jayegi. Agar aapne zameen market rate se saste me bhi khareedi hai, tab bhi aapko sarkari tax guideline rate par hi dena hoga. Isiliye, is calculator ka upyog karte samay hamesha apne area ki correct guideline value hi input box me enter karein taaki calculations 100% accurate rahein.

4. Exhaustive Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - 15 Comprehensive Structural Answers

Q1: Chhattisgarh me male buyer ke liye total zameen registry kharcha kitna percent hota hai?
Ans: Chhattisgarh me male buyer ke liye base stamp duty 5% hoti hai aur registration fee flat 4% hoti hai. Agar zameen urban kshetra me aati hai toh 1% extra cess judkar kul kharcha total property evaluation ka 10% ho jata hai, aur rural area me yeh kul 9% rehta hai.
Q2: Kya Chhattisgarh me mahilaon ko registry me koi tax concession milta hai?
Ans: Haan, mahila sashaktikaran ko badhava dene ke liye state government mahilaon (Female Buyers) ko base stamp duty me seedhe 1% ki chhoot deti hai. Unke liye base stamp duty 5% ke bajay sirf 4% lagti hai.
Q3: Urban area me zameen khareedne par extra surcharge kyu lagaya jata hai?
Ans: Chhattisgarh Municipal Corporation Rules ke anusar urban local bodies ke antargat aane wali zameen par shahar ke vikas aur infrastructure maintenance ke liye 1% ka additional cess ya nagariya upkar surcharge lagaya jata hai.
Q4: Rural area me zameen registry karate samay cess kitna lagta hai?
Ans: Gramin (Rural) kshetron me kisaano aur gramwasiyon ko rahat dene ke liye sarkari niyam ke tehat additional upkar surcharge bilkul zero (0%) hota hai. Is kshetra me koi extra cess nahi liya jata.
Q5: Agar main zameen Guideline Rate se saste me khareedu toh tax kis par dena hoga?
Ans: Chhattisgarh Revenue Rules ke mutabik registry tax hamesha Collector Guideline Rate ya fir Actual Market Agreement Price (dono me se jo bhi sabse zyada ho) par hi nikala jata hai. Agar aapne saste me bhi deal ki hai toh bhi tax guideline rate par hi banta hai.
Q6: Chhattisgarh me registration fee kitni hai aur kya isme chhoot milti hai?
Ans: Chhattisgarh me zameen ki property registration fee flat 4% nirdharit hai. Is fees me kisi bhi gender (male/female) ya kshetra (urban/rural) ke aadhar par koi chhoot nahi milti, yeh sabhi ke liye ek saman hai.
Q7: 1 Chhattisgarh Decimal me kitne Square Feet hote hain?
Ans: Chhattisgarh Land Revenue records ke standard measurements ke anusaar, ek Decimal (jise local bhasha me disamil bhi kehte hain) me exact 435.6 Square Feet land area hota hai.
Q8: Chhattisgarh me 1 Bigha zameen ka standard size kya hota hai?
Ans: State revenue benchmarks ke anusar Chhattisgarh me 1 standard Bigha zameen ka kul aakar 27,225 Square Feet ke barabar map kiya jata hai.
Q9: Ek Katha zameen me kitne square feet hote hain CG me?
Ans: Chhattisgarh me ek standard Katha ka maap 1,361.25 Square Feet nirdharit kiya gaya hai, jo ki aamtaur par ek bigha ka 20wa hissa (1/20) hota hai.
Q10: Registry slots aur online document draft submit karne ke liye official portal konsa hai?
Ans: Chhattisgarh me online stamp valuation, guidelines search aur registry data entry slot booking ke liye official website CG E-Panjiyaan (https://epanjiatest.cg.nic.in/) hai.
Q11: Bhuiyan CG website ka mukhya kaam kya hai registry process me?
Ans: Bhuiyan CG (https://bhuiyan.cg.nic.in/) Chhattisgarh sarkar ka digital land record system hai jahan se aap zameen ka Khasra, Khatauni, B-1 map nikalte hain taaki pata chale ki zameen ka asli malik kon hai aur property par koi vivad toh nahi hai.
Q12: Joint Holder registration karne par kitna percent stamp duty lagti hai?
Ans: Agar registry me do purush ya ek purush aur ek mahila joint holder hain, toh registry standard base slab ke mutabik 5% stamp duty par hi process ki jayegi. Mahila ko chhoot tabhi milti hai jab property unke akele ke naam par ho.
Q13: Kya is calculator ka data sub-registrar office me directly final mana jayega?
Ans: Yeh calculator ek automated advanced parsing algorithm tool hai jo accurate estimates pradan karta hai. Lekin registration office me micro levels par structural internal elements ya anya minor taxes ke kaaran final digital receipt me sateek rashi thodi badal sakti hai.
Q14: Registry ho jane ke baad Dakhil-Kharij (Mutation) kab karwana chahiye?
Ans: Sub-registrar office se successfully registry deed milne ke turant baad aapko Bhuiyan portal ke madhyam se ya Tehsildar office me Namantaran (Mutation) ke liye online apply kar dena chahiye taaki sarkari revenue records me aapka naam charh sake.
Q15: Kya Collector guideline rate har saal badalte hain?
Ans: Haan, market growth, corporate industrial developments aur infrastructure pipelines ko dhyan me rakhte hue District Collector ki adhyakshata wali committee har saal naye guideline circulars aur circle rates jaari karti hai.
