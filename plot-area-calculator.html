Land area calculation is very important in agriculture, real estate, and property management. Farmers need to know the exact size of their fields to plan crops and irrigation. Land Area Units Used in the Calculator

The plot area calculator converts the land area into several common land measurement units. These include:

Square Feet (Sq Ft)

Square feet is one of the most widely used land measurement units in real estate and residential property transactions. Small plots and houses are often measured in square feet.

Square Meter (Sq M)

Square meters are used in the metric system and are commonly used internationally for land measurement and property documentation.

Katha

Katha is a traditional land measurement unit used mainly in rural areas of India, Nepal, and Bangladesh. Farmers often use this unit when describing farmland.

Bigha

Bigha is another traditional unit used in agricultural land measurement. The size of a bigha may vary depending on the region.

Acre

An acre is a widely recognized international unit for measuring large areas of land. One acre equals 43,560 square feet.
