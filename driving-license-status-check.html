Driving License Status Kaise Check Kare Online 2026

DL Application Status Check Karne Ka Complete Guide

Driving license India me vehicle chalane ke liye ek important document hai. Agar aapne driving license ke liye apply kiya hai to aap online apna DL status check kar sakte ho.

Government ne Parivahan portal launch kiya hai jahan se aap driving license application status easily check kar sakte ho.

Driving License Status Kya Hota Hai

Driving license status ka matlab hai aapki DL application ki current progress. Isse pata chalta hai ki license approve hua hai ya abhi processing me hai.

  • Application received
  • Under processing
  • Approved
  • Dispatched

Driving License Status Check Karne Ke Liye Required Details

  • Application number
  • Date of birth
  • Internet connection

Driving License Status Online Kaise Check Kare

Step 1

Sabse pehle official website open kare:

Parivahan Official Website

Step 2

Driving License Related Services option select kare.

Step 3

Apna state select kare.

Step 4

Application Status option par click kare.

Step 5

Application number aur date of birth enter kare aur submit kare.

Driving License Status Check Karne Ke Benefits

  • Application progress pata chal jata hai
  • License dispatch status check kar sakte hain
  • Delay ka reason pata chal sakta hai

Driving License Delivery Time

Normally driving license apply karne ke baad 2–4 weeks me DL deliver ho jata hai.
