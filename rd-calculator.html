About This RD Calculator

The RD Calculator (Recurring Deposit Calculator) is a free online financial tool that helps you calculate the maturity amount of your recurring deposit investment. Recurring deposits are popular savings options offered by banks where you deposit a fixed amount every month and earn interest on it. This calculator helps you instantly estimate how much your investment will grow over time.

Instead of manually calculating the maturity value using complicated formulas, this RD calculator makes the process simple. You only need to enter your monthly deposit amount, the interest rate offered by the bank, and the investment period in months. The calculator will automatically show the maturity amount.

How to Use the RD Calculator

  • Enter the monthly deposit amount you plan to invest.
  • Enter the annual interest rate offered by the bank.
  • Enter the time period in months.
  • Click the Calculate button.
  • The calculator will instantly display the RD maturity amount.
