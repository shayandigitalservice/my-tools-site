Aadhaar Update Kya Hai?

Aadhaar update means correcting your personal details such as Address, Gender, or Date of Birth in your Aadhaar record. Accurate information is crucial for government schemes, KYC, and online services.

Why Online Update is Useful?

  • Save time – no need to visit a center.
  • Secure – uses OTP verification.
  • Quick processing – portal handles request efficiently.
  • Trackable – request status available online.

Required Documents

  • Address update: Utility Bill, Passport, Bank Statement, or other valid address proof.
  • Gender update: Passport, Government ID showing correct gender.
  • DOB update: Birth certificate, Passport, or School Certificate.
  • Registered mobile number active to receive OTP.

Step by Step Guide to Update Aadhaar Online

Step 1 – Open UIDAI Official Portal

👉 Official Aadhaar Update Portal

Click the above link to open the UIDAI self-service portal for Aadhaar updates.

Step 2 – Enter Aadhaar Number

Input your 12-digit Aadhaar number in the required field and solve the CAPTCHA to verify you are human.

Step 3 – OTP Verification

An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP correctly to proceed.

Step 4 – Select Update Type

Choose the field you want to update: Address, Gender, or Date of Birth. Fill in the correct details carefully.

Step 5 – Upload Supporting Documents

Scan the relevant document clearly and upload it. Make sure the file format is as per UIDAI requirements (PDF/JPG/PNG).

Step 6 – Submit Update Request

After verifying all details, submit your update request. You will receive an Update Request Number (URN) to track your request.

Step 7 – Track Status

Use your URN to check status online via the UIDAI portal. Status updates show pending verification, approved, or rejected.

Common Errors and Fixes

  • OTP not received: Check mobile number registered with Aadhaar.
  • Document rejected: Ensure document is clear, valid, and accepted proof type.
  • Wrong details entered: Re-submit request with correct details.
  • Portal errors: Try again after some time or clear browser cache.

FAQs

1. Can I update Aadhaar without registered mobile?

No, OTP verification requires the registered mobile number. Visit an Aadhaar center if your mobile is not registered.

2. How long does online update take?

Typically 7–10 working days after verification.

3. Can I update multiple fields at once?

Yes, you can update Address, Gender, and DOB in a single request, uploading relevant documents for each.

4. Is there a fee for online update?

Online updates are free via UIDAI portal.

5. How to know if update is approved?

Check your status with URN. Approved requests reflect updated information on your Aadhaar card and portal.

Important Tips

  • Always use the official UIDAI portal.
  • Keep your mobile number active.
  • Scan documents clearly.
  • Double-check details before submission.
  • Use URN to track status regularly.
