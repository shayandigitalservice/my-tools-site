What is a Loan Calculator?

A loan calculator is an online financial tool that helps users calculate their monthly EMI based on the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. It uses a standard mathematical formula to provide accurate results instantly.

Instead of manually calculating complex formulas, users can simply enter basic details and get immediate results. This makes it easier to compare loan options and choose the best one according to your financial situation.

How EMI is Calculated

EMI is calculated using a standard formula that considers the principal amount, interest rate, and loan tenure. The formula ensures that the borrower pays a fixed amount every month until the loan is fully repaid.

Using this loan calculator, you don’t need to worry about formulas. The tool automatically calculates everything and shows you clear results including monthly EMI, total payment, and total interest.
